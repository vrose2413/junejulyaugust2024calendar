Introduction

If you're a fan of swap meets, you're in luck – Orange County has plenty of them! From vintage clothing to antique furniture, you're sure to find something that catches your eye. In this article, we'll be taking a closer look at the Orange County swap meet calendar for 2024. So grab your calendar and let's get started!

What is a Swap Meet?

A swap meet is a type of flea market where vendors sell used or secondhand items. These can include clothing, furniture, electronics, and more. Swap meets are usually held outdoors and can be a great way to score some amazing deals.

Question: What's the difference between a swap meet and a flea market?

While the terms "swap meet" and "flea market" are often used interchangeably, there is a slight difference between the two. Flea markets tend to be larger and more commercialized, with vendors selling new merchandise in addition to used items. Swap meets, on the other hand, are typically smaller and more focused on secondhand goods.

Orange County Swap Meet Calendar

Now let's take a look at the 2024 Orange County swap meet calendar. Note that dates and locations are subject to change, so it's always a good idea to double-check before heading out.

January 2024

The first swap meet of the year will be held on January 7th at the Orange County Fairgrounds in Costa Mesa. This is a great place to start your year off right, with dozens of vendors selling everything from vintage clothing to handmade jewelry.

February 2024

On February 11th, head to the Anaheim Marketplace for their monthly swap meet. This market is known for its amazing food vendors, so come hungry and be prepared to sample some delicious treats.

March 2024

March will see the return of the popular Orange Coast College Swap Meet, held on the 3rd Sunday of every month. This swap meet is particularly great for collectors, with vendors selling a wide range of rare and unique items.

April 2024

Mark your calendars for April 14th, when the Fullerton Market will host its annual Spring Fling Swap Meet. This event is a must-visit for anyone looking for vintage and retro items, as well as handmade crafts and jewelry.

May 2024

The Orange County Market Place in Costa Mesa will hold its monthly swap meet on May 5th. This market is known for its variety of vendors, with everything from antiques to fresh produce up for grabs.

June 2024

June 9th is the date for the monthly swap meet at the Cypress College campus. This market is particularly great for families, with plenty of activities for kids and a wide range of vendors selling toys and games.

July 2024

The Orange County Fairgrounds will host another swap meet on July 7th. This is a great chance to beat the summer heat and score some amazing deals at the same time.

August 2024

August 11th will see the return of the Anaheim Marketplace swap meet. This market is known for its friendly atmosphere and welcoming vendors, so be sure to stop by and say hello.

September 2024

The Orange Coast College Swap Meet will be held on September 15th. This market is a favorite among collectors, with vendors selling everything from vintage toys to antique furniture.

October 2024

The Fullerton Market will host its annual Halloween Swap Meet on October 12th. This event is a great chance to score some spooky decorations and costumes, as well as vintage and retro items.

November 2024

The Orange County Market Place will hold its monthly swap meet on November 10th. This market is a great place to start your holiday shopping, with plenty of unique gift ideas up for grabs.

December 2024

The final swap meet of the year will be held on December 8th at the Cypress College campus. This market is a great way to wrap up your year of bargain hunting, with plenty of vendors selling holiday-themed items.

Conclusion

As you can see, Orange County has plenty of swap meets to keep you busy all year long. Whether you're a collector, a bargain hunter, or just looking for a fun way to spend a weekend, there's sure to be a swap meet that's right up your alley. So grab your shopping bags and get ready to score some amazing deals in 2024!
