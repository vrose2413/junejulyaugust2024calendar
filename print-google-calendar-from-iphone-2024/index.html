How To Print Your Google Calendar From Iphone 2024

Written by Pauline Lafleur Aug 14, 2022 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Google Calendar has become a popular tool for organizing one's schedule. With the rise of mobile devices, it's no wonder that people want to access their calendars on their iPhones. However, sometimes you need to have a physical copy of your calendar. In this article, we'll show you how to print your Google Calendar from your iPhone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open Google Calendar App

The first step is to open the Google Calendar app on your iPhone. If you don't have it installed, you can download it from the App Store.

Step 2: Select the Calendar You Want to Print

Once you have the app open, select the calendar you want to print. You can do this by tapping on the calendar name at the bottom of the screen.

Step 3: Tap on the Three Dots

Next, tap on the three dots in the top right-hand corner of the screen. This will open a menu of options.

Step 4: Select "Print"

From the menu, select "Print." This will open a new screen with print options.

Step 5: Choose Printer and Settings

Select your printer and print settings. You can choose the number of copies, paper size, and orientation. Make sure to preview the document before printing to ensure it looks the way you want it to.

Step 6: Print Your Calendar

Once you have selected your printer and settings, tap on "Print" to print your calendar.

FAQs

Can I Print a Specific Date Range?

Yes, you can. In the print options screen, select "Date Range" and choose the specific dates you want to print.

Can I Print Multiple Calendars?

Yes, you can. In the Google Calendar app, select the calendars you want to print before tapping on the three dots and selecting "Print."

What If I Don't Have a Printer?

If you don't have a printer, you can save the document as a PDF and send it to yourself or someone else to print.

Conclusion

Printing your Google Calendar from your iPhone is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Having a physical copy of your calendar can come in handy in a variety of situations. Now that you know how to print your calendar, you can easily stay organized both digitally and on paper.
