Introduction

If you're a student or parent in the Prince George's County Public School system, you're probably familiar with the A Day B Day calendar. This calendar determines which classes students attend on which days, alternating between A and B days throughout the school year. In this article, we'll be discussing the PGCPS A Day B Day calendar for the year 2024.

What is the PGCPS A Day B Day Calendar?

The A Day B Day calendar is a system used by PGCPS to schedule classes. On A days, students attend their A day classes, and on B days, they attend their B day classes. This system allows for a balanced schedule and ensures that students have equal time in each of their classes.

When does the PGCPS A Day B Day Calendar start and end?

The PGCPS A Day B Day calendar for 2024 will start on Monday, August 26th, 2024, and end on Friday, June 7th, 2025. This schedule includes a total of 180 school days.

Changes for the 2024 Calendar

What changes can we expect for the 2024 calendar?

While there are no major changes to the A Day B Day system for 2024, there may be slight changes to individual school schedules. It's important to check with your school to ensure that you have the correct schedule.

FAQs

How do I know if it's an A day or a B day?

You can find out if it's an A day or a B day by checking the PGCPS A Day B Day calendar. The calendar is available on the PGCPS website, as well as on individual school websites.

What happens if I miss a day?

If you miss a day, you'll miss the classes scheduled for that day. It's important to catch up on any missed work as soon as possible.

What if I have a schedule conflict?

If you have a schedule conflict, such as two classes scheduled at the same time, you should speak with your guidance counselor or school administration to resolve the issue.

How can I stay organized with the A Day B Day schedule?

There are several ways to stay organized with the A Day B Day schedule. You can use a planner or calendar to keep track of your classes, or you can use an online scheduling tool.

Conclusion

The PGCPS A Day B Day calendar is an important system for students and parents to be familiar with. By understanding the schedule and staying organized, students can have a successful school year. Remember to check with your school for any individual schedule changes, and don't hesitate to speak with school administration if you have any questions or concerns.
