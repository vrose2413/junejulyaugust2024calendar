When Does the Academic Year Start?

The academic year for Kent State University in 2024 starts on August 26th, which is a Friday. This is the first day of classes for the fall semester. If you're planning to study at Kent State in the fall, make sure you're ready to start on this date.

What Are the Important Dates for the Fall Semester?

Here are some of the important dates you should know for the fall semester:

  • August 26th: First day of classes
  • September 5th: Labor Day (no classes)
  • October 10th: Fall Break (no classes)
  • November 24th-26th: Thanksgiving Break (no classes)
  • December 9th: Last day of classes
  • December 12th-16th: Final exams

What About the Spring Semester?

The spring semester at Kent State University in 2024 starts on January 16th, which is a Monday. Here are some of the important dates for the spring semester:

  • January 16th: First day of classes
  • March 6th-10th: Spring Break (no classes)
  • April 14th-17th: Easter Break (no classes)
  • May 3rd: Last day of classes
  • May 4th-10th: Final exams
