What is the WFU Academic Calendar?

The WFU academic calendar outlines the schedule of events for the upcoming academic year. This includes important dates such as the start and end of classes, holidays, and exam periods. It’s important to keep up with the calendar to ensure that you don’t miss any important deadlines or events.

When does the Academic Year Start?

The academic year at WFU usually starts in late August, and the 2024-25 academic year is no different. The first day of classes for the fall semester is Monday, August 26, 2024. Make sure to mark this date on your calendar!

What are the Important Dates for the Academic Year?

There are several important dates to keep in mind for the upcoming academic year. Here are some of the most significant:

  • August 26, 2024: First day of fall semester classes
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day holiday
  • November 27-29, 2024: Thanksgiving holiday
  • December 11-17, 2024: Exam period for fall semester
  • January 13, 2025: First day of spring semester classes
  • March 9-15, 2025: Spring break
  • May 5-11, 2025: Exam period for spring semester
  • May 19, 2025: Commencement

What are the Summer Sessions?

After the spring semester ends, there are two summer sessions that take place at WFU. The first session runs from May 21 to June 28, 2025, and the second session runs from July 1 to August 8, 2025. These sessions are a great opportunity to take additional classes or complete credits during the summer months.

What About Holidays?

There are several holidays throughout the academic year at WFU. Some of the most important include Labor Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. It’s important to note that classes will not be held on these days, and some services on campus may be closed.

What is the Exam Period?

The exam period at WFU is a time when students take final exams for their classes. This usually takes place at the end of each semester, and it’s important to be aware of the exam schedule so that you can properly prepare. The exam period for the fall semester is December 11-17, 2024, and the exam period for the spring semester is May 5-11, 2025.
