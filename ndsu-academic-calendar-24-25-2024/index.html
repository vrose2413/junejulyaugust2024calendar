Introduction

As a student or faculty member at North Dakota State University (NDSU), it is important to keep track of important dates and deadlines. The academic calendar for the 2024-2025 school year has just been released, and we have all the information you need to know.

When Does the 2024-2025 School Year Begin and End?

The first day of classes for the 2024-2025 school year at NDSU is August 26, 2024. The last day of classes is May 9, 2025. There will be no classes held on Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Memorial Day.

What About Holidays?

In addition to Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Memorial Day, NDSU observes several other holidays during the academic year. These include:

  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day
  • November 28-29, 2024: Thanksgiving Break
  • December 23, 2024-January 1, 2025: Winter Break
  • January 20, 2025: Martin Luther King Jr. Day
  • March 17-21, 2025: Spring Break
  • April 18, 2025: Good Friday
  • May 26, 2025: Memorial Day

What About Final Exams?

Final exams for the fall semester will be held from December 12-18, 2024. Final exams for the spring semester will be held from May 12-18, 2025.

Registration Deadlines

Registration for the fall semester will be open from April 1-August 25, 2024. Registration for the spring semester will be open from November 4, 2024-January 17, 2025.

What About Drop/Add Deadlines?

The last day to drop a class without a "W" appearing on your transcript is September 5, 2024 for the fall semester and January 31, 2025 for the spring semester. The last day to add a class is September 5, 2024 for the fall semester and January 31, 2025 for the spring semester.

Conclusion

Knowing the important dates and deadlines for the 2024-2025 academic year at NDSU is crucial for success as a student or faculty member. Make sure to mark these dates on your calendar and plan accordingly.
