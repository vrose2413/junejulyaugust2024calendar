Important Dates

The first day of school for students in the Plain Local School District will be August 26, 2024. The last day of school is tentatively scheduled for May 23, 2025. There will be no school on Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, or Independence Day.

Winter Break

Winter break for the 2024-2025 year will begin on December 23, 2024, and end on January 3, 2025. Students will return to school on January 6, 2025.

Spring Break

Spring break will be from April 7-11, 2025. Students will have this time off to enjoy the spring weather and recharge for the remainder of the school year.
