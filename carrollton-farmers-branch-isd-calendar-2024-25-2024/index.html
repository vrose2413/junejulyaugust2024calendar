Introduction

Carrollton Farmers Branch Independent School District (CFBISD) is a public school district in Texas that serves the cities of Carrollton, Farmers Branch, and parts of Addison, Coppell, and Dallas. The district is committed to providing quality education to its students, and one way it achieves this is by maintaining a comprehensive calendar that outlines important dates throughout the year.

What is the CFBISD Calendar?

The CFBISD calendar is a document that lists important dates for the upcoming school year. It includes information on the first and last day of school, holidays, breaks, early release days, and more. The calendar is designed to help students, parents, and staff plan for the year ahead and stay informed about important events.

When is the CFBISD Calendar Released?

The CFBISD calendar is typically released in the spring, several months before the start of the upcoming school year. This allows families and staff to plan ahead and make necessary arrangements.

Important Dates

First Day of School

The first day of school for the 2024-25 school year in CFBISD is August 19, 2024. This is an important date for students, parents, and staff, as it marks the beginning of a new academic year.

Holidays and Breaks

CFBISD observes several holidays and breaks throughout the school year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Winter Break, Spring Break, and Memorial Day. It is important for families to be aware of these dates and plan accordingly.

Early Release Days

CFBISD has several early release days throughout the school year, which allow teachers to participate in professional development activities. On these days, students are released from school early, and parents should make arrangements to pick them up or ensure they have a safe way to get home.

FAQs

What if I Need to Take My Child Out of School for a Family Vacation?

CFBISD encourages families to plan vacations during scheduled breaks and holidays. However, if it is necessary to take your child out of school for a family vacation, you should notify the school in advance and make arrangements with your child's teacher to ensure they do not miss important assignments or assessments.

What Happens if School is Closed Due to Inclement Weather?

CFBISD closely monitors weather conditions and will make a decision about closing schools if necessary. If school is closed due to inclement weather, families will be notified through the district's communication channels, including email, phone, and social media.

What if I Have Questions or Concerns About the CFBISD Calendar?

If you have questions or concerns about the CFBISD calendar, you should contact your child's school or the district's administration office. They will be able to provide you with the information you need and address any concerns you may have.

Conclusion

The CFBISD calendar is an important tool for families, students, and staff. By staying informed about important dates and events throughout the school year, everyone can work together to ensure a successful academic experience. If you have any questions or concerns about the CFBISD calendar, be sure to reach out to the district for assistance.
