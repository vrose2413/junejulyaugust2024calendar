Are you a parent or a student in Dade County, Florida? Do you want to know about the upcoming school year's calendar? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we will discuss the Dade County Public School Calendar 2024, important dates, events, and other information you need to know for the upcoming school year.

When Does the Dade County Public School Start in 2024?

The Dade County Public School will start on August 19th, 2024, for all students. This is the first day of school for all students, including those in elementary, middle, and high schools. The school year will end on June 5th, 2025, which is a Monday.

What Are the Important Dates You Need to Know?

Here are some important dates you need to know for the upcoming school year:

  • August 19th, 2024: First day of school
  • September 2nd, 2024: Labor Day Holiday
  • November 11th, 2024: Veteran's Day Holiday
  • November 25th-29th, 2024: Thanksgiving Break
  • December 23rd, 2024-January 6th, 2025: Winter Break
  • March 24th-28th, 2025: Spring Break
  • May 26th, 2025: Memorial Day Holiday
  • June 5th, 2025: Last day of school

What About Teacher Planning Days?

There are a few teacher planning days that you need to keep in mind as well. These days are when students have the day off, but teachers have to go to school for planning and training. Here are the teacher planning days for the 2024-2025 school year:

  • August 16th-17th, 2024: Teacher Planning Days
  • October 18th, 2024: Teacher Planning Day
  • January 7th-8th, 2025: Teacher Planning Days
  • March 21st, 2025: Teacher Planning Day
  • June 6th-7th, 2025: Teacher Planning Days (after the last day of school)

What Are the School Hours?

The exact school hours may vary depending on the school your child attends. However, most elementary schools start at 8:35 am and end at 3:05 pm. Middle schools start at 9:10 am and end at 3:50 pm. High schools start at 7:20 am and end at 2:20 pm.

What Are the Extracurricular Activities?

Dade County Public Schools offer a variety of extracurricular activities for students. These activities include sports, music, arts, clubs, and more. If your child is interested in joining any of these activities, they should contact their school for more information.

How Can You Stay Updated?

If you want to stay updated on any changes or updates to the Dade County Public School Calendar 2024, you can visit the official website of the school district. They also have a social media presence, where they post updates and news regularly.

What About Holidays and Weather-Related Closures?

In case of holidays or weather-related closures, the school district will notify parents and students through their official website, social media, and local news outlets. It is always a good idea to keep an eye on these sources to stay updated.

Final Thoughts

We hope this article has provided you with all the information you need to know about the Dade County Public School Calendar 2024. Make sure to mark the important dates on your calendar and stay updated on any changes or updates. We wish you and your child a successful and fruitful school year ahead!
