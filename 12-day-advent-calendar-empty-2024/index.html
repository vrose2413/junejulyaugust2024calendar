Introduction

The holiday season is always an exciting time of the year, and advent calendars are a popular way to count down the days until Christmas. However, what do you do with the empty advent calendar after all the treats are gone? In this article, we will explore 12 creative ideas for repurposing your empty advent calendar in 2024.

Question:

What can you do with an empty advent calendar?

Answer:

There are many creative ways to repurpose an empty advent calendar, including using it for storage, decoration, and organization.

Idea 1: Jewelry Organizer

If you have a lot of jewelry, an empty advent calendar can make a great organizer. Simply remove the plastic trays and use the compartments to store your earrings, necklaces, and bracelets.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a jewelry organizer?

Answer:

By removing the plastic trays and using the compartments to store jewelry.

Idea 2: Mini Planters

You can also use an empty advent calendar as mini planters for small succulents or herbs. Simply add some soil and seeds to each compartment, and watch your plants grow.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as mini planters?

Answer:

By adding soil and seeds to each compartment and using them to grow small succulents or herbs.

Idea 3: Sewing Kit Organizer

An empty advent calendar can also be used as a sewing kit organizer. Use the compartments to store your thread, needles, and other sewing supplies.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a sewing kit organizer?

Answer:

By using the compartments to store thread, needles, and other sewing supplies.

Idea 4: Desk Organizer

Use an empty advent calendar to organize your desk supplies. The compartments can hold paperclips, push pins, rubber bands, and other small items.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a desk organizer?

Answer:

By using the compartments to hold small desk supplies like paperclips, push pins, and rubber bands.

Idea 5: Gift Box

An empty advent calendar can also be used as a gift box. Fill each compartment with small gifts or treats, and give it to someone special.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a gift box?

Answer:

By filling each compartment with small gifts or treats and giving it to someone special.

Idea 6: Art Supply Organizer

If you're an artist, an empty advent calendar can make a great art supply organizer. Use the compartments to store your paintbrushes, pencils, and other supplies.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as an art supply organizer?

Answer:

By using the compartments to store paintbrushes, pencils, and other art supplies.

Idea 7: Spice Rack

If you love cooking, an empty advent calendar can be repurposed as a spice rack. Simply add your favorite spices to each compartment, and use it to season your dishes.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a spice rack?

Answer:

By adding spices to each compartment and using it to season dishes.

Idea 8: Travel Organizer

An empty advent calendar can also be used as a travel organizer. Use the compartments to store your travel-sized toiletries, and you'll be ready for any trip.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a travel organizer?

Answer:

By using the compartments to store travel-sized toiletries.

Idea 9: Craft Supply Organizer

If you love crafting, an empty advent calendar can make a great craft supply organizer. Use the compartments to store your beads, buttons, and other supplies.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a craft supply organizer?

Answer:

By using the compartments to store beads, buttons, and other craft supplies.

Idea 10: Memory Keeper

An empty advent calendar can also be used as a memory keeper. Use the compartments to store small mementos, like ticket stubs or postcards.

Question:

How can an empty advent calendar be used as a memory keeper?

Answer:

By using the compartments to store small mementos, like ticket stubs or postcards.

Conclusion

An empty advent calendar doesn't have to go to waste. With these 12 creative ideas, you can repurpose it into something useful and beautiful. Whether you're organizing your jewelry or spices, or creating a gift box or memory keeper, an empty advent calendar can be transformed into something special.
