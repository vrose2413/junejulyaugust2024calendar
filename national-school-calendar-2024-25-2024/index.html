Introduction

The National School Calendar for 2024-25 has been released by the Ministry of Education. This calendar serves as a guide for all public and private schools in the country on the academic year's schedule and important dates. The calendar is prepared with the aim of ensuring that all schools follow the same academic schedule, which is essential for the smooth functioning of the education system.

Key Dates

The academic year for 2024-25 starts on September 1, 2024, and ends on June 30, 2025. The calendar includes holidays and breaks such as winter and summer vacations, public holidays, and teacher training days. The winter vacation will be from December 23, 2024, to January 5, 2025, while the summer vacation will be from June 28, 2025, to September 1, 2025.

Impact on Students and Teachers

The National School Calendar plays a crucial role in the lives of students and teachers. It helps them plan their academic year and prepare for important events such as exams, extracurricular activities, and holidays. It also ensures that students have an equal amount of school days, which is crucial for academic success.
