Iphone Calendar: How To View All Events In 2024

Written by Mable Stanley Oct 22, 2022 · 3 min read

As the year 2024 approaches, it’s time to start planning ahead. Your iPhone calendar is a great tool to keep track of important events, meetings, and appointments. However, it can be frustrating when you can’t see all events at once. In this article, we’ll show you how to view all events in your iPhone calendar for the year 2024.

Table of Contents

Introduction

As the year 2024 approaches, it’s time to start planning ahead. Your iPhone calendar is a great tool to keep track of important events, meetings, and appointments. However, it can be frustrating when you can’t see all events at once. In this article, we’ll show you how to view all events in your iPhone calendar for the year 2024.

Step 1: Open Your Calendar

The first step is to open your iPhone calendar app. You can access it from your home screen or by opening the “Calendar” folder.

Step 2: Select Year View

Next, you need to select the year view. This will allow you to see all events for the entire year. To do this, tap on the “Year” button located at the bottom of the screen.

Step 3: Scroll to 2024

Now, scroll down until you reach the year 2024. You can use the scroll bar on the right side of the screen to navigate faster. Once you find 2024, tap on it.

Step 4: View All Events

Congratulations! You can now view all events in your iPhone calendar for the year 2024. You can see each event listed on the specific date it’s scheduled for. You can also see the days of the week, national holidays, and other important dates.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I customize the year view?

A: Yes, you can customize the year view by selecting the “Settings” icon located at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can choose to show or hide certain calendars, change the default calendar, and adjust the start of the week.

Q: What if I have too many events to see at once?

A: If you have too many events to see at once, you can zoom in and out of the year view by using the pinch-to-zoom gesture. This will allow you to see more or fewer events at once.

Q: Can I add events directly from the year view?

A: Unfortunately, you cannot add events directly from the year view. You will need to switch back to the month or day view to add an event. However, you can tap on an existing event in the year view to view more details about it.

Conclusion

Viewing all events in your iPhone calendar for the year 2024 is easy with these simple steps. Whether you’re planning a vacation, a wedding, or a business trip, your iPhone calendar can help you stay organized and on track. Don’t forget to customize your year view to make it work best for you. Happy planning!
