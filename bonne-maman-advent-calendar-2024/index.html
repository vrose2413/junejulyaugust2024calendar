Introduction

Are you a fan of advent calendars? If yes, then you must have heard about the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar. Bonne Maman is a French brand that is known for its delicious jams and preserves. In 2024, they are launching their advent calendar, and it's going to be amazing! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar 2024.

What is Bonne Maman Advent Calendar?

Bonne Maman Advent Calendar is a special calendar that contains 24 jars of delicious jams and preserves. Each jar is different and has a unique flavor. The calendar is designed to be opened every day starting from December 1st until Christmas Eve. It's a perfect way to count down to Christmas and try out different flavors of jams and preserves.

What's Inside the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar?

As mentioned earlier, the calendar contains 24 jars of jams and preserves. The jars are of different sizes and flavors. The flavors include strawberry, raspberry, blueberry, apricot, blackberry, cherry, fig, and many more. Each jar is labeled with the date it should be opened, making it easy to keep track.

Why Choose Bonne Maman Advent Calendar?

There are many reasons why you should choose the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar. Firstly, the brand is known for its high-quality jams and preserves, so you can expect nothing but the best from this calendar. Secondly, the calendar is a perfect gift for anyone who loves jams and preserves. Finally, it's an excellent way to try out different flavors and expand your taste buds.

When Will the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar be Available?

The Bonne Maman Advent Calendar 2024 will be available in stores from November 1st, 2024. However, you can pre-order it online from the Bonne Maman website.

FAQs

How Much Does Bonne Maman Advent Calendar Cost?

The price of the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar varies depending on the country you're in. However, on average, it costs around $30-$40.

Can I Buy Bonne Maman Advent Calendar Online?

Yes, you can buy the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar online from the Bonne Maman website or other online retailers.

Is Bonne Maman Advent Calendar Suitable for Vegetarians?

Yes, all the ingredients used in the Bonne Maman Advent Calendar are suitable for vegetarians.

Conclusion

The Bonne Maman Advent Calendar 2024 is a must-have for anyone who loves jams and preserves. With its unique flavors and high-quality ingredients, it's the perfect way to count down to Christmas and try out new flavors. So, mark your calendars and get ready to enjoy the delicious flavors of Bonne Maman jams and preserves.
