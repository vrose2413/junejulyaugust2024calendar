Introduction

It's important for parents, students, and teachers to know the school calendar for Guilford County Schools. This article will provide information on the calendars for the academic years 2021-2022 and 2023-2024.

What is the Guilford County School calendar?

The Guilford County School calendar is a schedule of important dates for the academic year. It includes holidays, teacher workdays, early release days, and the first and last day of school.

What are the key dates for the 2021-2022 academic year?

The first day of school for students is August 23, 2021. There are several holidays throughout the year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Spring Break. The last day of school for students is June 10, 2022.

What are the key dates for the 2023-2024 academic year?

The first day of school for students is August 28, 2023. There are several holidays throughout the year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Spring Break. The last day of school for students is June 12, 2024.

What is the significance of teacher workdays?

Teacher workdays are important for teachers to prepare for the school year. During these days, teachers attend professional development sessions and work on lesson plans.

What are early release days?

Early release days are days when students are released from school earlier than usual. These days are often used for teacher planning and professional development.

Why is the school calendar important?

The school calendar is important because it provides a schedule for students, parents, and teachers to follow. It ensures that everyone is on the same page and knows when important events are taking place.

Is the school calendar subject to change?

Yes, the school calendar is subject to change. Weather events, school closures, and other unforeseen circumstances may require changes to the calendar. It's important to stay updated on any changes that may occur.
