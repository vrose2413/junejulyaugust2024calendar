Introduction

Memphis, Tennessee, is a vibrant city with a rich history, diverse culture, and a thriving arts and music scene. Each year, the city hosts a wide range of events, from music festivals and art exhibitions to food and wine tastings and sporting events. In this article, we will take a look at the Memphis TN calendar of events for 2024 and highlight some of the not-to-be-missed events.

What events can we expect in Memphis in 2024?

Memphis is known for its lively events scene, and 2024 is shaping up to be another exciting year. Some of the events you can expect to see on the calendar include:

The Beale Street Music Festival

The Beale Street Music Festival is an annual music festival that takes place in Memphis in May. The festival features a wide range of musical acts, from local bands to international superstars. In 2024, the festival will be held from May 3-5.

The Memphis in May International Festival

The Memphis in May International Festival is a month-long celebration of Memphis culture, including music, food, and sports. The festival includes events such as the World Championship Barbecue Cooking Contest, the Great American River Run, and the Beale Street Music Festival.

The Southern Heritage Classic

The Southern Heritage Classic is an annual football game between Tennessee State University and Jackson State University. The game is held at the Liberty Bowl Memorial Stadium and is a highlight of the fall events calendar in Memphis.

What are some other events to check out in Memphis in 2024?

While the events listed above are some of the biggest and most popular in Memphis, there are plenty of other events to check out throughout the year. Some other events to look out for include:

The Memphis Film Festival

The Memphis Film Festival is an annual event that celebrates independent cinema from around the world. The festival includes screenings, Q&A sessions with filmmakers, and panel discussions on topics related to the film industry.

The Memphis Food & Wine Festival

The Memphis Food & Wine Festival is a celebration of the city's culinary scene, featuring food and wine tastings from some of the city's top chefs and restaurants. The festival also includes live music and other entertainment.

The Cooper-Young Festival

The Cooper-Young Festival is a celebration of the Cooper-Young neighborhood, one of Memphis' most vibrant and eclectic areas. The festival includes live music, food and drink vendors, and arts and crafts vendors selling their wares.

How can visitors plan their trip to Memphis around these events?

If you're planning a trip to Memphis in 2024, it's a good idea to plan your itinerary around the events that interest you. Many of the events listed above have specific dates and times, so be sure to check the official event websites for more information.

What are some tips for attending events in Memphis?

Attending events in Memphis can be a lot of fun, but it's important to be prepared. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Book your accommodation early, as hotels and Airbnbs can fill up quickly during popular events.
  • Check the weather forecast and dress accordingly, as Memphis can be hot and humid in the summer months.
  • Plan your transportation in advance, as parking can be difficult to find during popular events.
  • Bring cash, as many events only accept cash for food and drink purchases.
  • Be respectful of the local culture and customs, and have fun!

Conclusion

Memphis is a city that knows how to have a good time, and the calendar of events for 2024 is a testament to that. Whether you're a music lover, a foodie, or a sports fan, there's something for everyone in Memphis. So start planning your trip today, and get ready to experience all that this vibrant city has to offer!
