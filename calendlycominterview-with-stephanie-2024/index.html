About Calendly.com

Calendly.com is a popular scheduling tool that has gained popularity among professionals and businesses. The company was founded in 2013 by Tope Awotona, and it is used by millions of people worldwide.

What inspired the creation of Calendly.com?

The founder of Calendly.com, Tope Awotona, was inspired by the difficulties he experienced when scheduling meetings. He found that scheduling meetings was a time-consuming process that involved a lot of back-and-forth emails and phone calls. He decided to create a tool that would simplify the scheduling process and save time for professionals.

Interview with Stephanie

Stephanie is a freelance writer and a Calendly.com user. She shares her experience of using the tool and how it has helped her in her work.

How has Calendly.com helped you in your work?

Calendly.com has been a game-changer for me. As a freelance writer, I have to schedule interviews with people from different time zones. Calendly.com has made it easy for me to schedule meetings without having to worry about time differences.

What features of Calendly.com do you find most useful?

The feature that I find most useful is the ability to set up different types of meetings. I can create different types of meetings, such as phone calls, video calls, or in-person meetings. I can also set up different durations for each type of meeting.

Benefits of Calendly.com

Calendly.com offers several benefits to professionals and businesses. Here are some of the benefits:

Calendly.com saves time by simplifying the scheduling process. It eliminates the need for back-and-forth emails and phone calls, which can be time-consuming.
