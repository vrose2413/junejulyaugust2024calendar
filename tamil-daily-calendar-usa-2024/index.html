What is a Tamil Daily Calendar?

A Tamil Daily Calendar is a traditional calendar used by Tamil people to keep track of important dates and events. It is based on the Tamil Solar Calendar and is widely used in Tamil Nadu, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Singapore, and other Tamil-speaking regions around the world. The calendar is used to determine auspicious dates for weddings, housewarming ceremonies, and other important events.
