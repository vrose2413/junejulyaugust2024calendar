Are you having trouble with managing your subscribed calendars on your iPhone? Do you want to remove a subscribed calendar but don't know how to do it? You've come to the right place! In this article, we'll guide you on how to delete subscribed calendar on iPhone 2024.

What is a Subscribed Calendar?

A subscribed calendar is a calendar that you can add to your iPhone from a website or an email invitation. It's a useful feature that allows you to keep track of important events, such as holidays, sports games, or TV shows. However, sometimes you may want to delete a subscribed calendar that you no longer need or want to see on your iPhone.

How to Delete a Subscribed Calendar on iPhone 2024?

Deleting a subscribed calendar on iPhone 2024 is a straightforward process. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  1. Open the Calendar app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap on the "Calendars" button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the "Subscribed" section and tap on the subscribed calendar that you want to delete.
  4. Tap on the "i" icon next to the subscribed calendar.
  5. Scroll down to the bottom of the screen and tap on the "Delete Calendar" button.
  6. Confirm that you want to delete the subscribed calendar by tapping on the "Delete" button.

That's it! The subscribed calendar will be removed from your iPhone, and you won't see its events anymore.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I recover a deleted subscribed calendar?

A: Unfortunately, once you delete a subscribed calendar on your iPhone, you can't recover it. You'll need to subscribe to the calendar again if you want to see its events.

Q: Will deleting a subscribed calendar delete its events?

A: No, deleting a subscribed calendar will only remove the calendar from your iPhone. It won't delete its events. If you want to remove the events, you'll need to delete them manually from the Calendar app.

Q: Can I delete multiple subscribed calendars at once?

A: Yes, you can delete multiple subscribed calendars at once by following the same steps mentioned above. Simply tap on the "Edit" button at the top of the "Calendars" screen, select the subscribed calendars you want to delete, and then tap on the "Delete" button.

Conclusion

Deleting a subscribed calendar on iPhone 2024 is a simple process that can be done in a few steps. By following the instructions above, you can easily remove a subscribed calendar that you no longer need or want to see on your iPhone. Remember that deleting a subscribed calendar won't delete its events, and you won't be able to recover it once it's deleted.
