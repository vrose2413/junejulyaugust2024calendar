What Events are Happening in 2024?

There are many events happening in Alexandria in 2024. Some of the highlights include the Vikingland Band Festival, the Douglas County Fair, the Apple Fest, and the Runestone Days Festival. These events are all very popular and attract visitors from all over the country.

Vikingland Band Festival

The Vikingland Band Festival is a celebration of music and marching bands. It takes place in June and features bands from all over the country. The festival includes a parade, a concert, and a competition between the bands.

Douglas County Fair

The Douglas County Fair is a classic county fair with all the traditional attractions. It takes place in August and includes livestock shows, carnival rides, and plenty of food and drink.

Apple Fest

Apple Fest is a celebration of all things apple. It takes place in September and features apple-themed food, drinks, and activities. There are also plenty of crafts and vendors selling apple-related products.

Runestone Days Festival

The Runestone Days Festival is a celebration of Alexandria's Viking heritage. It takes place in July and includes events like a Viking parade, a Viking encampment, and a Viking battle reenactment.
