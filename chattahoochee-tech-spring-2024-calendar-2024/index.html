What is Chattahoochee Technical College?

Chattahoochee Technical College is a public technical college located in Marietta, Georgia. It is one of the largest technical colleges in Georgia, offering more than 70 programs of study. The college has five campuses in the state, including Acworth, Canton, Marietta, Mountain View, and Woodstock.

What is the Spring 2024 Calendar?

The Spring 2024 Calendar is a schedule of all the important dates for the spring semester. The calendar includes registration dates, class start and end dates, holidays, and other events. The calendar is important for students to plan their semester and make sure they don't miss any important dates.

Registration Dates

The registration dates for Spring 2024 are from November 1, 2023, to January 6, 2024. It is important to register for classes as soon as possible to ensure you get the classes you need. The earlier you register, the better chance you have of getting the classes you want at the times that work best for you.

How do I register for classes?

You can register for classes online through the student portal or in person at any of the Chattahoochee Tech campuses. If you need assistance with registration, you can contact the Office of Admissions and Records.

Class Start and End Dates

The Spring 2024 semester starts on January 8, 2024, and ends on May 2, 2024. The semester is 16 weeks long, and classes meet for various hours depending on the program of study. Make sure to check your schedule for class times and locations.

What if I need to drop a class?

If you need to drop a class, you must do so before the drop deadline, which is February 7, 2024. If you drop a class after the deadline, you will receive a "W" on your transcript, which could affect your GPA.

Holidays and Breaks

Chattahoochee Technical College observes several holidays and breaks during the spring semester. The college will be closed on the following days:

  • January 21, 2024 - Martin Luther King Jr. Day
  • March 11-15, 2024 - Spring Break
  • April 19, 2024 - Good Friday

What if I have a class on a holiday?

If you have a class on a holiday, your instructor will provide you with instructions on how to make up the missed class or assignment.

Other Important Dates

In addition to registration, class start and end dates, and holidays, there are other important dates to keep in mind during the Spring 2024 semester:

  • January 7, 2024 - New Student Orientation
  • January 14, 2024 - Last day to add a class without permission
  • February 18-19, 2024 - Mid-term break
  • April 12-18, 2024 - Advising and registration for summer and fall semesters
  • May 2, 2024 - Last day of classes

What if I miss an important date?

If you miss an important date, such as the registration deadline or drop deadline, you should contact the Office of Admissions and Records as soon as possible to see if there are any options available to you.

Conclusion

Chattahoochee Technical College's Spring 2024 Calendar is an important tool for students to plan their semester and stay on track. Make sure to mark all the important dates in your calendar and reach out to the appropriate offices if you have any questions or concerns. Good luck with your studies!
