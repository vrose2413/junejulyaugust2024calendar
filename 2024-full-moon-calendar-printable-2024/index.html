What is a Full Moon Calendar?

A full moon calendar is a list of dates on which full moons will occur throughout the year. Full moons are significant for many reasons, from their beauty in the night sky to their influence on our emotions and behavior. Having a full moon calendar can help you plan events, ceremonies, or rituals that are aligned with the moon's energy.

Simply search for "2024 full moon calendar printable" on your preferred search engine, and you will find many options to choose from.

What Can I Expect from Each Full Moon in 2024?

Each full moon in 2024 will have its unique energy and influence. Here is a brief overview of what you can expect from each full moon:

January 1 - Wolf Moon

The Wolf Moon is the first full moon of the year and is associated with new beginnings, fresh starts, and setting intentions for the year ahead.

January 31 - Blue Moon

The Blue Moon is the second full moon in January and is a rare occurrence. It is associated with magic, mystery, and spiritual growth.

February 28 - Snow Moon

The Snow Moon is associated with the harsh winter and the need for rest and introspection. It is a time for self-care, healing, and reflection.

March 30 - Worm Moon

The Worm Moon is associated with the arrival of spring and the renewal of life. It is a time for growth, expansion, and new beginnings.

April 29 - Pink Moon

The Pink Moon is associated with the blooming of flowers and the celebration of love and beauty. It is a time for joy, gratitude, and appreciation.

May 29 - Flower Moon

The Flower Moon is associated with the abundance of nature and the fulfillment of our desires. It is a time for manifestation, creativity, and joy.

June 28 - Strawberry Moon

The Strawberry Moon is associated with the sweetness of life and the importance of nurturing our relationships. It is a time for connection, community, and love.

July 27 - Buck Moon

The Buck Moon is associated with the abundance of summer and the importance of taking action towards our goals. It is a time for courage, determination, and perseverance.

August 26 - Sturgeon Moon

The Sturgeon Moon is associated with the harvest season and the abundance of the earth. It is a time for gratitude, celebration, and sharing our blessings with others.

September 24 - Harvest Moon

The Harvest Moon is associated with the culmination of our efforts and the realization of our goals. It is a time for reaping the rewards of our hard work and enjoying the fruits of our labor.
