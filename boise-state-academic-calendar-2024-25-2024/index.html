Boise State Academic Calendar 2024-25

Written by Juan Stafford Apr 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Introduction

Are you a student at Boise State University? Do you want to plan ahead and know the academic calendar for the year 2024-25? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with all the important dates and deadlines you need to know.

What is the Boise State Academic Calendar?

The Boise State Academic Calendar is a schedule of important dates and deadlines that students need to know. It includes the start and end dates of semesters, holidays, registration deadlines, and exam periods.

Semester Dates

The fall semester for the year 2024 will begin on August 26th and end on December 13th. The spring semester will begin on January 6th and end on May 3rd. The summer semester will start on May 13th and end on August 9th.

Registration Deadlines

Registration for the fall semester will begin on April 1st, 2024. The deadline for registration without a late fee is August 9th. Late registration will be open until August 23rd, with a late fee of $50. For the spring semester, registration will begin on November 1st, 2024. The deadline for registration without a late fee is January 3rd, 2025. Late registration will be open until January 17th, with a late fee of $50.

Holidays

Boise State University recognizes several holidays throughout the academic year. There will be no classes held on Labor Day (September 2nd), Thanksgiving Break (November 28th-29th), Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 20th), Spring Break (March 16th-20th), and Memorial Day (May 27th).

Exam Periods

The fall semester exam period will be from December 16th-20th. The spring semester exam period will be from May 6th-10th. The summer semester exam period will be from August 12th-16th.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the fall semester start?

The fall semester for 2024 will begin on August 26th.

When does registration for the spring semester begin?

Registration for the spring semester will begin on November 1st, 2024.

When is Spring Break?

Spring Break for the year 2025 will be from March 16th-20th.

What is the deadline for fall semester registration without a late fee?

The deadline for fall semester registration without a late fee is August 9th, 2024.

When is the summer semester?

The summer semester for 2024 will start on May 13th and end on August 9th.

Conclusion

Knowing the academic calendar is an important part of being successful in college. With this information, you can plan ahead and stay on track with your studies. Remember to mark these important dates on your calendar and keep track of any changes that may occur. Good luck with your studies!
