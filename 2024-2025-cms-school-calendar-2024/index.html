Introduction

As we gear up to welcome the year 2024, the CMS school calendar for the academic year 2024-2025 has been released. This calendar determines the school holidays, breaks, and important events such as parent-teacher meetings, exams, and school trips.

What are the important dates?

The first day of school for the academic year is on August 12, 2024. The fall break will be from October 14 to October 18, 2024, and the winter break will be from December 23, 2024, to January 6, 2025. The last day of school will be on May 20, 2025.

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Parent-teacher meetings are an essential part of a child’s academic journey. The CMS school calendar has allocated several dates for these meetings throughout the year. The first parent-teacher meeting will be held on September 17, 2024, and the second meeting will be on January 14, 2025.

Exam Schedule

Exams are a crucial aspect of a student’s academic performance. The CMS school calendar has scheduled two sets of exams for the academic year 2024-2025. The first set of exams will be held from November 11 to November 15, 2024, and the second set will be from April 21 to April 25, 2025.

School Trips

School trips are a fun and educational experience for students. The CMS school calendar has allocated several dates for school trips throughout the academic year. The first school trip will be on September 27, 2024, and the second trip will be on March 21, 2025.
