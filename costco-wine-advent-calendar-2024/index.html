What is a Wine Advent Calendar?

A wine advent calendar is a special calendar that contains 24 small bottles of wine, each one for every day leading up to Christmas. It's a great way to celebrate the holiday season and try new wines from different regions around the world. Each wine bottle is usually wrapped in a festive paper, making it a fun and exciting way to count down to Christmas.
