The first day of the academic year is August 21, 2024, and the last day of school is May 30, 2025.

There are a total of 12 holidays in the Pflugerville ISD calendar 2024-2025. These include Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Winter Break, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Spring Break, Memorial Day, and more.

The Pflugerville ISD calendar 2024-2025 has 8 early release days throughout the school year. These days are scheduled for staff development and teacher planning.

What Are the Bad Weather Days?

In case of bad weather, Pflugerville ISD has scheduled 2 bad weather days for the academic year 2024-2025. These days are reserved for make-up days in case of school closures due to bad weather.

What About Parent-Teacher Conferences?

Parent-teacher conferences are scheduled on November 4 and 5, 2024, and February 6 and 7, 2025. On these days, students will be dismissed early.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

The graduation dates for the academic year 2024-2025 are May 31 and June 1, 2025. The graduation ceremonies will take place at the Frank Erwin Center in Austin, Texas.
