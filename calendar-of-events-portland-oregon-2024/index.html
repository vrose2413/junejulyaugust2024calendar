Calendar of Events Portland Oregon 2024

Introduction

Are you looking for exciting events to attend in Portland, Oregon in 2024? Look no further than our calendar of events! From festivals to concerts, we have it all.

January

Winter Light Festival

Question: What is the Winter Light Festival?

Answer: The Winter Light Festival is an annual event that takes place in the first week of January. It features mesmerizing light displays, live performances, and interactive art installations.

February

Portland International Film Festival

Question: What is the Portland International Film Festival?

Answer: The Portland International Film Festival is an annual event that takes place in February. It showcases hundreds of films from around the world and attracts filmmakers, actors, and film enthusiasts from all over the globe.

March

St. Patrick's Day Parade and Festival

Question: What is the St. Patrick's Day Parade and Festival?

Answer: The St. Patrick's Day Parade and Festival is an annual event that takes place in March. It features a festive parade, live music, and delicious food and drinks.

April

Portland Rose Festival

Question: What is the Portland Rose Festival?

Answer: The Portland Rose Festival is an annual event that takes place in April and May. It celebrates the city's love for roses with parades, concerts, and other fun activities.

May

Cinco de Mayo Fiesta

Question: What is the Cinco de Mayo Fiesta?

Answer: The Cinco de Mayo Fiesta is an annual event that takes place in May. It celebrates Mexican culture with live music, dancing, and delicious food and drinks.

June

Portland Pride Festival

Question: What is the Portland Pride Festival?

Answer: The Portland Pride Festival is an annual event that takes place in June. It celebrates the LGBTQ+ community with parades, live music, and other fun activities.

July

Waterfront Blues Festival

Question: What is the Waterfront Blues Festival?

Answer: The Waterfront Blues Festival is an annual event that takes place in July. It features live blues music, delicious food and drinks, and a beautiful view of the Willamette River.

August

PDX Pop Now! Music Festival

Question: What is the PDX Pop Now! Music Festival?

Answer: The PDX Pop Now! Music Festival is an annual event that takes place in August. It showcases local musicians and bands and attracts music enthusiasts from all over the city.

September

Feast Portland

Question: What is Feast Portland?

Answer: Feast Portland is an annual event that takes place in September. It celebrates the city's vibrant food and drink scene with tastings, cooking classes, and other food-related activities.

October

Portland Marathon

Question: What is the Portland Marathon?

Answer: The Portland Marathon is an annual event that takes place in October. It attracts runners from all over the world and features a scenic route through the city.
