Table of Contents

Introduction

The holiday season is just around the corner, and one of the most exciting traditions is the advent calendar. This year, why not create your own advent calendar with doors? Creating an advent calendar from scratch is a fun and rewarding activity that will add a personal touch to your holiday decorations. In this article, we will guide you step-by-step on how to create your own advent calendar with doors for the year 2024.

Materials Needed

Before we begin, let's gather the materials needed to create our advent calendar with doors. You will need:

  • Cardboard box
  • Wrapping paper or scrapbook paper
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Number stickers or markers
  • Treats or small gifts to fill the calendar
  • Ribbon or string

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Cut the Box

Take your cardboard box and cut off the top flaps. Next, cut the box into 24 equal-sized compartments. These compartments will serve as the doors for your advent calendar.

Step 2: Cover the Box

Choose your favorite wrapping paper or scrapbook paper and cover the outside of the box. Make sure to glue the paper securely onto the box.

Step 3: Number the Doors

Using number stickers or markers, number each of the 24 doors. You can use different colors or designs to make each door unique.

Step 4: Fill the Calendar

Now comes the fun part! Fill each compartment with treats or small gifts. You can choose to fill the calendar with candy, small toys, or even personal notes.

Step 5: Close the Doors

Close each of the 24 doors and secure them with glue or tape. Make sure that the doors are securely closed, so that the treats or gifts do not fall out.

Step 6: Add Ribbon or String

Finally, add ribbon or string to the top of the box to make it easy to hang your advent calendar. You can hang it on the wall or on your Christmas tree.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I personalize my advent calendar with doors?

A: You can personalize your advent calendar with doors by choosing wrapping paper or scrapbook paper with a design that matches your taste. You can also add personal notes or small gifts that are meaningful to you or your family.

Q: Can I reuse my advent calendar with doors?

A: Yes, you can reuse your advent calendar with doors year after year. Simply refill the compartments with new treats or gifts each year.

Q: How long will it take to create an advent calendar with doors?

A: The time it takes to create an advent calendar with doors will depend on your skill level and the materials you have on hand. However, it should take no more than a few hours to complete.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You have now created your very own advent calendar with doors for the year 2024. Your advent calendar will add a personal touch to your holiday decorations and bring joy to your family. Have fun opening each door and enjoying the treats and gifts inside!
