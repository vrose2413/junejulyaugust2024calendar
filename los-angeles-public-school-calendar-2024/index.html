What are the important dates to know?

The Los Angeles Public School Calendar for 2024 will include several important dates that students and parents should be aware of. The first day of school is expected to be on August 20, 2024, and the last day of school is expected to be on June 13, 2025. The calendar will also include the dates for winter and spring breaks, as well as other holidays and teacher in-service days.

Winter Break

The winter break for Los Angeles Public Schools during the 2024 academic year is expected to begin on December 23, 2024, and end on January 6, 2025. This break provides students and families with an opportunity to celebrate the holidays and spend time together. It is also a great time to travel and explore new places.

What are some fun activities to do during winter break?

Winter break is a great time to do some fun and exciting activities with your family and friends. Some popular activities include skiing or snowboarding in the mountains, ice-skating at an outdoor rink, visiting a winter festival, or taking a trip to a warm destination to escape the cold weather.

Spring Break

The spring break for Los Angeles Public Schools during the 2024 academic year is expected to begin on April 1, 2025, and end on April 8, 2025. This break provides students with a much-needed break from school and an opportunity to recharge before the final stretch of the academic year.

What are some productive ways to spend spring break?

Spring break is a great time to catch up on some much-needed rest and relaxation. However, it can also be a productive time to work on school projects or engage in community service. Students can also use this time to explore their interests and hobbies, or even start planning for college and future careers.

Teacher In-Service Days

The Los Angeles Public School Calendar for 2024 will include several teacher in-service days. These days are set aside for teachers to attend professional development workshops and training sessions, which helps them to improve their teaching skills and stay up-to-date with the latest educational trends.

What happens on teacher in-service days?

On teacher in-service days, students do not have to attend school. Instead, teachers spend the day attending workshops and training sessions. This provides them with an opportunity to learn new teaching strategies, collaborate with other teachers, and reflect on their practice. When teachers return to the classroom, they are better equipped to meet the needs of their students.
