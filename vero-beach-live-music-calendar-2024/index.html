Introduction

If you're a music enthusiast, then Vero Beach, Florida is the place to be. Known for its vibrant music scene, the city has a lot to offer, especially when it comes to live music. Whether you're into jazz, blues, rock, or country, you're sure to find something that suits your taste. In this article, we'll take a look at the Vero Beach live music calendar for 2024, so you can plan your music-filled year ahead.

The Venues

Before we dive into the calendar, it's important to note that Vero Beach has a variety of venues that offer live music. Some of the most popular ones include the Sunrise Theatre, Riverside Theatre, and the Vero Beach Museum of Art. With that in mind, let's take a look at the calendar.

The Calendar

January kicks off the year with a bang as the Sunrise Theatre hosts the annual Vero Beach Jazz and Blues Festival on the 13th and 14th. The festival features some of the biggest names in the industry, including Grammy Award-winning artists.

In February, the Riverside Theatre presents "Rock of Ages," a musical that takes you back to the 80s with classic rock hits. The show runs from February 7th to the 19th.

March sees the return of the Vero Beach Food, Wine, and Music Festival, held at the beautiful Riverside Park. The festival showcases the best of local cuisine, wine, and of course, music. It takes place on the 18th and 19th.

The month of April is all about country music, as the Vero Beach Country Music Festival takes over the Indian River County Fairgrounds on the 8th and 9th. The festival features both local and national country artists.

May is a more laid-back month, with the Vero Beach Museum of Art hosting "Jazz in the Garden" every Sunday. This free event features local jazz bands and is the perfect way to spend a lazy Sunday afternoon.

June brings the Vero Beach Summerfest, a two-day event that celebrates summer with live music, food trucks, and beach games. The festival takes place on the 17th and 18th at Riverside Park.

July is all about patriotism as the city hosts its annual Independence Day celebration at Riverside Park. The event features live music, food vendors, and a spectacular fireworks display.

August sees the return of the Vero Beach Blues Festival at the Riverside Theatre. The festival takes place on the 19th and 20th and features some of the best blues artists from around the country.

September is a month of classical music as the Atlantic Classical Orchestra kicks off its season with a concert at the Vero Beach High School Performing Arts Center on the 24th.

October brings the annual Vero Beach Oktoberfest, a celebration of German culture with live music, beer, and traditional cuisine. The festival takes place on the 7th and 8th at Riverside Park.

Question and Answer

Q: Are there any free live music events in Vero Beach?

A: Yes, there are several free events throughout the year, including Jazz in the Garden at the Vero Beach Museum of Art and the Independence Day celebration at Riverside Park.

Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks to the events?

A: It depends on the event. Some events allow outside food and drinks, while others do not. It's best to check the event's website or contact the venue directly for more information.

Conclusion

As you can see, Vero Beach has a lot to offer when it comes to live music. With events throughout the year, there's something for everyone. So mark your calendars and get ready for a year of great music in Vero Beach.
