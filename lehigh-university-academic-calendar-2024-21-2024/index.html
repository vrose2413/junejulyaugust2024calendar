Introduction

Lehigh University is a prestigious private research university in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. It was founded in 1865 and has since become known for its strong academic programs and research opportunities. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various fields, including engineering, business, arts, and sciences.

What is Lehigh University Academic Calendar?

The Lehigh University Academic Calendar is a schedule of important dates and deadlines for students, faculty, and staff. It includes the start and end dates for each semester, holidays, breaks, and other significant events. The calendar is typically released one or two years in advance to help students plan their courses and schedule accordingly.

Academic Programs at Lehigh University

Lehigh University offers a wide range of academic programs across various fields. Some of the popular undergraduate programs include engineering, business, arts, and sciences. The university also offers graduate and doctoral programs in these fields.

Engineering Programs

Lehigh University is known for its strong engineering programs, which include various disciplines such as chemical engineering, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, and materials science and engineering. The university also offers interdisciplinary programs such as bioengineering and environmental engineering.

Business Programs

The Lehigh University College of Business is one of the top-ranked business schools in the country. It offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in various business fields such as accounting, finance, marketing, and management.

Arts and Sciences Programs

Lehigh University offers a wide range of arts and sciences programs, including majors in psychology, sociology, political science, English, and history. The university also offers interdisciplinary programs such as environmental studies and global studies.

Research Opportunities at Lehigh University

Lehigh University is a research-intensive university, with various opportunities for students, faculty, and staff to engage in research activities. The university has several research centers and institutes, including the Center for Advanced Materials and Nanotechnology, the Center for Photonics and Nanoelectronics, and the Center for Polymer Science and Engineering.

Undergraduate Research Opportunities

Lehigh University offers several undergraduate research opportunities, including the Summer Scholars Program, the Mountaintop Initiative, and the Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) program. These programs provide students with hands-on research experience and the opportunity to work with faculty members on various research projects.

Graduate Research Opportunities

Lehigh University offers several graduate research opportunities, including the Graduate Research Fellowship Program, the Dissertation Fellowship Program, and the Research Assistantship Program. These programs provide graduate students with financial support and the opportunity to conduct research in their respective fields.

Conclusion

Lehigh University is a prestigious private research university that offers a wide range of academic programs and research opportunities.
