Introduction

Alabama State University is a public institution located in Montgomery, Alabama. It was founded in 1867 and has since grown to become one of the top universities in the state. As the year 2023 comes to a close, students, faculty, and staff are already looking forward to the academic calendar for 2024. In this article, we will discuss the important dates and events that will take place during the academic year 2024 at Alabama State University.

When Does the Academic Year Start?

The academic year for Alabama State University typically starts in late August or early September. For the year 2024, classes will begin on August 26th, 2024. This is a Wednesday, which means students will have a full week of classes before the Labor Day holiday.

What Are the Important Dates to Remember?

There are several important dates to remember during the academic year at Alabama State University. Here are some of the most notable: - August 26, 2024: First day of classes - September 7, 2024: Labor Day holiday - October 11-12, 2024: Fall break - November 25-29, 2024: Thanksgiving break - December 9-13, 2024: Final exams - December 14, 2024: Fall semester ends

Spring Semester 2025

After the fall semester ends, students will have a short break before the spring semester begins. Classes for the spring semester will start on January 13th, 2025. The spring semester will be 16 weeks long, ending on May 2nd, 2025.

What About Spring Break?

Spring break is always an exciting time for students, and the dates for 2025 have already been set. Spring break for Alabama State University will take place from March 7th to March 14th, 2025. This is a great time for students to relax, recharge, and spend time with friends and family.

Summer Semester 2025

After the spring semester ends, students have the option to take summer classes. The summer semester at Alabama State University is divided into two sessions. The first session will start on May 12th, 2025, and end on June 19th, 2025. The second session will start on June 23rd, 2025, and end on July 31st, 2025.

What Are Some Important Dates for Summer Semester?

Here are some important dates to remember for the summer semester at Alabama State University: - May 12, 2025: First day of first session - May 31, 2025: Memorial Day holiday - June 23, 2025: First day of second session - July 4, 2025: Independence Day holiday - July 31, 2025: Last day of classes

Conclusion

In conclusion, the academic calendar for Alabama State University in 2024 is packed with important dates and events. From the first day of classes to final exams, students, faculty, and staff will stay busy throughout the year. Whether you are a current student or planning to attend Alabama State University in the future, it is important to stay up-to-date with the academic calendar. With this information, you can plan your schedule accordingly and make the most of your time at this prestigious institution.
