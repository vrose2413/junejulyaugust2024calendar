Introduction

For students, planning ahead is crucial to academic success. The Academy of Art University has released its academic calendar for the year 2024, allowing students to plan their schedules accordingly. In this article, we will provide an overview of the academic calendar and answer some frequently asked questions.

Academic Calendar Overview

The Academy of Art University's academic calendar for 2024 is divided into two semesters: fall and spring. The fall semester will begin on August 26, 2024, and end on December 20, 2024. The spring semester will begin on January 13, 2025, and end on May 9, 2025. There will also be a summer semester, which will begin on June 2, 2025, and end on August 8, 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I register for classes?

Registration for classes begins on April 1, 2024, and ends on August 25, 2024, for the fall semester. For the spring semester, registration begins on November 1, 2024, and ends on January 12, 2025. For the summer semester, registration begins on April 1, 2025, and ends on June 1, 2025.

Are there any holidays during the semester?

Yes, there are several holidays during each semester, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, and Martin Luther King Jr. Day. Please refer to the academic calendar for specific dates.

When are final exams?

Final exams for the fall semester will take place from December 16-20, 2024. Final exams for the spring semester will take place from May 5-9, 2025. Final exams for the summer semester will take place from August 4-8, 2025.

What happens if I need to drop a class?

If a student needs to drop a class, they must do so by the end of the add/drop period, which ends on September 6, 2024, for the fall semester, February 7, 2025, for the spring semester, and June 8, 2025, for the summer semester.

Can I take classes during the summer semester?

Yes, students have the option to take classes during the summer semester. However, it is important to note that the summer semester is condensed, and classes may move at a faster pace than during the fall or spring semesters.

Conclusion

The Academy of Art University's academic calendar for 2024 provides students with a clear timeline of important dates and deadlines. By planning ahead and utilizing the resources provided by the university, students can ensure their academic success. If you have any further questions, please refer to the academic calendar or reach out to the university's academic advising department.
