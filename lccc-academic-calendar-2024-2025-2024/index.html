Introduction

If you're a student at Lorain County Community College (LCCC), you're probably already thinking about the upcoming academic year. One of the most important things to keep in mind is the academic calendar. Here's everything you need to know about the LCCC academic calendar for 2024-2025.

Important Dates

The academic year for 2024-2025 at LCCC will begin on August 26, 2024, and end on May 9, 2025. There will be several breaks throughout the year, including:

  • Fall Break: October 14-15, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 27-29, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 16, 2024 - January 12, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 17-21, 2025

It's important to keep these dates in mind when planning your schedule for the year.

Course Offerings

LCCC offers a wide variety of courses throughout the academic year. You can find the full course catalog on their website, but here are some of the highlights:

  • Business and Entrepreneurship
  • Computer Science and Information Technology
  • Health Care and Nursing
  • Liberal Arts
  • Science and Engineering

Whether you're looking to earn your associate's degree or just take a few classes to improve your skills, LCCC has something for everyone.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I register for classes at LCCC?

A: You can register for classes online through the LCCC website. If you need assistance, you can also visit the Enrollment Services Center on campus.

Q: What if I need to withdraw from a class?

A: If you need to withdraw from a class, you should speak with your instructor first. They can help you decide if withdrawing is the best option. If you do decide to withdraw, you'll need to fill out a withdrawal form and submit it to the Enrollment Services Center.

Conclusion

The LCCC academic calendar for 2024-2025 is packed with opportunities for learning and growth. Whether you're a new student or a returning one, it's important to stay organized and on top of your schedule. By keeping these important dates and details in mind, you can make the most of your time at LCCC and achieve your academic goals.
