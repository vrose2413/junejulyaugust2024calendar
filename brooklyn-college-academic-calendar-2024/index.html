Introduction

Brooklyn College is a renowned institution that offers a wide range of academic programs. As a student, keeping track of important dates and deadlines is crucial to your success. What is the Brooklyn College Academic Calendar?

The Brooklyn College academic calendar is a schedule of important dates and deadlines for the academic year. It includes information about the start and end of each semester, holidays, registration dates, and other important events.

When Does the Academic Year Start and End?

The fall semester for 2024 will start on August 28th, and the spring semester will begin on January 22nd, 2025. The academic year will end on May 22nd, 2025.

There are several important dates to remember for the 2024 academic year. What is the Registration Deadline?

The registration deadline for the fall semester is August 22nd, 2024, and the registration deadline for the spring semester is January 15th, 2025. It is important to register before the deadline to avoid any late fees or other penalties.

What Happens During Final Exam Week?

Final exam week is the last week of each semester, and it is a crucial time for students. During this week, students take their final exams, and it is important to prepare well in advance. Make sure to review all your notes and attend any review sessions offered by your professors.

You can stay up-to-date with the Brooklyn College academic calendar by checking the college's website regularly. You can also download the academic calendar to your mobile device or computer.
