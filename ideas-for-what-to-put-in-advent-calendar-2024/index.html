Ideas for What to Put in Advent Calendar 2024

Advent calendars are a great way to count down the days until Christmas. Each day, you get to open a new surprise, which can be anything from candy to small toys. If you're planning to create your own advent calendar for 2024, here are some ideas for what to put in it:

Candy and Treats

Candy is a classic choice for advent calendars. You can choose from a variety of chocolates, hard candies, gummy candies, and more. You can also include treats like cookies, brownies, or other baked goods.

Small Toys and Trinkets

If you're making an advent calendar for kids, small toys and trinkets can be a great option. You can include things like toy cars, figurines, stickers, or small games. For adults, you can include things like keychains, bookmarks, or other small gifts.

Christmas Ornaments

Another great option for advent calendars is to include a new Christmas ornament each day. This can be a great way to build up your collection of ornaments over time. You can choose from a variety of styles, including traditional, modern, or personalized.

Activities and Crafts

If you want to make your advent calendar more interactive, you can include activities and crafts. You can include things like coloring pages, puzzles, or even small DIY projects that can be completed each day.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I include gift cards in my advent calendar?

A: Yes, gift cards can be a great addition to your advent calendar. You can include gift cards for coffee shops, restaurants, or online stores. Just make sure to check the expiration dates and any restrictions before including them.

Q: How many items should I include in my advent calendar?

A: This really depends on your budget and how big you want your advent calendar to be. Some people choose to include 24 items, while others might include only 12 or 16. You can also choose to include a mix of small and larger items to keep things interesting.

Q: Can I reuse my advent calendar from year to year?

A: Yes, if you make a reusable advent calendar, you can definitely use it year after year. Just make sure to store it carefully to keep it in good condition.

Conclusion

There are so many options for what to include in your advent calendar. Whether you choose candy, toys, ornaments, activities, or a mix of everything, it's sure to be a fun and festive way to count down the days until Christmas.
