What is the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 is a limited edition advent calendar that is set to release in November 2024. The calendar is inspired by the classic holiday movie Home Alone and features 24 doors that reveal a surprise Lego build each day leading up to Christmas.

What Can You Expect from the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 will feature various Home Alone-themed Lego builds, including settings and characters from the movie. You can expect to find mini figures of Kevin, Marv, Harry, and other iconic characters, along with builds of the McCallister house, the Wet Bandits' van, and other memorable scenes from the movie.

Where Can You Buy the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 will be available for purchase from Lego stores and online retailers such as Amazon and Walmart. However, due to its limited edition status, it is expected to sell out quickly, so make sure to grab yours as soon as possible!

What Makes the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 Special?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 is special because it combines two beloved holiday traditions: advent calendars and Lego building sets. It also appeals to fans of the classic Home Alone movie, making it the perfect gift for both Lego enthusiasts and movie buffs alike.

What Ages is the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 Suitable For?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 is suitable for children ages 6 and up, as well as adults who enjoy building with Lego bricks. It is also a great way for families to bond during the holiday season, as they can work together to build the different sets each day.

How Do You Use the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024?

The Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 is simple to use. Each day leading up to Christmas, you open one of the 24 doors to reveal a surprise Lego build. The builds can be completed on their own or combined with other Home Alone Lego sets to create a larger scene.

What Should You Expect from the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024 Unboxing?

When you unbox the Home Alone Lego Advent Calendar 2024, you can expect to find 24 numbered doors, each containing a different Lego build. You will also find an instruction booklet to help you assemble the builds, along with a poster featuring scenes from the movie.
