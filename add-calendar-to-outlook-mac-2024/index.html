Add Calendar To Outlook On Mac In 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Oct 04, 2022 · 3 min read

Before we get started, make sure you have the latest version of Outlook installed on your Mac. You can do this by going to the App Store and checking for updates.

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you're a Mac user, you probably already know how important it is to stay organized. One of the best ways to do that is by using a calendar app. And when it comes to calendar apps, Microsoft Outlook is one of the most popular choices out there. But how do you add a calendar to Outlook on Mac in 2024? In this article, we'll show you just that.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we get started, make sure you have the latest version of Outlook installed on your Mac. You can do this by going to the App Store and checking for updates.

Step 1: Open Outlook

The first step is to open Outlook on your Mac. You can do this by clicking on the Outlook icon in your Applications folder or by searching for it in Spotlight.

Step 2: Click on “File”

Once you have Outlook open, click on “File” in the top left corner of the screen.

Step 3: Select “New Calendar Subscription”

From the drop-down menu, select “New Calendar Subscription.”

Step 4: Enter the Calendar URL

Next, you'll need to enter the URL for the calendar you want to add. This will usually be provided to you by the person or organization who created the calendar.

Step 5: Customize the Settings

Once you've entered the URL, you can customize the settings for the calendar. You can choose the color for the calendar, the name of the calendar, and how often the calendar should update.

Step 6: Click “OK”

When you're done customizing the settings, click “OK” to add the calendar to Outlook.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I add multiple calendars to Outlook on Mac?

A: Yes, you can add multiple calendars to Outlook on Mac. Simply repeat the steps above for each calendar you want to add.

Q: How do I delete a calendar from Outlook on Mac?

A: To delete a calendar from Outlook on Mac, right-click on the calendar you want to delete and select “Delete Calendar.”

Q: Can I share my calendar with others in Outlook on Mac?

A: Yes, you can share your calendar with others in Outlook on Mac. To do this, right-click on the calendar you want to share and select “Sharing Permissions.” From there, you can choose who you want to share the calendar with and what level of access they should have.

Conclusion

Adding a calendar to Outlook on Mac is a simple and straightforward process. By following the steps above, you can easily add all of the important calendars in your life to Outlook and stay organized in 2024 and beyond.
