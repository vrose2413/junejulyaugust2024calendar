How To Sync Outlook Calendar With Samsung Calendar In 2024

Introduction

Are you tired of manually updating your calendar on both your Outlook and Samsung devices? Well, you're in luck! In this tutorial, we'll walk you through the steps to sync your Outlook calendar with your Samsung calendar in 2024.

Why Syncing Your Calendars is Important

Having your calendars synced saves time and ensures you're always up-to-date with your schedule. By syncing your calendars, any changes made on one device will automatically update on the other.

How to Sync Outlook Calendar with Samsung Calendar

Step 1: Open the Samsung Calendar app. Step 2: Tap the three horizontal lines in the top left corner. Step 3: Select "Manage calendars" from the drop-down menu. Step 4: Tap "Add account" and select "Microsoft Exchange ActiveSync." Step 5: Enter your Outlook email address and password. Step 6: Follow the prompts to complete the setup.

Question and Answer

Q: What if I don't have a Microsoft Exchange ActiveSync option on my Samsung device? A: You can download and install the "Microsoft Outlook" app from the Google Play Store and follow the same steps as above. Q: Will syncing my calendars affect my privacy? A: No, syncing your calendars only shares your schedule information between devices. Your personal information and data remain private.

Conclusion

Syncing your Outlook calendar with your Samsung calendar in 2024 is simple and efficient. By following the steps outlined in this tutorial, you'll save time and never miss a beat in your schedule.
