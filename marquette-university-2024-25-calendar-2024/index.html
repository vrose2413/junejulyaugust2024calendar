Why is the Academic Calendar Important?

The academic calendar is an essential tool for students, faculty, and staff at Marquette University. It provides a roadmap of the academic year, including start and end dates for each semester, registration deadlines, and holidays. Knowing the important dates on the academic calendar can help you plan your schedule, stay on track for graduation, and avoid any scheduling conflicts.

Important Dates for the 2024-25 Academic Year

The academic year at Marquette University is divided into two semesters: fall and spring. Here are the essential dates for the 2024-25 academic year:

  • Fall Semester Begins: August 26, 2024
  • Last Day to Add/Drop Classes: September 10, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 27-December 1, 2024
  • Finals Week: December 16-20, 2024
  • Fall Semester Ends: December 21, 2024
  • Spring Semester Begins: January 13, 2025
  • Last Day to Add/Drop Classes: January 27, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 16-20, 2025
  • Finals Week: May 5-9, 2025
  • Spring Semester Ends: May 10, 2025

Question and Answer

Q: What happens if I miss the deadline to add/drop classes?

A: If you miss the deadline to add or drop a class, you may still be able to make changes to your schedule by submitting a Late Add/Drop Form. However, there are strict guidelines for late adds and drops, and you may need to provide documentation to support your request.

Q: Can I take classes during the summer semester?

A: Yes, Marquette University offers summer sessions, including online courses, internships, and study abroad programs. Check the university’s website for more information on summer courses and registration dates.

Conclusion

Knowing the important dates on the academic calendar can help you plan your academic year at Marquette University. Make sure to mark your calendar with the start and end dates for each semester, registration deadlines, and holidays. If you have any questions or concerns about the academic calendar, contact the university’s Registrar’s Office for assistance.
