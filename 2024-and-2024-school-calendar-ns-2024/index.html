Introduction

When Does the 2024 School Year Start and End?

The 2024 school year is set to start on August 26, 2024, and will end on June 6, 2025. This is a total of 190 school days, including holidays and breaks.

Question: What are the holiday breaks in the 2024 school calendar?

The holiday breaks for the 2024 school calendar include:

  • Thanksgiving Break - November 28-29, 2024
  • Winter Break - December 23, 2024 - January 3, 2025
  • Spring Break - March 24-28, 2025
  • Memorial Day - May 26, 2025

Are There Any Teacher Work Days?

Yes, there are a total of 8 teacher work days throughout the school year. These days are typically used for professional development and planning.

Question: What happens on teacher work days?

On teacher work days, teachers have the opportunity to attend workshops, collaborate with colleagues, and plan their lessons for the upcoming weeks. These days are important for teachers to continue their professional development and provide the best education for their students.

What Are Some Important Events in the 2024 School Year?

There are several important events that will take place during the 2024 school year, including:

  • Back to School Night - August 29, 2024
  • Parent-Teacher Conferences - November 11-12, 2024 and February 10-11, 2025
  • Graduation - June 6, 2025

Question: What is Back to School Night?

Back to School Night is an event where parents can meet their child's teacher and learn more about the classroom expectations, curriculum, and goals for the school year. It's a great opportunity for parents to ask questions and get to know the teacher.

Question: When is Graduation?

Graduation for the 2024 school year will take place on June 6, 2025. This is an important milestone for students, as they celebrate the completion of their high school or middle school education.

Conclusion

Knowing the 2024 school calendar is important for students, parents, and teachers to plan ahead and stay on track throughout the school year. With this information, you can mark your calendars for important dates, plan your vacations around the holiday breaks, and prepare for the upcoming school year.
