What are the Garbage Pickup Days in Toledo for 2024?

Garbage pickup in Toledo is done on a weekly basis, but the day depends on the location. The city is divided into five different garbage districts, and each district has its designated garbage pickup day. The districts are:

  • District 1: Monday
  • District 2: Tuesday
  • District 3: Wednesday
  • District 4: Thursday
  • District 5: Friday

It's essential to know your district to avoid missing your garbage pickup day. You can find your district by visiting the city's website or contacting the city's waste management department.

What Time Does Garbage Pickup Start in Toledo?

Gabage pickup in Toledo starts at 7:00 am and goes on until all the scheduled pickups are done. It's important to have your garbage out by 7:00 am on your designated pickup day to avoid missing the pickup.

What Should You Do If You Missed Your Garbage Pickup Day?

If you missed your garbage pickup day, you have to wait until your next scheduled pickup day. You cannot leave your garbage out until that day as it will violate the city's sanitation laws. It's crucial to keep track of your garbage pickup days to avoid any inconvenience.

What Should You Do If You Have More Garbage Than the Bin Can Hold?

If you have more garbage than your bin can hold, you can contact the city's waste management department to request an extra bin. You can also take your excess garbage to the nearest landfill or transfer station. However, you'll have to pay a fee for this service.

What Should You Do If Your Garbage Bin is Damaged or Stolen?

If your garbage bin is damaged or stolen, you can contact the city's waste management department to request a replacement. The department will assess the damage and provide you with a new bin free of charge.

Conclusion

Knowing the Toledo garbage pickup calendar for 2024 is crucial to ensure efficient waste management. It's important to keep track of your garbage pickup day and follow the city's sanitation laws.
