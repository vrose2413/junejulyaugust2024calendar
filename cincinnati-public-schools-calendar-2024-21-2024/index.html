When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for the 2024-2025 school year will be August 19, 2024, and the last day of school will be June 6, 2025. This includes 180 instructional days and 5 professional development days for teachers.

What Are the Key Dates to Remember?

There are several key dates to remember for the 2024-2025 school year, including:

  • Thanksgiving Break: November 27-29, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 23, 2024-January 3, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 17-21, 2025
  • Memorial Day: May 26, 2025

It is important to note that these dates are subject to change and should be confirmed with your child's school.

How Do I Access the School Calendar?

The CPS calendar for the 2024-2025 school year can be accessed on the district's website. You can also find a printable version of the calendar to hang on your fridge or keep in your planner for easy reference.

What Happens If There Are Snow Days?

In the event of inclement weather, CPS may need to cancel school for the day. If this happens, the district will communicate the closure through various channels, including social media, local news stations, and the district's website. Make sure to have a plan in place for snow days, such as arranging for childcare or working from home.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation dates for the 2024-2025 school year have not yet been announced. However, they typically take place in late May or early June. Check with your child's school for more information.

What Are Some Tips for Staying Organized?

With so many important dates to remember, it can be overwhelming to stay organized. Here are a few tips to help:

  • Use a planner or digital calendar to keep track of important dates.
  • Set reminders on your phone or computer for key dates, such as parent-teacher conferences or school holidays.
  • Check the school calendar regularly and make note of any changes or updates.
  • Communicate with your child's teacher or school administrator if you have any questions or concerns.

What Are Some Benefits of Knowing the School Calendar?

Knowing the school calendar can have several benefits, including:

  • Being able to plan vacations and trips around school holidays and breaks.
  • Preparing for parent-teacher conferences and other important school events.
  • Staying informed about school closures or delays due to weather or other emergencies.

Staying up-to-date with the CPS calendar for the 2024-2025 school year is crucial for parents, teachers, and students.
