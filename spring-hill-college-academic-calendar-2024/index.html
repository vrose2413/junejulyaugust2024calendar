What is an Academic Calendar?

An academic calendar is a schedule of important dates and deadlines for a college or university. It typically includes the start and end dates of each semester or term, as well as holidays, breaks, and other important events. The academic calendar is an essential tool for students, faculty, and staff to plan their schedules and stay on track throughout the academic year.
