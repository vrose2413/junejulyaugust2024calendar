Chase Freedom Flex 5 Calendar 2024: Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Juan Stafford Jun 07, 2022 · 3 min read

Are you a Chase Freedom Flex cardholder? If so, you might be wondering what rewards you can expect in 2024. Chase has recently released its 5% cashback calendar for the year, and we’ve got all the details you need to know.

Are you a Chase Freedom Flex cardholder? If so, you might be wondering what rewards you can expect in 2024. Chase has recently released its 5% cashback calendar for the year, and we’ve got all the details you need to know.

What is the Chase Freedom Flex 5 Calendar?

The Chase Freedom Flex 5 Calendar is a schedule of bonus categories that will earn you 5% cashback on your purchases. Each quarter, Chase announces a new set of categories, and you’ll need to activate them in order to earn the bonus rewards.

What Are the Bonus Categories for 2024?

Here are the bonus categories for each quarter of 2024:

  • January - March: Grocery Stores, Drugstores, and Commuter Transportation
  • April - June: Gas Stations, Home Improvement Stores, and Wholesale Clubs
  • July - September: Restaurants, PayPal, and Select Streaming Services
  • October - December: Amazon.com, Department Stores, and Select Streaming Services

How Do I Activate the Bonus Categories?

To activate the bonus categories, simply log in to your Chase account and click on the “Activate Now” button for each category. You’ll need to activate the bonus categories every quarter in order to earn the 5% cashback.

What Are the Benefits of the Chase Freedom Flex Card?

The Chase Freedom Flex card offers a number of benefits in addition to the 5% cashback bonus categories:

  • No annual fee
  • $200 sign-up bonus after spending $500 in the first three months
  • 0% intro APR on purchases and balance transfers for the first 15 months
  • Cell phone protection insurance when you pay your monthly bill with your card
  • Travel and purchase protection benefits

What Should I Know Before Using My Card?

Before using your Chase Freedom Flex card, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Always pay your balance in full each month to avoid interest charges
  • Be sure to activate the bonus categories every quarter
  • Take advantage of the sign-up bonus by spending $500 in the first three months
  • Use your card for purchases that fall within the bonus categories to maximize your rewards

Final Thoughts

The Chase Freedom Flex 5 Calendar for 2024 offers a great opportunity to earn cashback rewards on your purchases. By activating the bonus categories each quarter and using your card wisely, you can maximize your rewards and take advantage of all the benefits that come with being a Chase Freedom Flex cardholder.

So what are you waiting for? Start planning your purchases for the year and get ready to earn some serious cashback!

Question and Answer

Q: Is there a limit to how much cashback I can earn?

A: No, there is no limit to how much cashback you can earn. However, the 5% bonus categories do have a cap of $1,500 in purchases per quarter.

Q: Can I use my Chase Freedom Flex card outside of the United States?

A: Yes, you can use your card outside of the United States. However, you may be subject to foreign transaction fees, so be sure to check with Chase before using your card abroad.

Q: How long do I have to activate the bonus categories?

A: You have until the 14th day of the last month in the quarter to activate the bonus categories. For example, if the bonus categories are for January-March, you have until March 14th to activate them.
