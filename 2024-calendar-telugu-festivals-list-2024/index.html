What is the Telugu Calendar?

The Telugu calendar is a lunar calendar that is followed by the Telugu-speaking people in South India. It is also known as the Panchangam or Panjika. The calendar is based on the movements of the moon and is divided into 12 months. Each month corresponds to a zodiac sign and has specific festivals and auspicious days.
