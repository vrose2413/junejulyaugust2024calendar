Introduction

Purdue University Global (PUG) is a public online university that offers various degree programs. The academic calendar for 2024 is now available, and it provides important dates for students to keep in mind.

What is Purdue University Global?

Purdue University Global is an online university that offers degree programs in various fields. It is a part of the Purdue University System and is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university aims to provide accessible and affordable education to students across the world.

What is the Academic Calendar?

The academic calendar is a schedule of important dates for students. It includes the start and end dates of each term, holidays, and other important events.

Important Dates

Spring Term

The spring term of 2024 will begin on January 8, 2024, and will end on April 28, 2024. There will be a spring break from March 11, 2024, to March 17, 2024.

Summer Term

The summer term of 2024 will begin on May 6, 2024, and will end on August 25, 2024. There will be no summer break.

Fall Term

The fall term of 2024 will begin on September 3, 2024, and will end on December 20, 2024. There will be a fall break from November 25, 2024, to November 29, 2024.

Summer Term

FAQs

When should I register for classes?

You should register for classes as soon as possible to ensure that you can enroll in the courses you want. It is recommended that you register at least two weeks before the start of the term.

What happens if I miss the registration deadline?

If you miss the registration deadline, you may still be able to enroll in classes during the late registration period. However, this is not guaranteed and may result in a late fee.

How do I access the academic calendar?

You can access the academic calendar on the Purdue University Global website. It is also available on the student portal.

What should I do if I need to withdraw from a course?

If you need to withdraw from a course, you should contact your academic advisor. They will be able to guide you through the process and provide you with any necessary information.

Conclusion

The academic calendar for Purdue University Global in 2024 provides important dates for students to keep in mind. It is important to register for classes early and to pay attention to deadlines. If you have any questions or concerns, be sure to reach out to your academic advisor for assistance.
