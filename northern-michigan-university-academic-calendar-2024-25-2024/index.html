Introduction

Northern Michigan University is a public university in Marquette, Michigan. It offers undergraduate and graduate degree programs to over 8,000 students. When does the academic year start and end?

The academic year for Northern Michigan University 2024-25 will start on August 26, 2024, and end on May 3, 2025. There will be two semesters, fall and winter, and a summer session.

What are the important dates for the fall semester?

What are the important dates for the winter semester?

What are the dates for the summer session?

Registration and Drop/Add Dates
