Outlook Sync With Google Calendar 2024: A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Ben Javu Apr 23, 2023 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Outlook is one of the most popular email clients in the world. However, when it comes to syncing with Google Calendar, many users face difficulties. In this article, we will discuss how to sync your Outlook with Google Calendar in 2024.

What is Outlook?

Outlook is a personal information manager developed by Microsoft. It is mainly used for email, but it also includes a calendar, task manager, contact manager, note-taking, journal, and web browsing.

What is Google Calendar?

Google Calendar is a time-management and scheduling calendar service developed by Google. It allows users to create and edit events.

Why Sync Outlook with Google Calendar?

There are many reasons why you might want to sync your Outlook with Google Calendar. For example, you might use Outlook at work but prefer Google Calendar for your personal life.

What are the Benefits of Syncing Outlook with Google Calendar?

Some of the benefits of syncing Outlook with Google Calendar include:
  • Having all your calendars in one place
  • Being able to view and edit your calendars from any device
  • Automatically syncing your events between Outlook and Google Calendar

How to Sync Outlook with Google Calendar in 2024

Syncing Outlook with Google Calendar is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:
  1. Open Outlook and click on the "File" tab.
  2. Click on "Options" and then select "Calendar".
  3. Under "Calendar Options", click on "Add Holidays".
  4. Click on "Add Calendar" and then select "From Internet".
  5. Paste the Google Calendar URL into the box and click "OK".
  6. Click "Yes" to subscribe to the calendar.
  7. Go to Google Calendar and click on the three dots next to the calendar you want to sync.
  8. Select "Settings and sharing".
  9. Under "Integrate calendar", copy the calendar link.
  10. Go back to Outlook and click on "Open Calendar".
  11. Select "From Internet" and paste the calendar link.
  12. Click "OK" to add the calendar to Outlook.

FAQs

Q. Can I sync multiple calendars?

Yes, you can sync multiple calendars. Simply repeat the steps above for each calendar you want to sync.

Q. How often will my calendars sync?

Your calendars will sync automatically every few minutes.

Q. Can I edit events in both Outlook and Google Calendar?

Yes, you can edit events in both Outlook and Google Calendar. The changes will automatically sync between the two.

Conclusion

Syncing Outlook with Google Calendar is a great way to keep all your calendars in one place. It's a straightforward process that can be done in just a few steps. By following the steps above, you can easily sync your Outlook with Google Calendar in 2024.
