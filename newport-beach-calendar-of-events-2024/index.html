What's New in 2024?

Newport Beach is always evolving, and 2024 is no exception. The city has planned some exciting new events that will be sure to capture your attention. One of the most anticipated events is the Newport Beach Food and Wine Festival. This festival will showcase the best of Newport Beach's culinary scene, with top chefs from around the world creating mouth-watering dishes.

Question:

When is the Newport Beach Food and Wine Festival taking place?

Answer:

The Newport Beach Food and Wine Festival is scheduled for May 24-27, 2024.

Top Events in 2024

While there are plenty of events to choose from, here are a few of our top picks:

Newport Beach Jazz Festival:

If you're a fan of jazz music, you won't want to miss the Newport Beach Jazz Festival. This event takes place in June and features some of the biggest names in jazz music.

Newport Beach Film Festival:

The Newport Beach Film Festival is a must-attend event for film enthusiasts. The festival takes place in April and showcases some of the best independent and international films.

Newport Beach Beerfest:

The Newport Beach Beerfest is a popular event that takes place in October. This event features over 150 craft beers from around the world, as well as food trucks and live music.

Question:

When does the Newport Beach Beerfest take place?

Answer:

The Newport Beach Beerfest is scheduled for October 5, 2024.

Family-Friendly Events

If you're traveling with kids, Newport Beach has plenty of family-friendly events to keep everyone entertained. Here are a few of our top picks:

Newport Beach Christmas Boat Parade:

The Newport Beach Christmas Boat Parade is a holiday tradition that takes place in December. This event features elaborately decorated boats cruising through Newport Harbor.

Newport Beach 4th of July Fireworks:

The Newport Beach 4th of July Fireworks is a spectacular event that takes place on the beach. The fireworks display is one of the largest in Southern California.

Newport Beach Farmers' Market:

The Newport Beach Farmers' Market is a weekly event that takes place on Sundays. This market features local produce, crafts, and live music.
