What is Aldi's beer advent calendar?

Aldi's beer advent calendar is a festive box filled with 24 different beers, one for each day leading up to Christmas. This is the perfect gift for beer lovers who want to try a variety of new and exciting beers during the holiday season.

How much does the calendar cost?

The price of the 2024 calendar is not yet confirmed, but it is expected to be around the same price as previous years, which is typically around $50. This is a great value considering the variety and quality of the beers included.

The Aldi beer advent calendar is typically available in Aldi stores across the United States during the holiday season. However, it is important to note that these calendars tend to sell out quickly, so it is recommended to purchase early or check with your local store for availability.
