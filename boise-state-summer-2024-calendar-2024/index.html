Boise State Summer 2024 Calendar: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Juan Stafford Mar 26, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

The summer of 2024 is just around the corner, and as a Boise State student, you may be wondering what your schedule will look like. Whether you’re taking classes, participating in internships, or enjoying some time off, it’s important to know what dates are important. In this article, we’ll go over the Boise State Summer 2024 Calendar, so you can plan your summer accordingly.

When Does Summer 2024 Begin and End?

The Boise State Summer 2024 semester will begin on May 6th, 2024, and will end on August 16th, 2024. This means that students will have a total of 14 weeks to complete their coursework or engage in other activities.

What are the Important Dates to Remember?

There are several important dates to keep in mind during the Boise State Summer 2024 semester. These include:
  • May 6th: First day of classes
  • May 30th: Memorial Day (university closed)
  • July 4th: Independence Day (university closed)
  • August 5th: Last day of classes
  • August 16th: End of the summer semester

What Classes Will be Offered?

Boise State will be offering a wide variety of classes during the summer of 2024. These will include both undergraduate and graduate courses, as well as online and in-person options. Some popular courses that will be offered include:
  • Intro to Psychology
  • Media Writing and Reporting
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Human Anatomy and Physiology

How Can I Register for Classes?

Registration for Boise State Summer 2024 classes will begin on March 18th, 2024. To register, you will need to log into your myBoiseState account and select the classes you wish to take. It’s important to register as early as possible, as classes can fill up quickly.

What if I Need Financial Aid?

If you need financial aid to help pay for your summer classes, you will need to fill out the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). The deadline to submit your FAFSA for the summer of 2024 is April 1st, 2024. Make sure to submit your application early, as it can take several weeks to process.

What Internship Opportunities are Available?

Boise State offers a wide range of internship opportunities for students during the summer months. These opportunities can help you gain real-world experience in your field of study and make important connections in your industry. Some popular internships available to Boise State students include:
  • Boise Philharmonic
  • Idaho Department of Environmental Quality
  • St. Luke’s Health System
  • The Idaho Statesman
  • Boise Fire Department

Can I Take Summer Classes Online?

Yes! Boise State offers a variety of online courses during the summer months. This can be a great option if you need to stay at home or work during the summer but still want to take classes. Online courses offer the same quality education as in-person courses and can be completed from anywhere with an internet connection.

What Other Activities Can I Participate in?

Boise State offers a variety of activities and events during the summer months. These can include:
  • Outdoor Adventure Program trips
  • Boise State Summer Symphony
  • Summer Movie Series
  • Boise State Summer Sports Camps
  • Summerfest Art and Craft Show

How Can I Stay Organized?

With so much going on during the summer months, it’s important to stay organized. One way to do this is to use a planner or calendar to keep track of important dates and deadlines. You can also use digital tools like Google Calendar or Trello to stay on top of your schedule.

Conclusion

The Boise State Summer 2024 semester offers a wide range of opportunities for students. Whether you’re taking classes, participating in internships, or enjoying some time off, it’s important to stay on top of your schedule and make the most of your summer. By using the information in this article, you can plan your summer accordingly and make it a success.
