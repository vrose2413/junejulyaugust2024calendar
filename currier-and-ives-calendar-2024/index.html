The History of Currier and Ives

Currier and Ives was a successful printmaking company that operated in the United States from 1834 to 1907. The company was known for producing high-quality lithographs that depicted scenes of American life. These prints were affordable and accessible to the general public, which made them incredibly popular.

The company produced a wide range of prints, including landscapes, historical events, and portraits. Some of the most popular prints featured winter scenes, which is why the Currier and Ives Calendar has become synonymous with the winter season.
