What is Buffalo Trace Bourbon?

Buffalo Trace Bourbon is a brand of Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey produced by the Buffalo Trace Distillery located in Franklin County, Kentucky. The brand has a rich history, dating back to 1773 when it was first established by Hancock Lee.
