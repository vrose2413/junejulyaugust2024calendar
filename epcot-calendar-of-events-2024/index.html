Epcot Calendar Of Events 2024: Your Ultimate Guide

Introduction

Epcot, the second theme park to open at the Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida, has been a popular destination for tourists and locals alike since 1982. With its combination of entertainment, education, and technology, Epcot attracts millions of visitors each year. If you’re planning a visit to Epcot in 2024, you’ll want to know what events are happening. In this article, we’ll give you the lowdown on the Epcot calendar of events for 2024.

What’s New in 2024?

Every year, Epcot introduces new attractions, shows, and events to keep things fresh and exciting. In 2024, there are several new additions to look forward to. One of the most anticipated is the debut of the Guardians of the Galaxy roller coaster, which promises to be one of the fastest and most thrilling rides in all of Disney World. Another new attraction is the “Journey of Water” walk-through experience, inspired by the film “Moana.”

Question:

What are some of the new attractions at Epcot in 2024?

Answer:

The new attractions at Epcot in 2024 include the Guardians of the Galaxy roller coaster and the “Journey of Water” walk-through experience.

Epcot Festivals

Epcot is known for its festivals, which celebrate everything from food and wine to art and music. In 2024, there are four major festivals to look out for:

1. Epcot International Festival of the Arts

This festival, which takes place in January and February, features live performances, art exhibits, and interactive workshops. It’s a great opportunity to experience the creativity and talent of Disney’s artists and performers.

2. Epcot International Flower & Garden Festival

Running from March to June, this festival showcases beautiful gardens, topiaries, and outdoor kitchens serving up fresh, seasonal cuisine. It’s a great way to celebrate the arrival of spring.

3. Epcot International Food & Wine Festival

One of the most popular festivals at Epcot, this event takes place from August to November and features food and drink from around the world. It’s a great opportunity to try new dishes and sample regional specialties.

4. Epcot International Festival of the Holidays

This festive event takes place in November and December and celebrates holiday traditions from around the world. From special food and drink offerings to live entertainment, this festival is a great way to get into the holiday spirit.

Question:

What are the four major festivals at Epcot in 2024?

Answer:

The four major festivals at Epcot in 2024 are the Epcot International Festival of the Arts, the Epcot International Flower & Garden Festival, the Epcot International Food & Wine Festival, and the Epcot International Festival of the Holidays.

Tips for Visiting Epcot in 2024

If you’re planning a visit to Epcot in 2024, here are some tips to help you make the most of your trip:

1. Plan Ahead

With so much to see and do at Epcot, it’s important to plan ahead to ensure you don’t miss anything. Use the My Disney Experience app to check wait times, make dining reservations, and schedule FastPass+ experiences.

2. Dress Comfortably

Epcot is a big park with lots of walking, so be sure to wear comfortable shoes and clothing. Dress in layers, as the weather can be unpredictable in Florida.

3. Try New Things

One of the best things about Epcot is the opportunity to try new things, whether it’s a new food or a new attraction. Don’t be afraid to step outside your comfort zone and try something new.

4. Take Advantage of Special Events

From festivals to holiday celebrations, Epcot offers a variety of special events throughout the year. Be sure to check the calendar and plan your visit accordingly.

Question:

What are some tips for visiting Epcot in 2024?

Answer:

Some tips for visiting Epcot in 2024 include planning ahead, dressing comfortably, trying new things, and taking advantage of special events.

Conclusion

Epcot is a one-of-a-kind theme park that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re a foodie, a thrill-seeker, or an art lover, you’ll find plenty to enjoy at Epcot in 2024. With new attractions and festivals, as well as tried-and-true favorites, Epcot is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Disney World. So start planning your trip today!
