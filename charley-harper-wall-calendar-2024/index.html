Who is Charley Harper?

Charley Harper was an American artist and illustrator born in 1922. He was known for his unique and modern style of wildlife art, characterized by bold shapes, colors, and minimal details. His work has been featured in various publications, museums, and galleries around the world.

What is the Charley Harper Wall Calendar?

The Charley Harper Wall Calendar is a yearly calendar that showcases some of Harper's most iconic artworks. Each month is represented by a different piece, ranging from birds and animals to landscapes and abstract designs. The calendar comes in a large format, perfect for displaying on your wall or desk.

What's new in the 2024 edition?

The 2024 edition of the Charley Harper Wall Calendar features twelve never-before-seen artworks by the artist. These pieces were discovered in Harper's archives and have been carefully selected to represent his unique style and vision. Additionally, the calendar includes important dates and holidays for the year 2024.

Why should you get the Charley Harper Wall Calendar?

If you're a fan of wildlife art, modern design, or simply looking for a beautiful and functional calendar, the Charley Harper Wall Calendar is a must-have. It's a great way to appreciate and enjoy Harper's art on a daily basis, while also keeping track of your schedule and important dates.

Where can you buy the Charley Harper Wall Calendar?

The Charley Harper Wall Calendar is available for purchase online on various websites, such as Amazon and the official Charley Harper website. It's also sold in selected bookstores, gift shops, and art galleries around the world.

How much does the Charley Harper Wall Calendar cost?

What are the benefits of using a wall calendar?

Using a wall calendar has many benefits, such as:

  • Helping you stay organized and on track with your schedule
  • Providing a visual representation of time and deadlines
  • Serving as a decorative and functional item for your home or office
  • Reducing the need for digital devices and screen time
  • Allowing you to easily plan and coordinate with others

What are some tips for displaying the Charley Harper Wall Calendar?

Here are some tips for displaying the Charley Harper Wall Calendar:

  • Write down important dates, events, or notes in colorful markers or stickers
  • Rotate the artwork every month to appreciate each piece equally
