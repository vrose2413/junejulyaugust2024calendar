If you're a student or parent at St. Peter's Prep in Jersey City, you're probably wondering what's in store for the school calendar in 2024. We've got you covered with all the important dates and events you need to know about.

When Does the School Year Start and End?

The 2024 school year at St. Peter's Prep will begin on September 4th, and end on June 14th. The school year will be broken down into two semesters, with final exams taking place at the end of each semester.

What Are the Major Holidays and Breaks?

St. Peter's Prep follows a traditional school calendar, with several major holidays and breaks throughout the year. The first major break will be for Thanksgiving, with students off from November 27th to November 29th. The Christmas break will begin on December 23rd and end on January 3rd. Spring break will take place from April 15th to April 22nd.

What About Graduation and Other Events?

The Class of 2024 will graduate on June 14th, with the ceremony taking place at the Prudential Center in Newark. Other important events throughout the year will include Homecoming, the Winter Formal, and the Junior Prom.

Academic Schedule

St. Peter's Prep operates on a rotating block schedule, with each day consisting of eight periods. The school day begins at 8:00am and ends at 2:45pm. Students are required to take a minimum of six courses each semester, with the option to take up to eight.

What Are the Graduation Requirements?

In order to graduate from St. Peter's Prep, students must complete a minimum of 24 credits. This includes four credits in English, four credits in social studies, three credits in mathematics, three credits in science, and two credits in a foreign language. Students also have the option to take additional elective courses to fulfill the remaining credits.

Extracurricular Activities

St. Peter's Prep offers a wide range of extracurricular activities for students to get involved in. These include sports teams, clubs, and student organizations.

What Sports Teams Are Available?

St. Peter's Prep has a variety of sports teams for students to join, including football, basketball, baseball, soccer, and lacrosse. The school also has a successful swimming and diving team, as well as a crew team.

What Clubs and Organizations Are Available?

There are over 50 clubs and organizations available for students to join at St. Peter's Prep. These include academic clubs, such as the Math Club and the Science Club, as well as service organizations like the Key Club and the National Honor Society.
