What is the 21 Day Fix Extreme Workout Calendar?

The 21 Day Fix Extreme workout calendar is a three-week program that combines intense workouts with a comprehensive nutrition plan. Created by fitness expert Autumn Calabrese, this program is designed to help you burn fat, tone your muscles, and get in the best shape of your life.

What Does the Program Include?

The program includes six 30-minute workouts that target different muscle groups, including upper body, lower body, cardio, and more. In addition, the program includes a nutrition plan that focuses on portion control and healthy eating habits.

Workout Calendar

The workout calendar is designed to help you stay on track and achieve your fitness goals in just 21 days. Each week, you'll complete a certain number of workouts, with rest days in between. Here's a breakdown of the workout calendar:

- Monday: Plyo Fix Extreme

Nutrition Plan

The nutrition plan is a critical component of the 21 Day Fix Extreme workout calendar. The plan focuses on portion control and clean eating, with an emphasis on lean protein, vegetables, and healthy fats. The program includes color-coded containers that help you measure out your portions and stay on track with your nutrition goals.

FAQs

Q: Is the 21 Day Fix Extreme workout calendar suitable for beginners?

A: While the program is designed to be challenging, it can be modified for beginners. If you're new to working out, be sure to start with lighter weights and listen to your body.

Q: Do I need any equipment to complete the workouts?

A: Yes, you'll need a set of dumbbells and a resistance band to complete the workouts. You may also want to invest in a yoga mat for the Pilates and yoga workouts.

Q: Can I still eat out while on the nutrition plan?

A: Yes, you can still eat out while on the nutrition plan. The program includes guidance on how to make healthy choices when dining out.

Q: What happens after the 21 days are over?

A: After completing the 21 Day Fix Extreme workout calendar, you can repeat the program or move on to another fitness program to continue your fitness journey.
