When does the Spring 2024 semester begin?

The Spring 2024 semester at Kennesaw State University will begin on January 8th, 2024. This is the first day of classes for the semester, so students should make sure they are prepared and have all necessary materials for their courses.

What holidays are observed during the Spring 2024 semester?

During the Spring 2024 semester, Kennesaw State University will observe several holidays. These include Martin Luther King Jr. Day on January 20th, Presidents' Day on February 17th, and Memorial Day on May 27th. On these holidays, the university will be closed and no classes will be held.

What is Spring Break?

Spring Break is a week-long break for students during the Spring semester. For the Spring 2024 semester, Spring Break will be held from March 11th to March 15th. Students can use this time to relax, travel, or catch up on coursework.

When is the last day of classes for the Spring 2024 semester?

The last day of classes for the Spring 2024 semester at Kennesaw State University will be April 30th, 2024. However, students should check with their individual professors to see if they have any assignments or exams due after this date.

When are final exams held?

Final exams for the Spring 2024 semester at Kennesaw State University will be held from May 2nd to May 8th, 2024. Students should check the official schedule for their exam dates and times.

Commencement is the ceremony where students receive their degrees and celebrate their accomplishments. For the Spring 2024 semester, Commencement will be held on May 14th, 2024.
