Football fans, get ready! The Green Bay Packers will be kicking off their season in September 2024. The opening game is always a big event in Green Bay, with fans from all over the country coming to see their favorite team play. This year's game will be against the Chicago Bears, one of the Packers' biggest rivals.

In February 2024, Green Bay will be hosting its annual Winter Festival. This event showcases all the fun winter activities that Wisconsin has to offer. From ice skating to snowshoeing, there will be something for everyone. There will also be live music and delicious food to enjoy.

Music lovers, mark your calendars for July 2024. Green Bay will be hosting a three-day music festival featuring some of the biggest names in the industry. Beer lovers, rejoice! In October 2024, Green Bay will be hosting its annual Oktoberfest celebration. This event features live music, delicious food, and of course, plenty of beer. There will also be a costume contest, so be sure to dress up in your best lederhosen or dirndl.
