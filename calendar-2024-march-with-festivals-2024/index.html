What are the cultural and religious festivals in March 2024?

March 2024 will be a month that is filled with various cultural and religious festivals. Some of the festivals that will be celebrated during this month include:

  • Holi - This is a Hindu festival that is celebrated on March 2. It is also known as the festival of colors as people throw colored powder at each other to celebrate the victory of good over evil.
  • Purim - This is a Jewish festival that is celebrated on March 3-4. It commemorates the salvation of the Jewish people from the evil Haman in ancient Persia.
  • St. Patrick's Day - This is an Irish festival that is celebrated on March 17. It is a day to celebrate Irish culture and heritage.
  • Nowruz - This is a Persian festival that is celebrated on March 20. It marks the beginning of the Persian New Year and is a time for renewal and new beginnings.
