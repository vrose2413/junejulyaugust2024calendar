Introduction

The Desert Sands Unified School District has released its calendar for the 2024-25 academic year. It is important for parents, students, and teachers to familiarize themselves with the calendar to plan their schedules accordingly.

When does the school year start and end?

The academic year for 2024-25 will begin on August 19th, 2024, and will end on June 6th, 2025. There will be a total of 180 school days.

What holidays and breaks are included in the calendar?

The calendar includes several holidays and breaks for students and teachers. These include Labor Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving Break, Winter Break, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Spring Break, and Memorial Day.

What are the important dates to remember?

There are several important dates to remember throughout the academic year. These include Back to School Night, Parent-Teacher Conferences, and Graduation.

Back to School Night

Back to School Night will be held on August 22nd, 2024. This is an opportunity for parents to meet their child's teachers and learn about the curriculum for the upcoming school year.

Parent-Teacher Conferences

Parent-Teacher Conferences will be held on November 4th and 5th, 2024. This is a chance for parents to meet with their child's teachers to discuss their academic progress.

Graduation

Graduation for high school seniors will be held on June 5th, 2025. This is a milestone event for students and their families, and marks the end of their high school journey.

Conclusion

It is important for parents, students, and teachers to familiarize themselves with the Desert Sands Unified School District calendar for the 2024-25 academic year. By doing so, they can plan their schedules accordingly and ensure that they do not miss any important dates.
