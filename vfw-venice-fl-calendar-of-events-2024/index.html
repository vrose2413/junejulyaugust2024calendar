VFW Venice FL Calendar of Events 2024

Are you looking for exciting events to attend in Venice, FL in 2024? Look no further than the VFW calendar of events! Whether you're a local or just visiting, there's something for everyone at these events.

January

The new year kicks off with the VFW's annual Polar Plunge on January 1st. Take a dip in the chilly waters of the Gulf of Mexico and start your year off with a refreshing splash. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a chili cook-off competition, where locals compete for the title of best chili in town.

February

Valentine's Day is celebrated at the VFW with a romantic dinner and dance. Bring your sweetheart and dance the night away to live music. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a Mardi Gras party, complete with beads, masks, and a Cajun-style buffet.

March

March brings the VFW's annual St. Patrick's Day parade and festival. Join the locals as they march through the streets of Venice, followed by a festival with live music, food, and drinks. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a golf tournament fundraiser, with all proceeds going to support local veterans.

April

The VFW celebrates Earth Day with a beach clean-up and picnic. Spend the day helping keep our beaches clean, then enjoy a picnic lunch with fellow volunteers. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a spring carnival, with rides, games, and fair food.

May

May brings the VFW's Memorial Day ceremony, honoring those who have given their lives in service to our country. Join the VFW in paying tribute to our fallen heroes. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a luau-themed party, with a pig roast, tropical drinks, and hula dancing.

June

The VFW's summer concert series kicks off in June, with live music every Friday night. Bring a lawn chair and enjoy the music under the stars. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a Father's Day BBQ, with burgers, hot dogs, and all the fixings.

July

Independence Day is celebrated at the VFW with a fireworks display over the Gulf of Mexico. Bring a blanket and watch the show from the beach. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a Fourth of July picnic, with classic American picnic fare.

August

The VFW's annual community garage sale takes place in August. Clean out your closets and bring your unwanted items to sell, or come shop for treasures. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a back-to-school party, with games, prizes, and school supply giveaways for local kids.

September

The VFW's Labor Day weekend pig roast is a can't-miss event. Enjoy slow-roasted pork and all the sides, along with live music. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a charity auction, with all proceeds going to support local veterans.

October

October brings the VFW's annual Halloween party, with costumes, dancing, and spooky treats. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a fall festival, with pumpkin carving, apple cider, and hayrides.

November

The VFW's Veterans Day ceremony honors local veterans and their service to our country. Join the VFW in paying tribute to our heroes. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a Thanksgiving feast, with all the traditional fixings.

December

The VFW's holiday party is a festive celebration of the season, with a visit from Santa, caroling, and a holiday feast. Later in the month, the VFW hosts a New Year's Eve bash, with live music, dancing, and a champagne toast at midnight.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I find out more information about these events?

A: Visit the VFW's website or Facebook page for more information on each event, including dates, times, and ticket prices.

Q: Do I need to be a member of the VFW to attend these events?

A: No, these events are open to the public. All are welcome to attend and celebrate with the VFW.

Q: Are these events family-friendly?

A: Yes, many of these events are designed for families and children. Check the event descriptions for more information on age-appropriateness.
