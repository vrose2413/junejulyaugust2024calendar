What is the Telugu Calendar?

The Telugu calendar is a traditional calendar used by Telugu-speaking people in India and around the world. It is based on the movement of the moon and consists of 12 months. Each month is named after a star or a constellation.

What is the Significance of the Telugu Calendar?

The Telugu calendar is an important part of Telugu culture and tradition. It is used to determine auspicious dates for weddings, housewarming ceremonies, and other important events. It is also used to calculate the dates for festivals and religious celebrations.

April 2024: Important Dates and Festivals

April 2024 is an important month in the Telugu calendar. It is the month of Vaisakha and is considered auspicious for new beginnings and fresh starts. Here are some important dates and festivals to keep in mind:

Ugadi

Ugadi is the Telugu New Year and is celebrated in the month of Vaisakha. In 2024, Ugadi falls on April 6th. It is a time for new beginnings and is celebrated with great enthusiasm and joy.

Rama Navami

Rama Navami is a Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of Lord Rama. It falls on the ninth day of the Hindu month of Chaitra, which usually falls in April. In 2024, Rama Navami falls on April 10th.

Chandramana Yugadi

Chandramana Yugadi is celebrated on the first day of the Hindu lunar calendar. It falls on the day of the new moon in the month of Chaitra, which usually falls in April. In 2024, Chandramana Yugadi falls on April 6th.
