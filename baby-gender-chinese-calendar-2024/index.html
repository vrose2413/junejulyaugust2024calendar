What is the Chinese Calendar Method for Gender Prediction?

The Chinese Calendar Method, also known as the Chinese Gender Predictor Chart, is an ancient way of predicting the gender of an unborn baby based on the mother's age at conception and the month of conception. This method is said to be over 700 years old and has been used by millions of couples worldwide.

How Does the Chinese Calendar Method Work?

The Chinese Calendar Method is based on the Chinese lunar calendar, which is different from the Western Gregorian calendar. The chart has a grid of 12 columns and 28 rows, which represent the 12 lunar months and the 28 days of each month. The mother's age at conception is placed on the left side of the chart, and the month of conception is placed at the top of the chart. The intersection of the two points on the chart will reveal the predicted gender of the baby.

Can the Chinese Calendar Method Predict the Gender of My Baby?

There is no scientific evidence to support the accuracy of the Chinese Calendar Method. However, many couples have claimed that the method has predicted the gender of their baby correctly. It is important to note that the Chinese Calendar Method is just a prediction and should not be relied on as the sole basis for preparing for the arrival of a baby.

Is the Chinese Calendar Method Accurate?

The accuracy of the Chinese Calendar Method is disputed, with some claiming that it has a success rate of up to 90%, while others claim that it is no more accurate than a coin toss. The accuracy of the method may depend on factors such as the accuracy of the mother's age and the accuracy of the lunar calendar used.

What are the Tips for Using the Chinese Calendar Method?

If you want to use the Chinese Calendar Method to predict the gender of your baby, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a reliable Chinese lunar calendar.
