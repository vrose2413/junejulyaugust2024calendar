Introduction

Are you a student at the University of Arizona? Are you looking for information about the Spring 2024 semester? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about the U of A Spring 2024 Calendar 2024. From important dates to registration deadlines, we’ve got you covered.

Important Dates

The U of A Spring 2024 semester begins on January 8, 2024, and ends on May 10, 2024. Here are some important dates to keep in mind:

  • January 7, 2024 – Last day to register without late fee
  • January 14, 2024 – Last day to add a class without instructor permission
  • February 18, 2024 – Last day to drop a class without a “W” grade
  • March 15-19, 2024 – Spring Break
  • April 19, 2024 – Last day to drop a class with a “W” grade
  • May 10, 2024 – Last day of classes

Registration

Registration for the Spring 2024 semester begins on November 6, 2023, and ends on January 7, 2024. It is important to register early to ensure that you get the classes you need. If you miss the January 7th deadline, you can still register until January 14th, but you will be charged a late fee.

Class Schedule

The U of A Spring 2024 class schedule is now available online. You can view it by logging into your UAccess account. Make sure to check the schedule regularly, as it is subject to change.

Tuition and Fees

The cost of tuition and fees for the Spring 2024 semester varies depending on your residency status, the number of credits you are taking, and other factors. You can view the current tuition and fees on the U of A Bursar’s website.

Financial Aid

If you need help paying for college, you can apply for financial aid. The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) for the 2023-2024 academic year is now available. Make sure to submit your FAFSA by the priority deadline of February 1, 2024, to be considered for the most aid.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still register for classes after January 7th?

A: Yes, you can still register until January 14th, but you will be charged a late fee.

Q: When is Spring Break?

A: Spring Break is from March 15-19, 2024.

Q: When is the last day to drop a class with a “W” grade?

A: The last day to drop a class with a “W” grade is April 19, 2024.

Conclusion

Now that you have all the information you need about the U of A Spring 2024 Calendar 2024, you can start planning your semester. Remember to register early, check the class schedule regularly, and apply for financial aid if needed. Good luck with your studies!
