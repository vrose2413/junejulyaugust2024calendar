When Does the Academic Year Start and End?

The academic year for Trinity College Dublin starts in September and ends in May. For the year 2024/23, the academic year will start on September 2, 2024, and end on May 16, 2025.

What Are the Important Dates to Remember?

Here are some important dates to remember for Trinity College Dublin's academic calendar 2024/23:

  • Orientation Week: August 26-30, 2024
  • Start of Teaching Term 1: September 2, 2024
  • Christmas Break: December 21, 2024-January 3, 2025
  • Start of Teaching Term 2: January 13, 2025
  • Easter Break: April 7-22, 2025
  • End of Teaching Term 2: May 3, 2025
  • Examination Period: May 6-16, 2025

What Are the Different Terms?

Trinity College Dublin has two teaching terms. Teaching Term 1 runs from September to December, and Teaching Term 2 runs from January to April. There is a break between the two terms for Christmas and Easter.

What Are the Examination Periods?

For the year 2024/23, the examination periods are as follows:

  • Teaching Term 1: December 16-20, 2024
  • Teaching Term 2: May 6-16, 2025

What Are the Holidays?

Trinity College Dublin observes several holidays throughout the year. Here are the holidays for the academic year 2024/23:

  • Christmas Break: December 21, 2024-January 3, 2025
  • Easter Break: April 7-22, 2025
  • May Bank Holiday: May 6, 2025

Can Students Take Time Off During Term Time?

Trinity College Dublin understands that students may need to take time off during term time due to unforeseen circumstances. However, students are advised to discuss their situation with their course coordinator before taking time off. Students who miss classes or assessments without prior approval may face academic penalties.

What Happens if a Class or Assessment Falls on a Holiday?

Classes and assessments scheduled on a holiday will be rescheduled to a different date. Students will be informed of the new date and time by their course coordinator.

Can Students Take Summer Courses?

Yes, Trinity College Dublin offers summer courses for students who wish to earn extra credits or catch up on missed coursework. The summer courses are usually held from May to August.

Trinity College Dublin's academic calendar for 2024/23 is packed with important dates and holidays.
