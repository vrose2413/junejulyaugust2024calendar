Introduction

When does the academic year start and end?

The academic year for Carver College of Medicine starts on August 26th, 2024 and ends on May 9th, 2025. This includes both the fall and spring semesters, as well as breaks in between.

What about breaks?

There are several breaks throughout the academic year. Fall break is from October 14th to October 15th, 2024. Winter break is from December 14th, 2024 to January 12th, 2025. Spring break is from March 16th to March 20th, 2025.

Registration and Deadlines

Registration for the academic year starts on April 1st, 2024 and ends on August 1st, 2024. It is important for students to register as soon as possible to secure their classes and ensure that they are on track to graduate on time.

What are the deadlines for dropping or adding classes?

The deadline for dropping or adding classes is September 9th, 2024. After this date, students will not be able to drop or add classes without permission from their academic advisor.

Conclusion
