How To Sync Your Outlook Calendar With Google Calendar In 2024

Written by Ben Javu May 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

In today's digital age, it's important to stay organized and keep track of important events and meetings. Outlook and Google Calendar are two popular tools that people use to manage their schedules. In this article, we will show you how to sync your Outlook calendar with your Google Calendar, so that you can access your events and meetings from both platforms.

Why Syncing Your Outlook Calendar with Google Calendar is Important

There are several reasons why you might want to sync your Outlook calendar with your Google Calendar. For example, you might use Outlook for work-related events and meetings, while you use Google Calendar for personal events and appointments. Syncing the two calendars will help you keep track of all your events and meetings in one place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Export Your Outlook Calendar

The first step is to export your Outlook calendar. To do this, open Outlook and click on "File"> "Open & Export"> "Import/Export". Choose "Export to a file" and then select "Outlook Data File (.pst)". Choose the calendar you want to export and select a location to save the file.

Step 2: Import Your Outlook Calendar into Google Calendar

The next step is to import your Outlook calendar into Google Calendar. Go to Google Calendar and click on the gear icon in the top right corner. Select "Settings"> "Import & Export". Choose the file you exported from Outlook and select the Google Calendar you want to import the events into.

Step 3: Set Up Automatic Syncing

To ensure that your Outlook and Google calendars stay in sync, you can set up automatic syncing. To do this, download and install the Google Calendar Sync tool. Once installed, open the tool and select the Outlook calendar you want to sync with your Google Calendar. Choose the sync intervals and click "Save" to start the syncing process.

FAQ

Q: Can I sync multiple Outlook calendars with Google Calendar?

A: Yes, you can sync multiple Outlook calendars with Google Calendar. Simply export each calendar separately and import them into Google Calendar.

Q: Will events on my Outlook calendar show up as busy on my Google Calendar?

A: Yes, events on your Outlook calendar will show up as busy on your Google Calendar. This will help you avoid scheduling conflicts.

Q: Can I still use Outlook and Google Calendar separately after syncing them?

A: Yes, you can still use Outlook and Google Calendar separately after syncing them. However, any changes you make to one calendar will not be reflected in the other calendar unless you manually sync them again.

Conclusion

Syncing your Outlook calendar with your Google Calendar is a simple process that can save you time and help you stay organized. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily sync your calendars and access your events and meetings from both platforms. Don't let scheduling conflicts get in the way of your productivity - sync your calendars today!
