Delete Recurring Event Iphone Calendar 2024

Written by Mable Stanley Apr 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you struggling to delete recurring events from your iPhone calendar for the year 2024? Do you find it frustrating to delete each event individually? If so, you're not alone. Many iPhone users face this issue and find it time-consuming. But don't worry, there's an easy solution to this problem. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to delete recurring events from your iPhone calendar for the year 2024.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open the Calendar App

The first step is to open the calendar app on your iPhone. You can find the calendar app on your home screen. Tap on the app to open it.

Step 2: Select the Recurring Event

Once you've opened the calendar app, select the recurring event that you want to delete. Tap on the event to open it.

Step 3: Tap on "Edit"

After opening the recurring event, tap on the "Edit" button located at the top right corner of the screen.

Step 4: Tap on "Delete Event"

Once you've tapped on the "Edit" button, scroll down the screen and tap on the "Delete Event" button. You'll find this button located at the bottom of the screen.

Step 5: Choose "Delete This Event Only" or "Delete All Future Events"

After tapping on the "Delete Event" button, you'll be prompted to choose between "Delete This Event Only" or "Delete All Future Events." If you want to delete only the selected event, choose "Delete This Event Only." If you want to delete all future events of the same series, choose "Delete All Future Events."

Step 6: Tap on "Delete"

Once you've made your selection, tap on the "Delete" button to confirm the deletion of the event(s).

Question and Answer

Q: Can I delete multiple recurring events at once?

A: Unfortunately, you can't delete multiple recurring events at once. You'll have to delete each event individually.

Q: Will deleting a recurring event delete all events in the series?

A: No, deleting a recurring event will only delete the selected event or all future events in the series, depending on your selection.

Q: Can I recover a deleted recurring event?

A: No, once you've deleted a recurring event, it's gone for good. Make sure to double-check before deleting any events.

Conclusion

Deleting recurring events from your iPhone calendar for the year 2024 is easy and straightforward. Just follow the steps we've outlined in this article, and you'll be able to delete any recurring event in no time. Remember, always double-check before deleting any events, as they can't be recovered once deleted.
