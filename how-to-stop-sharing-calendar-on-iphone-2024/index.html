How To Stop Sharing Calendar On Iphone 2024

Written by Ben Javu Aug 23, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Sharing your calendar with family and friends is a convenient feature on the iPhone. However, there may be times when you want to stop sharing your calendar for privacy reasons. In this article, we will discuss how to stop sharing your calendar on iPhone 2024.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open your Calendar App

The first step is to open your calendar app on your iPhone. This can be done by tapping on the calendar icon on your home screen.

Step 2: Tap on the “Calendars” Button

Once you have opened the calendar app, you will see a “Calendars” button at the bottom of the screen. Tap on this button to access your calendar settings.

Step 3: Select the Calendar You Want to Stop Sharing

In the “Calendars” section, you will see a list of all the calendars that you have set up on your iPhone. Select the calendar that you want to stop sharing by tapping on it.

Step 4: Tap “Edit”

After selecting the calendar, you will see an “Edit” button at the top right-hand corner of the screen. Tap on this button to access the calendar settings.

Step 5: Turn Off “Shared Calendar”

In the calendar settings, you will see an option for “Shared Calendar”. This is where you can control who you are sharing your calendar with. To stop sharing your calendar, simply turn off the “Shared Calendar” option.

Step 6: Confirm Your Changes

After turning off the “Shared Calendar” option, you will be asked to confirm your changes. Tap on “Stop Sharing” to confirm that you want to stop sharing your calendar.

Question and Answer

Q: Will Stopping Sharing My Calendar Delete Any Events?

A: No, stopping sharing your calendar will not delete any events that you have added to your calendar. It simply stops other people from being able to see your events.

Q: Can I Still See Events on Calendars That I am No Longer Sharing?

A: No, once you stop sharing your calendar, you will no longer be able to see events on that calendar.

Q: Can I Start Sharing My Calendar Again Later?

A: Yes, you can start sharing your calendar again at any time by following the same steps and turning on the “Shared Calendar” option.

Conclusion

Stopping sharing your calendar on iPhone 2024 is a straightforward process. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can ensure your privacy and keep your events to yourself. Remember, you can always start sharing your calendar again at any time.
