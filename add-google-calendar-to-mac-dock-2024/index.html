How To Add Google Calendar To Mac Dock In 2024

Written by Ben Javu Jul 15, 2022 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you're a Mac user, you know how important it is to have quick access to your favorite apps. One app that you might use frequently is Google Calendar. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to add Google Calendar to your Mac Dock, so you can access it with just one click.

Step 1: Open Google Calendar

To get started, open Google Calendar in your web browser. If you're not already signed in, enter your Google account information to access your calendar.

Step 2: Create a New Bookmark

Once you have Google Calendar open, click on the "Bookmarks" menu in your browser and select "Add Bookmark." Give the bookmark a name, such as "Google Calendar," and choose a location to save it. We recommend saving it to your bookmarks bar for easy access.

Step 3: Edit the Bookmark

Now that you have created a new bookmark for Google Calendar, it's time to edit it. Right-click on the bookmark and select "Edit." In the URL field, add "javascript:void(window.open('https://calendar.google.com/calendar/r','_blank'));" without quotes.

Step 4: Drag the Bookmark to Your Dock

Finally, it's time to add the Google Calendar bookmark to your Mac Dock. Simply drag the bookmark from your browser's bookmarks bar and drop it onto your Dock. You can now access Google Calendar with just one click!

FAQs

Can I Customize the Google Calendar Icon in My Dock?

Yes, you can customize the Google Calendar icon in your Dock. Right-click on the icon and select "Options" and then "Show in Finder." This will open the icon's file location. From there, you can replace the icon with a new one.

Can I Remove the Google Calendar Icon from My Dock?

Yes, you can remove the Google Calendar icon from your Dock. Simply right-click on the icon and select "Options" and then "Remove from Dock."

Can I Add Other Apps to My Dock?

Yes, you can add other apps to your Dock in the same way you added Google Calendar. Simply create a new bookmark for the app and edit the URL field to open the app when clicked.

Conclusion

Adding Google Calendar to your Mac Dock is a simple process that can save you time and increase your productivity. With just one click, you can access your calendar and stay organized. We hope this tutorial has been helpful!
