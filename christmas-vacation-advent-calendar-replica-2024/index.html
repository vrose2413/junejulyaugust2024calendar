What is an Advent Calendar?

In case you are not familiar with an Advent Calendar, it is a special calendar used to count down the days until Christmas. The calendar typically has 24 doors, each of which can be opened on a specific day leading up to Christmas. Behind each door, you will find a treat or small gift.
