Everything You Need To Know About The Ms State Calendar For Fall 2024

Written by Pauline Lafleur Oct 29, 2022 · 3 min read

As we approach the year 2024, many students and faculty members are already wondering about the MS State calendar for fall 2024. If you’re one of them, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the MS State calendar for fall 2024, including important dates, holidays, and more.

Introduction

As we approach the year 2024, many students and faculty members are already wondering about the MS State calendar for fall 2024. If you’re one of them, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the MS State calendar for fall 2024, including important dates, holidays, and more.

Important Dates

The MS State calendar for fall 2024 will officially begin on August 19, 2024, which is the first day of classes for most students. However, some students may have earlier start dates depending on their program or course schedule. It’s important to check with your advisor or department to confirm your start date.

The fall semester will continue until December 13, 2024, which is the last day of classes. Final exams will take place from December 16-20, 2024. It’s important to note that some classes may have different exam schedules, so be sure to check with your professor.

Holidays

Like every year, there will be several holidays during the fall semester of 2024. Here are the dates to keep in mind:

  • September 2, 2024 - Labor Day
  • November 11, 2024 - Veterans Day
  • November 28-29, 2024 - Thanksgiving Break

During these holidays, classes will not be in session and most university offices will be closed. However, some essential services may still be available, so be sure to check with the relevant department or office if you need assistance.

Registration Dates

If you’re planning to enroll in classes for the fall 2024 semester, it’s important to know the registration dates. Here are the key dates to keep in mind:

  • April 1, 2024 - Priority registration begins for current students
  • June 1, 2024 - Registration opens for new students
  • August 18, 2024 - Last day to register or add classes

It’s important to register for classes as soon as possible to ensure you get the courses you need and the schedule that works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: What happens if I miss the registration deadline?

A: If you miss the registration deadline, you may still be able to add or drop classes during the first week of classes. However, this is subject to availability and you may not be able to get into the courses you need.

Q: Can I change my class schedule after the semester begins?

A: Yes, you can make changes to your class schedule during the add/drop period, which typically lasts for the first week of classes. After that, you may still be able to withdraw from a course, but you will not be able to add any new classes.

Conclusion

By now, you should have a good understanding of the MS State calendar for fall 2024. Whether you’re a student, faculty member, or staff member, it’s important to keep these dates in mind to ensure a successful semester. If you have any further questions or concerns, be sure to reach out to the relevant department or office for assistance.
