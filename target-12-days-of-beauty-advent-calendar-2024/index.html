What is the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar?

The Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar is a limited edition holiday calendar that includes 12 beauty products. Each day leading up to Christmas, you get to open a new door and discover a new product. The calendar is perfect for those who love trying out new beauty products or for those who want to spoil themselves during the holiday season.

What's Inside the Calendar?

The contents of the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar vary each year, but you can expect to find a mix of skincare, makeup, and hair care products. In past years, the calendar has included products from popular brands such as Pixi, Bliss, and e.l.f. cosmetics. You can expect to find full-sized products as well as travel-sized products in the calendar.

When Will the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar be Available?

The Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar is usually released in early November, just in time for the holiday season. Keep an eye out for announcements from Target or check your local store for availability.

How Much Does the Calendar Cost?

The price of the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar varies each year, but it typically costs around $50. Keep in mind that the value of the products inside the calendar is usually worth much more than the price you pay for the calendar.

Is the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar Worth It?

If you're a beauty enthusiast or just love trying out new products, then the Target 12 Days of Beauty Advent Calendar is definitely worth it. The calendar gives you the opportunity to try out new products and brands at a discounted price. Plus, it's a fun way to count down to Christmas!
