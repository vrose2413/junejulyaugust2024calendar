Maui Calendar of Events 2024

If you're planning a trip to Maui in 2024, you're in for a treat! This beautiful island has so much to offer, from stunning beaches to lush rainforests. But one of the best things about Maui is its calendar of events. No matter what time of year you visit, there's always something exciting happening. Here's a look at some of the top events to look forward to in 2024:

January - March

Question: What events can I attend in January?

If you're in Maui in January, be sure to check out the Maui Whale Festival. This month-long event celebrates the annual migration of humpback whales to the Hawaiian islands. You can attend whale watching tours, educational seminars, and other fun activities.

Question: What's happening in February?

February is a great time to visit Maui if you're a fan of music. The Maui Songwriters Festival takes place this month, featuring performances from some of the best songwriters in the country. You can also attend workshops and other events to learn more about the craft of songwriting.

Question: Anything happening in March?

March is all about food on Maui, with the Maui County Agricultural Festival taking place this month. This event celebrates the island's local farmers, chefs, and food producers, with tastings, cooking demonstrations, and more.

April - June

Question: What's going on in April?

In April, you can attend the Maui Steel Guitar Festival, a celebration of the unique sound of Hawaiian steel guitar. This event features performances from some of the best steel guitarists in the world, as well as workshops and other activities.

Question: What's happening in May?

May is a great time to be on Maui if you're a fan of sports. The Maui Jim Maui Invitational takes place this month, featuring some of the best college basketball teams in the country. You can also attend other sporting events, such as golf tournaments and beach volleyball competitions.

Question: Anything happening in June?

June is a great time to be on Maui if you're a fan of the arts. The Maui Film Festival takes place this month, featuring screenings of some of the best independent films from around the world. You can also attend workshops and other events to learn more about filmmaking.

July - September

Question: What's going on in July?

In July, you can attend the Maui Jim Maui Classic, a world-class surfing competition that takes place at one of the island's most beautiful beaches. You can also attend other water sports events, such as paddleboarding and outrigger canoe racing.

Question: What's happening in August?

August is a great time to be on Maui if you're a fan of astronomy. The Maui Astronomy Club hosts a stargazing event this month, where you can observe the night sky with telescopes and learn more about astronomy.

Question: Anything happening in September?

In September, you can attend the Maui Marathon, a scenic race that takes you through some of the island's most beautiful landscapes. You can also attend other fitness events, such as yoga retreats and hiking expeditions.

October - December

Question: What's going on in October?

October is a great time to be on Maui if you're a fan of culture. The Maui County Fair takes place this month, featuring traditional Hawaiian music, dance, and food. You can also attend other cultural events, such as hula competitions and lei-making workshops.

Question: What's happening in November?

November is a great time to be on Maui if you're a fan of the outdoors. The XTERRA World Championship takes place this month, featuring some of the best off-road triathletes in the world. You can also attend other outdoor events, such as trail running races and mountain biking competitions.

Question: Anything happening in December?

In December, you can attend the Maui Arts and Cultural Center's Winter Wonderland, a festive event featuring holiday music, dance, and theater performances. You can also attend other holiday events, such as tree lighting ceremonies and Christmas parades.

No matter what time of year you visit Maui in 2024, there's sure to be an event that will make your trip even more memorable. So start planning your trip today and get ready to experience all that this beautiful island has to offer!
