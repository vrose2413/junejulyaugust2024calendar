Questions and Answers

Q: What if I miss the registration deadline?

A: If you miss the registration deadline, you may be able to register late with a $50 late fee. However, this is not guaranteed and depends on the availability of space in the classes you want to take.

Q: What is a W?

A: A W stands for "withdrawal." If you drop a class after the deadline to drop without a W, you will receive a W on your transcript. This does not affect your GPA, but it may affect your eligibility for financial aid or scholarships.

Q: What if I need to take a leave of absence?
