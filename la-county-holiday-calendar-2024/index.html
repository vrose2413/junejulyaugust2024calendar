When are the Major Holidays in LA County in 2024?

LA County observes many of the same holidays as the rest of the country. There are also some holidays that are unique to the area. Here is a list of the major holidays in LA County in 2024:

New Year's Day

New Year's Day falls on January 1st and is a national holiday. Most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Martin Luther King Jr. Day

Martin Luther King Jr. Day falls on the third Monday in January. It is a federal holiday and most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Presidents' Day

Presidents' Day falls on the third Monday in February. It is a federal holiday and most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Cesar Chavez Day

Cesar Chavez Day is a state holiday in California and is observed on March 31st. It honors the life and work of civil rights activist Cesar Chavez.

Memorial Day

Memorial Day falls on the last Monday in May. It is a federal holiday and most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Independence Day

Independence Day, also known as the Fourth of July, is a national holiday and falls on July 4th. Most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Labor Day

Labor Day falls on the first Monday in September. It is a federal holiday and most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Veterans Day

Veterans Day falls on November 11th and is a national holiday. Most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Thanksgiving Day

Thanksgiving Day falls on the fourth Thursday in November. It is a national holiday and most businesses and government offices will be closed.

Christmas Day

Christmas Day is a national holiday and falls on December 25th. Most businesses and government offices will be closed.

What are Some Lesser-Known Holidays in LA County?

LA County also has some holidays that are unique to the area. Here are a few lesser-known holidays that you might want to mark on your calendar:

Juneteenth

Juneteenth is a holiday that celebrates the end of slavery in the United States. It is observed on June 19th and is becoming more widely recognized in LA County.

Los Angeles Marathon

The Los Angeles Marathon takes place in March and is a major event in the city. Many businesses will be closed or have limited hours on this day.
