Calvert County Calendar Of Events 2024

Written by Pauline Lafleur Mar 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you're looking for something to do in Calvert County in 2024, you're in luck! The county has a diverse array of events planned for the year, ranging from festivals to concerts to sporting events. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the highlights of the upcoming calendar of events.

What are some of the biggest events happening in Calvert County in 2024?

One of the most highly anticipated events of the year is the Calvert County Fair, which is set to take place in September. This annual event is a celebration of the county's agricultural heritage, and features livestock shows, carnival rides, and live music. Another major event to look out for is the Southern Maryland Wine, Jazz, R&B, and Funk Festival, which is scheduled for August. This festival brings together some of the best local wineries and musicians for a day of wine tasting and live music.

What about sporting events?

Sports fans will have plenty to look forward to in 2024. The Chesapeake Bayhawks, a professional lacrosse team based in Annapolis, will be playing several home games in Calvert County throughout the season. The county is also home to several youth sports leagues, including baseball, softball, and soccer.

Tips for Attending Events

If you're planning on attending any of the events on the 2024 calendar, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First and foremost, be sure to check the event's website or social media pages for up-to-date information on dates, times, and ticket prices. It's also a good idea to arrive early, especially for popular events like the Calvert County Fair. This will give you plenty of time to park, find your way around, and get settled in before the event begins.

What should I bring to events?

Depending on the event, you may want to bring a few essentials with you. For outdoor events, be sure to pack sunscreen, a hat, and plenty of water. If you're attending a concert or festival, consider bringing a small backpack or tote bag to carry essentials like your phone, wallet, and any souvenirs you may purchase.

Review of Past Events

Of course, Calvert County has a rich history of hosting successful events. In 2023, the county hosted the annual Patuxent River Appreciation Days festival, which celebrates the natural beauty of the Patuxent River and its surrounding communities. The festival featured live music, food and craft vendors, and educational exhibits about the river's ecology and history. Visitors also had the opportunity to take boat tours and participate in outdoor activities like kayaking and fishing.

What do attendees have to say about past events?

According to attendees, the Patuxent River Appreciation Days festival was a huge success. Visitors praised the event's family-friendly atmosphere, delicious food, and engaging exhibits. Many also noted that they appreciated the opportunity to learn more about the river and its importance to the local community.

Tutorial: How to Plan Your Event

If you're interested in planning your own event in Calvert County, there are a few key steps you should follow. First, determine your event's purpose and target audience. This will help you choose a venue and plan activities that will appeal to your attendees. Next, create a budget and timeline for your event. Be sure to include all necessary expenses, such as venue rental, equipment rental, and marketing costs. Finally, promote your event through social media, flyers, and other channels. Consider partnering with local businesses or organizations to increase visibility and attendance.

What are some common mistakes to avoid when planning an event?

One common mistake is failing to plan for contingencies. Be sure to have backup plans in place in case of inclement weather, equipment failure, or other unforeseen circumstances. Another mistake is underestimating attendance or failing to properly staff your event. Be sure to have enough volunteers or employees on hand to ensure that everything runs smoothly.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Calvert County has a wide variety of events planned for 2024, from festivals to sporting events to concerts. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor from out of town, there's sure to be something on the calendar that piques your interest. So mark your calendars and get ready for a fun-filled year in Calvert County!
