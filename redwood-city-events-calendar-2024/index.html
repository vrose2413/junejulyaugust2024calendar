Introduction

Are you looking for exciting events to attend in Redwood City in 2024? Look no further! We have compiled a comprehensive calendar of events that you don't want to miss. From music festivals to food fairs, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city.

What Makes Redwood City Such a Great Place for Events?

Redwood City is a city located in the heart of Silicon Valley, California. It has a diverse population and a rich cultural heritage, which makes it an ideal place for events. The city is home to some of the best event venues in the region, including the Fox Theatre, the San Mateo County Event Center, and the Port of Redwood City.

Music Festivals

One of the most anticipated events in Redwood City is the Music on the Square series, which takes place every Friday night from June to September. The series features live music performances from a variety of genres, including jazz, blues, rock, and pop. The event is free to attend and attracts a large crowd of music lovers.

Another music festival to look out for is the Redwood City Salsa Festival, which takes place in July. The festival features live salsa music, dance performances, and a variety of food and drinks from local vendors.

What Are Some Other Exciting Events in Redwood City?

Food Fairs

Redwood City is known for its diverse food scene and the city celebrates this with a number of food fairs throughout the year. One of the most popular food fairs is the Redwood City Restaurant Week, which takes place in January. The event features prix fixe menus from some of the city's best restaurants.

The Redwood City Farmers' Market is another event that food lovers should not miss. The market takes place every Saturday from May to November, and features a variety of fresh produce, prepared foods, and crafts from local vendors.

Arts and Culture

Redwood City is home to a number of art galleries and museums, which host a variety of exhibitions and events throughout the year. One of the most notable events is the Redwood City Art and Wine Festival, which takes place in September. The festival features live music, wine tasting, and a variety of art and crafts from local vendors.

The Fox Theatre is another cultural landmark in Redwood City, which hosts a variety of events throughout the year, including concerts, comedy shows, and film screenings.

What Are Some Family-Friendly Events in Redwood City?

Festivals and Fairs

Redwood City is a family-friendly city and hosts a number of events that are suitable for all ages. One of the most popular events is the Redwood City Hometown Holidays, which takes place in December. The event features a parade, live music, and a tree lighting ceremony.

Another family-friendly event is the Redwood City Fourth of July Celebration, which features a parade, live music, and a fireworks display.

Conclusion

Redwood City is a vibrant and exciting city, with a calendar full of events that cater to a variety of interests. Whether you're a music lover, foodie, or art enthusiast, there is something for everyone in this city. So mark your calendars and get ready for an unforgettable year of events in Redwood City!

Q&A

Q: Are there any free events in Redwood City?

A: Yes, many events in Redwood City are free to attend, including the Music on the Square series and the Redwood City Farmers' Market.

Q: What is the best time to visit Redwood City?

A: The best time to visit Redwood City is during the summer months, when many outdoor events take place, such as the Music on the Square series and the Redwood City Salsa Festival.

Q: Can I bring my pet to events in Redwood City?

A: It depends on the event. Some events allow pets, while others do not. It is best to check with the event organizers before bringing your pet.
