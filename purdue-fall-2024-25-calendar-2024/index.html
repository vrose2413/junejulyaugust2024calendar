Academic Calendar Overview

The Fall 2024 semester at Purdue University will begin on August 26, 2024, and end on December 13, 2024. The following are the important dates to keep in mind:

Registration

Registration for the Fall 2024 semester will begin on March 25, 2024, for graduate students and April 1, 2024, for undergraduate students.

Classes Begin

Classes for the Fall 2024 semester will begin on August 26, 2024.

Thanksgiving Break

Thanksgiving Break will take place from November 27-December 1, 2024.

Final Exams

Final exams for the Fall 2024 semester will take place from December 6-13, 2024.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the Purdue Fall 2024-25 Calendar be officially released?

The Purdue Fall 2024-25 Calendar will be officially released in the Spring of 2024. Keep an eye on the Purdue University website for updates.

Will there be any changes to the academic calendar due to COVID-19?

As of now, Purdue University plans to resume in-person classes and return to a normal academic calendar for the Fall 2024 semester. However, the university will continue to monitor the situation and make adjustments as necessary.

What should I do if I miss the registration deadline?

If you miss the registration deadline, you may still be able to add classes during the first week of the semester with the instructor's permission. However, it is always best to register on time to ensure your spot in the classes you need.

When will I receive my class schedule?

You can view your class schedule on myPurdue once you have registered for classes. The schedule will also be available on the first day of classes.

What should I do if I need to drop a class?

If you need to drop a class, you can do so on myPurdue up until the drop deadline. Be sure to check with your academic advisor before dropping a class to avoid any negative impacts on your academic progress.

Conclusion

Now that you have all the important dates and information about the Purdue Fall 2024-25 Calendar, you can start preparing for the upcoming academic year with confidence. Don't forget to mark your calendars and register for classes on time!
