Are you a student or faculty member at Harvard Law School? If so, it’s never too early to start planning your schedule for the 2024-25 academic year. The HLS academic calendar has been released, and we’ve got all the important dates and deadlines you need to know.

When Does the 2024-25 Academic Year Begin?

The first day of classes for the 2024-25 academic year at HLS is Monday, August 26, 2024. This is the same date as the first day of classes for undergraduate students at Harvard University.

What Are the Major Dates to Remember?

There are several important dates to keep in mind as you plan your schedule for the 2024-25 academic year at HLS. Here are some of the most significant:

  • August 26, 2024: First day of classes
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day (no classes)
  • October 14-15, 2024: Fall Reading Period
  • October 16-27, 2024: Fall Exams
  • November 27-December 1, 2024: Thanksgiving Break (no classes)
  • December 10, 2024: Last day of classes
  • January 6-7, 2025: Winter Reading Period
  • January 8-19, 2025: Winter Exams
  • March 3-4, 2025: Spring Reading Period
  • March 5-16, 2025: Spring Exams
  • May 28, 2025: Commencement

How Can You Access the Full Academic Calendar?

If you want to see the full HLS academic calendar for the 2024-25 academic year, you can access it on the Harvard Law School website. The calendar includes important dates for registration, add/drop deadlines, and more.

Why Is It Important to Plan Ahead?

Planning ahead is crucial for success in law school. By knowing the important dates and deadlines for the upcoming academic year, you can ensure that you don't miss any key opportunities or fall behind in your coursework. It also allows you to plan your personal and professional obligations around your academic schedule.

What Else Should You Consider When Planning Your Schedule?

In addition to the important dates and deadlines listed on the HLS academic calendar, there are several other factors to consider when planning your schedule. Here are a few tips:

  • Think about your course load: How many classes do you want to take each semester? What are your academic goals for the year?
  • Consider extracurricular activities: Are there any student organizations you want to join? Do you plan to participate in any moot court or mock trial competitions?
  • Factor in your personal and professional obligations: Do you have any family or work commitments that may impact your schedule?
  • Don't forget about self-care: Law school can be stressful, so make sure to prioritize self-care activities like exercise, meditation, or spending time with friends and family.

Conclusion

Planning your schedule for the 2024-25 academic year at HLS may seem overwhelming, but with the help of the academic calendar and these tips, you can set yourself up for success. Remember to prioritize your academic goals, but also take care of yourself and enjoy the journey!
