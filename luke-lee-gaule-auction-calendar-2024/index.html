Introduction

Looking for an auction calendar for the year 2024? Look no further than the Luke Lee Gaule Auction Calendar. This comprehensive guide provides a list of all the auctions that Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty will be hosting in the upcoming year. Whether you're a seasoned auction-goer or a newbie to the scene, this calendar is a must-have for anyone interested in buying or selling at auction.

What is Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty?

Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty is a professional auction and real estate company based in Springfield, Illinois. With over 40 years of experience in the industry, they specialize in conducting auctions for estates, farms, and antiques. They also offer real estate services, including buying and selling properties.

Why Should You Attend an Auction?

Attending an auction can be a fun and exciting experience. It's an opportunity to find unique items that you may not be able to find anywhere else. Auctions also offer competitive prices, as items are sold to the highest bidder. Additionally, auctions can be a great way to meet new people and socialize with fellow auction-goers.

The Luke Lee Gaule Auction Calendar 2024

The Luke Lee Gaule Auction Calendar for 2024 is packed with exciting auctions throughout the year. From antiques and collectibles to farm equipment and real estate, there's something for everyone. Here are some of the highlights:

January - Antique Auction

Kick off the new year by attending the annual Antique Auction on January 6th. This event features a wide range of antiques, including furniture, glassware, and collectibles.

March - Estate Auction

On March 12th, Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty will be conducting an Estate Auction. This auction features items from local estates, including furniture, artwork, and jewelry.

May - Farm Equipment Auction

If you're in the market for farm equipment, be sure to attend the Farm Equipment Auction on May 7th. This auction features tractors, combines, and other farm machinery.

July - Real Estate Auction

On July 15th, Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty will be conducting a Real Estate Auction. This auction features properties from across the Midwest, including homes, farms, and commercial properties.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I register for an auction?

A: To register for an auction, simply visit the Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty website and fill out the registration form. You can also register in person on the day of the auction.

Q: What forms of payment are accepted?

A: Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty accepts cash, checks, and credit cards. However, some auctions may have specific payment requirements, so be sure to check the auction listing for details.

Q: Can I bid online?

A: Currently, Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty does not offer online bidding. However, they do accept absentee bids, which can be submitted prior to the auction.

Conclusion

Attending an auction can be a thrilling experience, and the Luke Lee Gaule Auction Calendar for 2024 offers a wealth of opportunities to buy and sell unique items. Whether you're looking for antiques, farm equipment, or real estate, be sure to check out the calendar and attend one of the many auctions hosted by Luke Lee Gaule Auction & Realty.
