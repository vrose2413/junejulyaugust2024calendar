Boston Public School Calendar 2024-25

As the school year comes to an end, it's time to start planning for the next one. The Boston Public School Calendar for 2024-25 has been released, and there are some important dates to keep in mind. In this article, we'll go over the calendar and answer some common questions about the upcoming school year.

When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for students in Boston Public Schools is September 4, 2024. The last day of school is June 12, 2025. There will be a total of 180 school days, including holidays and vacations.

What Are the Holidays and Vacations?

Fall Vacation

From November 25 to November 29, 2024, there will be a fall vacation. Thanksgiving Day falls on November 28, 2024, which is a Thursday.

Winter Vacation

From December 23, 2024 to January 1, 2025, there will be a winter vacation. Christmas Day falls on December 25, 2024, which is a Thursday.

February Vacation

From February 17 to February 21, 2025, there will be a February vacation.

Spring Vacation

From April 21 to April 25, 2025, there will be a spring vacation.

Memorial Day

Memorial Day falls on May 26, 2025, which is a Monday. There will be no school on this day.

Are There Early Release Days?

Yes, there will be early release days throughout the school year. The dates for early release days vary depending on the school, so be sure to check with your child's school for more information.

What About Snow Days?

If there is inclement weather, there may be a snow day. Boston Public Schools will notify families and staff if there is a snow day, and the day will be made up at a later date.

Conclusion

Now that you know the important dates for the Boston Public School Calendar for 2024-25, you can start planning for the next school year. Remember to check with your child's school for more information on early release days and other important dates. We hope this article has been helpful in answering your questions about the upcoming school year.
