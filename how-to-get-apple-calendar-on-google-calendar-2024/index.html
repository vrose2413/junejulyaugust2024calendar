How To Get Apple Calendar On Google Calendar In 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Apr 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you an Apple user who wants to use Google Calendar instead of the native Apple Calendar? You might be asking, "Is it possible to get Apple Calendar on Google Calendar?" The answer is yes! In this article, we will guide you on how to get Apple Calendar on Google Calendar in 2024.

Step 1: Export Apple Calendar

The first step is to export your Apple Calendar. Open the Calendar app on your Mac and select the calendar you want to export. Click on File > Export > Export. Choose a location to save the file, and click on Export again.

Question: Can I Export Multiple Calendars?

Yes, you can export multiple calendars. Just follow the same steps for each calendar.

Step 2: Import to Google Calendar

Next, you need to import the exported file to Google Calendar. Go to calendar.google.com and sign in to your account. Click on the three lines on the top left corner and select Settings > Import & Export. Choose the exported file from your Mac and click on Import.

Question: Will My Apple Calendar Events Sync Automatically with Google Calendar?

No, the events will not sync automatically. You need to repeat the process every time you want to update your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar events.

Step 3: Sync with Apple Calendar App

To sync your Google Calendar with the Apple Calendar app, you need to add your Google account to your Mac. Go to System Preferences > Internet Accounts > Google. Enter your Google account credentials and select the option to sync calendars.

Question: Can I Sync Multiple Google Calendars with Apple Calendar?

Yes, you can sync multiple Google Calendars with the Apple Calendar app. Just repeat the process for each calendar.

Step 4: Use Google Calendar on Apple Devices

Now that you have synced your Google Calendar with the Apple Calendar app, you can use it on your Apple devices. Open the Calendar app on your iPhone or iPad, and select your Google Calendar from the list of calendars.

Question: Can I Add Events to Google Calendar from Apple Devices?

Yes, you can add events to your Google Calendar from your Apple devices. Just make sure you select your Google Calendar as the default calendar.

Conclusion

Getting Apple Calendar on Google Calendar might seem like a daunting task, but it is actually quite simple. Just follow the steps outlined in this article, and you will be able to use Google Calendar on your Apple devices in no time. Remember to repeat the process every time you want to update your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar events.
