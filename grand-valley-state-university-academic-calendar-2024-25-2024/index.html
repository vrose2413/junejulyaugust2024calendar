Grand Valley State University Academic Calendar 2024-25

Introduction

Grand Valley State University (GVSU) is a public university located in Michigan, United States. The university has gained recognition for its high academic standards and exceptional facilities. As we approach the academic year 2024-25, it is essential for students, faculty, and staff to have access to the GVSU academic calendar. This article will provide a detailed overview of the academic calendar for the academic year 2024-25.

When does the academic year 2024-25 start?

The GVSU academic year 2024-25 is scheduled to start on August 26, 2024. This is the first day of classes for the fall semester.

What are the important dates for the fall semester?

The fall semester runs from August 26, 2024, to December 13, 2024. Here are the important dates to note: - August 26, 2024: First day of classes - September 2, 2024: Labor Day (university closed) - November 27-29, 2024: Thanksgiving break (university closed) - December 9-13, 2024: Final exam week

What are the important dates for the winter semester?

The winter semester runs from January 6, 2025, to April 25, 2025. Here are the important dates to note: - January 6, 2025: First day of classes - January 20, 2025: Martin Luther King Jr. Day (university closed) - March 3-9, 2025: Spring break - April 21-25, 2025: Final exam week

What about the summer semester?

The summer semester is divided into two sessions. Session I runs from May 5, 2025, to June 27, 2025, and session II runs from June 30, 2025, to August 22, 2025.

What are the important dates for the summer semester?

Here are the important dates to note: - May 5, 2025: Session I first day of classes - May 26, 2025: Memorial Day (university closed) - June 30, 2025: Session II first day of classes - July 4, 2025: Independence Day (university closed) - August 22, 2025: Last day of classes for the summer semester

What else should I know about the academic calendar?

It is important to note that the academic calendar is subject to change. Any changes will be communicated to students, faculty, and staff as soon as possible. It is also important to note that the academic calendar includes important deadlines for registration, fee payment, and add/drop periods. Students are encouraged to review the academic calendar regularly to stay informed.

Conclusion

The GVSU academic calendar for the academic year 2024-25 is an essential tool for students, faculty, and staff. This article has provided a detailed overview of the academic calendar, including important dates for the fall, winter, and summer semesters. It is important to note that the academic calendar is subject to change, and students should review it regularly to stay informed.
