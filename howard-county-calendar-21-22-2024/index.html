Howard County Calendar 21-22 2024: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Juan Stafford Jul 07, 2022 · 3 min read

Howard County Public School System (HCPSS) has released its academic calendar for the year 2023-2024. The calendar includes important dates such as the first and last days of school, holidays, and breaks. As a student or parent, it’s essential to be aware of these dates to plan vacations, appointments, and other activities. In this article, we’ll provide you with a detailed overview of the Howard County Calendar 21-22 2024.

Introduction

When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for students in HCPSS is August 28, 2023. The last day of school is set for June 6, 2024, for most students. However, high school seniors will have their last day on May 24, 2024, and middle school students will have their last day on June 3, 2024. The calendar also includes three inclement weather makeup days that may be used if needed.

What Are the Holidays and Breaks?

The academic calendar includes several holidays and breaks. Students and staff will have a day off on Labor Day (September 4, 2023), Thanksgiving Day and the day after (November 23-24, 2023), winter break (December 25, 2023, to January 1, 2024), Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 15, 2024), Presidents’ Day (February 19, 2024), and Memorial Day (May 27, 2024).

There will also be a spring break from April 15 to April 22, 2024, and two professional development days for staff on November 6, 2023, and March 4, 2024.

What About Parent-Teacher Conferences and Early Dismissals?

Parent-teacher conferences will be held on November 20, 2012, and March 20, 2024. There will be early dismissals for students on these days. Additionally, there will be early dismissals on December 21, 2023, and June 6, 2024, to mark the end of the semester and school year, respectively.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation ceremonies for high schools in HCPSS will be held on May 23-25, 2024. The dates and times for each school will be released closer to the event.

How Can You Access the Calendar?

The Howard County Calendar 21-22 2024 can be found on the HCPSS website. You can also download a PDF version of the calendar from the website for easy reference.

Conclusion

Knowing the important dates on the Howard County Calendar 21-22 2024 is crucial for parents and students. It allows for better planning of vacations, appointments, and other activities. By following the calendar, you can ensure that you don’t miss any important events throughout the school year.
