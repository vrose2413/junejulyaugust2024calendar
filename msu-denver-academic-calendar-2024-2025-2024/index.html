Introduction

MSU Denver has released its academic calendar for the year 2024-2025. This calendar contains important dates for students, faculty, and staff. It is essential to keep track of these dates to ensure a successful academic year. In this article, we will discuss the MSU Denver academic calendar 2024-2025 in detail and answer some frequently asked questions.

When does the academic year start?

The academic year for MSU Denver starts on August 26, 2024. This is the first day of classes for the fall semester.

When are the breaks?

The fall break will be from October 14-15, 2024, and Thanksgiving break will be from November 27-December 1, 2024. The winter break will start on December 20, 2024, and classes will resume on January 6, 2025. The spring break will be from March 16-20, 2025, and the semester break will start on May 9, 2025.

When are the finals?

Finals for the fall semester will be from December 9-15, 2024, and for the spring semester, it will be from May 5-11, 2025.

When is graduation?

Graduation for the 2024-2025 academic year will be on May 17, 2025.

FAQs

What is the last day to drop a class?

The last day to drop a class without a "W" grade is September 9, 2024, for the fall semester and February 3, 2025, for the spring semester.

When is the last day to withdraw from a class?

The last day to withdraw from a class with a "W" grade is November 11, 2024, for the fall semester and April 7, 2025, for the spring semester.

When is the last day to apply for graduation?

The last day to apply for graduation is March 1, 2025.

When is the last day to pay tuition?

The last day to pay tuition without a late fee is August 1, 2024, for the fall semester and January 2, 2025, for the spring semester.

When do classes end?

Classes end on December 6, 2024, for the fall semester and May 2, 2025, for the spring semester.

Conclusion

It is crucial for students, faculty, and staff to be aware of the MSU Denver academic calendar 2024-2025. This calendar contains essential dates that will ensure a successful academic year. Knowing when classes start, when breaks are, and when finals are will help students plan their schedules accordingly.
