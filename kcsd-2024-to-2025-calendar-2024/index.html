Introduction

The Kansas City School District (KCSD) has released its academic calendar for the academic year 2024-2025. When Does School Start and End?

The first day of school for KCSD students will be on August 12, 2024. The last day of school will be on May 23, 2025. The academic year will consist of 174 days, with breaks and holidays throughout the year.

What Are the Holidays and Breaks?

The KCSD calendar includes several holidays and breaks throughout the year. These include:

  • Labor Day: September 2, 2024
  • Fall Break: October 14-18, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 23, 2024-January 3, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 17-21, 2025
  • Memorial Day: May 26, 2025

What About Snow Days?

In the event of inclement weather, the KCSD has built-in snow days throughout the year. If the district needs to close due to severe weather, these days will be used to make up any missed instruction time.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation dates for the KCSD high schools are as follows:

  • Central High School: May 24, 2025
  • East High School: May 25, 2025
  • Lincoln College Preparatory Academy: May 26, 2025
  • Northeast High School: May 27, 2025
  • Northwest High School: May 28, 2025
  • Southwest Early College Campus: May 29, 2025
  • Southeast High School: May 30, 2025

What Are the Early Release Days?

The KCSD calendar includes several early release days throughout the year. On these days, students will be released from school early to allow for teacher professional development. The early release dates for the 2024-2025 academic year are:

  • September 25, 2024
  • November 6, 2024
  • January 29, 2025
  • March 12, 2025

What About Teacher Work Days?

The KCSD calendar includes several teacher work days throughout the year. On these days, students will not have school. The teacher work days for the 2024-2025 academic year are:

  • August 8-9, 2024
  • January 2-3, 2025
  • May 27-28, 2025

The KCSD 2024-2025 calendar includes important dates for the academic year, including the first and last day of school, holidays, breaks, snow days, and early release days. Planning ahead can help students and families make the most of the academic year and ensure a successful school year.
