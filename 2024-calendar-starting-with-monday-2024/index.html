What is the 2024 Calendar?

The 2024 calendar is a calendar that will be used in the year 2024. It is a 12-month calendar that starts on January 1st and ends on December 31st. The calendar is used to keep track of days, weeks, and months throughout the year.

Will the 2024 Calendar Start with Monday?

Yes, the 2024 calendar will start with Monday. This means that January 1st, 2024, will fall on a Monday. The calendar will follow a traditional format, with each month having either 30 or 31 days, except for February, which will have 28 days, or 29 in a leap year.

What is Leap Year?

Leap year is a year that has an extra day added to February. This is done to keep the calendar year synchronized with the solar year, which is the length of time it takes for the Earth to make one orbit around the sun. Leap years occur every four years, except for years that are divisible by 100 but not divisible by 400.

When is the Next Leap Year?

The next leap year will be in 2024. This means that February 2024 will have 29 days instead of the usual 28 days.
