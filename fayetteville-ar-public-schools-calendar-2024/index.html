Introduction

As we approach the year 2024, parents and students in Fayetteville, Arkansas are wondering about the upcoming school year calendar. It's important to plan ahead for vacations, appointments, and other events. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about the Fayetteville AR public schools calendar 2024.

When Does the School Year Begin and End?

According to the Fayetteville Public Schools website, the first day of school for the 2024-2025 academic year is August 19, 2024. The last day of school is May 29, 2025. This includes 176 instructional days, as required by the Arkansas Department of Education.

What Are the Holidays and Breaks?

Fayetteville public schools follow a traditional calendar with several holidays and breaks throughout the year. Here are the important dates to remember for the 2024-2025 school year: - Labor Day: September 2, 2024 - Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024 - Winter Break: December 23, 2024-January 3, 2025 - Spring Break: March 23-27, 2025 - Memorial Day: May 26, 2025

Are There Any Early Release Days?

Yes, Fayetteville public schools have several early release days throughout the year. This allows for professional development and teacher planning time. Here are the early release days for the 2024-2025 school year: - September 25, 2024 - October 23, 2024 - November 20, 2024 - December 18, 2024 - February 19, 2025 - March 19, 2025 - April 23, 2025 - May 28, 2025

What About Inclement Weather?

In the event of inclement weather, Fayetteville public schools may cancel classes or have a delayed start time. The school district will notify parents and staff through the district website, social media, and local news outlets.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation dates for Fayetteville public schools depend on the high school. Here are the tentative dates for the 2024-2025 school year: - Fayetteville High School: May 23, 2025 - Ramay Junior High School: May 22, 2025 - Woodland Junior High School: May 21, 2025

Can I Download the Calendar?

Yes, parents and students can download the Fayetteville AR public schools calendar 2024 on the district website. The calendar is available in both PDF and Google Calendar formats.

What If I Have More Questions?

If you have more questions about the Fayetteville AR public schools calendar 2024, you can contact the district office at (479) 444-3000. You can also visit the district website for more information.

Conclusion

Planning ahead is crucial for a successful school year. With the Fayetteville AR public schools calendar 2024, parents and students can prepare for holidays, breaks, and important events. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to the district office.
