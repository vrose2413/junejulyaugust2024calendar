Understanding the Telugu Calendar:

The Telugu calendar is a traditional lunar calendar used in the South Indian state of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is based on the cycles of the moon and includes both solar and lunar months. The calendar has a unique system of naming years, and 2024 is known as the "Sarvadhari" year.

Is Today a Good Day?

According to the Telugu calendar, today is the 23rd of Vaikasi, which is considered an auspicious day for various activities such as starting new ventures, buying property, and performing religious ceremonies. However, it is important to note that the auspiciousness of a day depends on various factors such as the individual's horoscope, the location of the activity, and other astrological considerations.

Question and Answer:

Q: What are some common activities that are considered auspicious on an auspicious day as per the Telugu calendar?

A: Some common activities that are considered auspicious include starting new ventures, buying property, performing religious ceremonies, and initiating new relationships such as marriages and engagements.

Q: Are there any activities that should be avoided on an inauspicious day?

A: Yes, there are certain activities that are considered inauspicious on certain days such as traveling, starting new projects, and signing important documents. It is best to consult with an astrologer or a knowledgeable person before undertaking any important activity.
