How To Share Your Google Calendar With Apple Calendar In 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Oct 15, 2022 · 2 min read

Are you tired of manually entering all your appointments and events into your Apple Calendar? Well, look no further because in 2024, Google has made it possible to share your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar seamlessly. In this article, we will guide you step-by-step on how to share your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar.

Are you tired of manually entering all your appointments and events into your Apple Calendar? Well, look no further because in 2024, Google has made it possible to share your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar seamlessly. In this article, we will guide you step-by-step on how to share your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar.

Why Share your Google Calendar with Apple Calendar?

Sharing your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar can be extremely useful for those who use both devices. It eliminates the hassle of having to enter the same events into both calendars and ensures that all your events are in sync. Additionally, sharing your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar allows you to easily collaborate and share events with others.

How to Share your Google Calendar with Apple Calendar?

Step 1: Open your Google Calendar and go to the settings menu by clicking on the gear icon in the top right corner.

Step 2: Click on “Settings and sharing” and scroll down to the “Integrate calendar” section.

Step 3: Click on “Public address in iCal format” and copy the link provided.

Step 4: Open your Apple Calendar and go to “File” > “New Calendar Subscription”.

Step 5: Paste the link you copied in Step 3 and click “Subscribe”.

Your Google Calendar should now be visible in your Apple Calendar. Any changes made in your Google Calendar will automatically be updated in your Apple Calendar.

Question and Answer

Q: What if I don’t want all my events to be visible in my Apple Calendar?

A: You can select which events you want to share by creating a new calendar in your Google Calendar and adding the events you want to share to that calendar. Then, follow the same steps mentioned above and subscribe to the new calendar instead of your main calendar.

Q: Can I share my Apple Calendar with Google Calendar?

A: Yes, you can share your Apple Calendar with your Google Calendar by exporting your Apple Calendar and importing it into your Google Calendar. However, any changes made in your Apple Calendar will not be automatically updated in your Google Calendar.

Conclusion

Sharing your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar can save you time and ensure that all your events are in sync. By following the simple steps mentioned above, you can easily share your Google Calendar with your Apple Calendar in 2024.
