What is the Melissa and Doug Monthly Calendar?

The Melissa and Doug Monthly Calendar is a wall calendar designed for families and individuals who want to stay organized and on top of their schedules. It's made from high-quality materials and features bright and engaging artwork that will appeal to both kids and adults.

Features

The Melissa and Doug Monthly Calendar has a number of features that make it stand out from other wall calendars:

Reusable Stickers

One of the most unique features of this calendar is the reusable stickers. Each month comes with a sheet of stickers featuring different holidays and events. Kids will love placing the stickers on the calendar and parents will appreciate the opportunity to teach them about different holidays and events.

Dry Erase Surface

The Melissa and Doug Monthly Calendar is made with a dry erase surface, which means you can write on it with a dry erase marker and wipe it clean. This is perfect for families who need to make changes to their schedules throughout the month.

Large Grid

The calendar features a large grid for each month, with plenty of space to write down appointments, birthdays, and other important events.
