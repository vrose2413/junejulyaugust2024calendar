When Does the Academic Year Start and End?

The academic year at Ohio State University typically starts in late August and ends in mid-May. For the 2024 academic year, classes will start on August 26, 2024, and end on May 11, 2025.

What Are the Important Dates to Know?

Fall Semester

  • August 26, 2024: First day of classes
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day (no classes)
  • November 27-29, 2024: Thanksgiving Break (no classes)
  • December 13-19, 2024: Final Exams

Spring Semester

  • January 6, 2025: First day of classes
  • March 10-16, 2025: Spring Break
  • April 28-May 4, 2025: Final Exams
  • May 11, 2025: Last day of classes/Commencement

What Are the Registration Deadlines?

The registration deadlines vary depending on the semester and type of student. For fall semester, the deadline is typically in early August. For spring semester, the deadline is typically in early January. It's important to check the OSU website for specific dates.

What Are the Holidays and Breaks?

OSU observes several holidays throughout the academic year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, and Memorial Day. In addition, there are breaks during the fall and spring semesters. For the 2024 academic year, the fall break will be October 21-22, 2024, and the spring break will be March 10-16, 2025.

What Are the Course Offerings?

OSU offers a wide range of courses across multiple disciplines. Some of the popular majors include business, engineering, healthcare, and education. It's important to check the course catalog to see which courses will be offered during the 2024 academic year.

What Are the Graduation Requirements?

Each major has its own set of graduation requirements, so it's important to check with your academic advisor to see what you need to do to graduate. In general, students must complete a certain number of credit hours, maintain a certain GPA, and fulfill any major-specific requirements.

What Are the Extracurricular Activities?

OSU has a vibrant campus life with plenty of extracurricular activities to choose from. Some of the popular options include student clubs, intramural sports, and cultural organizations. It's a great way to meet new people and get involved on campus.

What Are the Housing Options?

OSU offers a variety of housing options for students, including on-campus dorms, apartments, and off-campus housing. It's important to explore all the options and find the one that best fits your needs and budget.

What Are the Financial Aid Options?

OSU offers a range of financial aid options for students, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs. It's important to fill out the FAFSA and explore all the options to help pay for college.

Conclusion

Attending Ohio State University in 2024 can be an exciting and rewarding experience.
