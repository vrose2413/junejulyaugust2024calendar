Introduction

As the year 2023 comes to a close, it’s time to start planning for the upcoming year. For parents and students in Lubbock ISD, one of the most important things to consider is the academic calendar. When does the academic year start and end?

The academic year for Lubbock ISD will start on August 19, 2024, and will end on May 23, 2025. The school year will be divided into two semesters, with each semester lasting around 18 weeks.

What are the holidays and breaks for the academic year?

Lubbock ISD has several holidays and breaks scheduled for the academic year 2024-21 2024. Here are some of the important dates to remember:

  • Labor Day: September 2, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 23, 2024 to January 6, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 10-14, 2025
  • Memorial Day: May 26, 2025

What are the teacher workdays?

There are several teacher workdays scheduled throughout the academic year. These are days when teachers will be working, but students will not be in school. Here are the teacher workdays for the academic year 2024-21 2024:

  • August 15-16, 2014
  • October 14, 2014
  • January 6-7, 2015
  • February 13, 2015
  • April 17, 2015

What is the graduation date?

The graduation date for Lubbock ISD seniors will be on May 24, 2025. The exact time and location are yet to be determined, but students and parents will be notified as soon as the details are finalized.

What are the important dates for testing?

For students in Lubbock ISD, there are several important testing dates to remember. Here are some of the dates for the academic year 2024-21 2024:

  • PSAT/NMSQT: October 16, 2024
  • STAAR EOC: December 2-6, 2014 and May 5-9, 2015
  • AP Exams: May 5-16, 2015

Conclusion

That’s everything you need to know about the Lubbock ISD calendar for the academic year 2024-21 2024. By planning ahead and keeping these dates in mind, you can make sure that you and your child are prepared for the upcoming school year.
