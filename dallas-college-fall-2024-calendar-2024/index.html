Introduction

Dallas College has released its calendar for Fall 2024, and students are eager to know the important dates for the upcoming semester. The calendar includes important events, deadlines, holidays, and academic schedules that students need to keep in mind.

When does the Fall 2024 semester start?

The Fall 2024 semester starts on August 26, 2024, which is a Thursday. Classes will begin at their regularly scheduled times, and students are advised to check their schedules and class locations before the first day of class.

When is Labor Day?

Labor Day falls on September 2, 2024, which is a Friday. All Dallas College campuses will be closed in observance of the holiday.

When is the last day to drop a class?

The last day to drop a class without receiving a grade is September 6, 2024, which is a Tuesday. After this date, students can still drop classes until November 8, 2024, but they will receive a grade of “W” on their transcript.

When is Thanksgiving Break?

Thanksgiving Break starts on November 27, 2024, which is a Monday, and ends on November 29, 2024, which is a Wednesday. All Dallas College campuses will be closed during this time.

When is the last day of classes?

The last day of classes for the Fall 2024 semester is December 13, 2024, which is a Tuesday. After this date, students will have a study day on December 14, 2024, before final exams begin on December 15, 2024.

Tips for a Successful Semester

Here are some tips for students to have a successful semester:

1. Attend all classes

Attending all classes is crucial for success in college. It helps students stay on top of their coursework and understand the material better.

2. Manage time effectively

Time management is important to balance schoolwork, extracurricular activities, and personal life. Students should create a schedule and prioritize their tasks.

3. Seek help when needed

Students should not hesitate to seek help when they are struggling with coursework. Dallas College offers tutoring services and has resources available for students.

Question and Answer

Q: How many holidays are there in Fall 2024?

A: There are two holidays in Fall 2024 – Labor Day on September 2, 2024, and Thanksgiving Break from November 27-29, 2024.

Q: Can students drop a class after November 8, 2024?

A: Yes, students can still drop classes after November 8, 2024, but they will receive a grade of “W” on their transcript.

Q: When do final exams start?

A: Final exams for the Fall 2024 semester start on December 15, 2024.

Conclusion

Dallas College’s Fall 2024 calendar provides important dates that students need to keep in mind. By following the tips for a successful semester and seeking help when needed, students can have a productive and fulfilling semester.
