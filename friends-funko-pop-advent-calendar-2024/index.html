The Countdown to Christmas Just Got More Exciting!

The holiday season is always filled with so much excitement and joy, and what better way to celebrate than with an advent calendar? And this year, the Friends Funko Pop advent calendar is going to make your countdown to Christmas even more special!

If you're a fan of the popular TV show Friends, then you definitely need to get your hands on this advent calendar. It's the perfect way to show your love for the show while also getting into the festive spirit.

What is the Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar?

The Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar is a special calendar that features a different Friends-themed Funko Pop figure for each day leading up to Christmas. There are a total of 24 figures, one for each day of December leading up to Christmas Eve.

Each figure is a surprise, so you won't know which one you'll get until you open the corresponding door on the calendar. But don't worry, every figure is a fan-favorite character from the show, so you're sure to love them all!

What Characters Can You Expect?

The Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar features all of your favorite characters from the show, including Rachel, Ross, Chandler, Monica, Joey, and Phoebe. Each character is dressed in a festive outfit, making them the perfect addition to your holiday decor.

Some of the characters even come with special accessories, like Ross with his holiday armadillo costume or Joey with his turkey head. You'll have to wait and see which figures you get!

Where Can You Buy It?

The Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar will be available for purchase at all major retailers, including Amazon, Target, and Walmart. It's expected to be released in early November, so be sure to mark your calendars and get yours before they sell out!

Is It Worth the Price?

The Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar is priced at around $60, which may seem a bit steep for an advent calendar. But when you consider that you're getting 24 Funko Pop figures, each of which can cost around $10 individually, it's actually a great deal!

Not only that, but it's a unique and fun way to celebrate the holiday season. And if you're a fan of Friends, it's definitely worth the price to add these festive figures to your collection.

Final Thoughts

The Friends Funko Pop Advent Calendar is the perfect way to count down to Christmas while also celebrating your love for the show. With 24 fan-favorite characters dressed in festive outfits, it's sure to bring a smile to your face every day in December.

Be sure to get yours before they sell out, and enjoy the holiday season with your favorite Friends characters!
