How To Move Your Calendar To The Bottom Of Outlook 2024

Written by Pauline Lafleur Sep 30, 2022 · 2 min read

Are you tired of scrolling down to see your calendar in Outlook 2024? By default, Outlook places the calendar on the right side of the screen, which can be frustrating if you prefer to have it at the bottom. Fortunately, there is an easy solution to this problem.

Table of Contents

The Problem:

Are you tired of scrolling down to see your calendar in Outlook 2024? By default, Outlook places the calendar on the right side of the screen, which can be frustrating if you prefer to have it at the bottom. Fortunately, there is an easy solution to this problem.

The Solution:

To move your calendar to the bottom of Outlook 2024, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open Outlook 2024

The first step is to open Outlook 2024 on your computer. Once it’s open, navigate to the “View” tab, which is located in the top menu bar.

Step 2: Click on “To-Do Bar”

In the “View” tab, click on “To-Do Bar”. This will open a drop-down menu with several options.

Step 3: Select “Options”

In the “To-Do Bar” drop-down menu, select “Options”. This will open a new window with several customization options.

Step 4: Choose “Calendar”

Within the “To-Do Bar Options” window, choose “Calendar” from the list of available options. This will move the calendar to the bottom of the screen.

Step 5: Click “OK”

Once you have selected “Calendar”, click “OK” to apply the changes. Your calendar should now be located at the bottom of Outlook 2024.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I customize the size of my calendar?

A: Yes, you can customize the size of your calendar by clicking and dragging the bottom edge of the calendar. This will resize the calendar to your desired height.

Q: What if I want to move my calendar back to the right side of the screen?

A: To move your calendar back to the right side of the screen, simply follow the same steps but choose “Right” instead of “Bottom” in Step 4.

Q: Will this change affect other users on my computer?

A: No, this change will only affect your user account on the computer. Other users will not be affected.

Conclusion:

Moving your calendar to the bottom of Outlook 2024 is a simple and easy process. By following the steps outlined above, you can customize your Outlook experience to better suit your needs. Whether you prefer your calendar at the bottom or on the right side of the screen, Outlook 2024 has you covered.
