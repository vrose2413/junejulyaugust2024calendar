Discover The Best Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar For 2024

Written by Juan Stafford May 21, 2022 · 3 min read

Advent calendars are a popular way to countdown the days until Christmas. Traditionally, they contained a piece of chocolate for each day leading up to the holiday. However, in recent years, advent calendars have become more diverse, with options ranging from beauty products to craft beer.

What is an Advent Calendar?

Advent calendars are a popular way to countdown the days until Christmas. Traditionally, they contained a piece of chocolate for each day leading up to the holiday. However, in recent years, advent calendars have become more diverse, with options ranging from beauty products to craft beer.

What is an Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar?

An Illinois craft beer advent calendar is a unique take on the traditional advent calendar concept. Instead of chocolate, each day leading up to Christmas features a different craft beer from Illinois. It's a fun way to try new beers and support local breweries during the holiday season.

Where Can I Find an Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar?

You can find Illinois craft beer advent calendars at a variety of retailers, including liquor stores and specialty beer shops. Some breweries may also offer their own advent calendars for purchase.

What Breweries are Featured in the Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar?

The breweries featured in the Illinois craft beer advent calendar may vary depending on the specific product you purchase. However, you can expect to see a range of breweries from across the state, including established names and up-and-coming favorites.

What Types of Beer are Included in the Advent Calendar?

The types of beer included in the Illinois craft beer advent calendar will also vary depending on the product you purchase. However, you can expect to see a variety of styles, including IPAs, stouts, porters, lagers, and more.

How Should I Store My Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar?

It's important to store your Illinois craft beer advent calendar properly to ensure that the beer stays fresh. Keep the calendar in a cool, dark place, such as a basement or pantry. Avoid exposing the beer to direct sunlight or high temperatures, as this can affect the taste and quality.

When Should I Start Drinking the Beers in My Advent Calendar?

The best time to start drinking the beers in your Illinois craft beer advent calendar is up to you! Some people choose to start on December 1st and enjoy one beer each day leading up to Christmas. Others prefer to wait until closer to the holiday to start their countdown.

Can I Buy an Illinois Craft Beer Advent Calendar as a Gift?

Yes, an Illinois craft beer advent calendar makes a great gift for any beer lover. Many retailers offer pre-packaged advent calendars that are ready to give as a gift. You can also create your own advent calendar by selecting a variety of beers and packaging them in a festive way.

What are the Benefits of Trying Different Beers?

Trying different beers is a fun and exciting way to expand your palate and discover new favorites. It can also help you learn more about the brewing process and the different styles of beer that are available.

How Can I Support Local Breweries?

One of the best ways to support local breweries is by purchasing their products. When you buy an Illinois craft beer advent calendar, you are supporting the breweries that are featured in the product. You can also visit local breweries, attend events, and spread the word about your favorite beers on social media.
