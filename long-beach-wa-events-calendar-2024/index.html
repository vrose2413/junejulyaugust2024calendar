What are the Main Events?

Long Beach is home to a number of annual events that are eagerly anticipated by locals and visitors alike. Some of the main events that will be taking place in 2024 include the Kite Festival, the SandSations Sandcastle Competition, and the Razor Clam Festival. These events are always a hit with families and provide plenty of entertainment for people of all ages.

Are There Any New Events?

Yes! In 2024, Long Beach will be introducing a new event called the Beach Clean-Up Day. This event is designed to bring the community together to help keep the beaches clean and beautiful. Volunteers will be provided with gloves and trash bags, and there will be plenty of opportunities to socialize and enjoy the fresh air while making a positive impact on the environment.

What About Music and Art?

Long Beach has a thriving arts and music scene, and 2024 will be no exception. The town will be hosting a number of concerts, art shows, and other cultural events throughout the year. Be sure to check out the Long Beach Museum of Art and the Columbia Pacific Heritage Museum for a taste of the local culture.

What Should I Know About Accommodations?

Long Beach has a variety of accommodations, including hotels, motels, vacation rentals, and campgrounds. During peak season, it is recommended that you book your accommodations in advance as they tend to fill up quickly. Be sure to check out the Long Beach Visitors Bureau website for a full list of available accommodations.

What Kind of Food Can I Expect?

Long Beach has a variety of dining options, ranging from seafood shacks to fine dining restaurants. Be sure to try some of the local seafood, including razor clams, Dungeness crab, and Pacific salmon. If you're in the mood for something sweet, be sure to check out the local ice cream shops and bakeries for a tasty treat.

What About Shopping?

Long Beach has a number of charming shops and boutiques that offer a wide range of goods, including souvenirs, clothing, and home decor. Be sure to check out the local farmers market for fresh produce and artisanal goods. If you're looking for a unique shopping experience, be sure to visit the World Kite Museum and Gift Shop.

Are There Any Outdoor Activities?

Long Beach is known for its beautiful beaches and stunning natural scenery. Visitors can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, kayaking, and fishing. Be sure to check out the Discovery Trail for a scenic walk or bike ride along the coast.

What Should I Know About Parking?

Parking in Long Beach can be challenging during peak season. There are a number of public parking lots available, but they tend to fill up quickly. Be sure to arrive early to secure a spot, or consider taking public transportation or walking to your destination.

What Should I Bring?

Be sure to pack plenty of sunscreen, as the sun can be strong on the coast. It is also recommended that you bring a light jacket or sweater, as the temperature can drop significantly in the evenings. If you plan on spending time on the beach, be sure to bring a beach towel, a beach chair, and plenty of snacks and water.

Conclusion

Long Beach, WA, is a charming coastal town that offers plenty of entertainment and activities for visitors of all ages. With a variety of annual events, cultural attractions, and outdoor activities, there is something for everyone in Long Beach.
