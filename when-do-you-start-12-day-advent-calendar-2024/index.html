Advent calendars are a popular way to count down the days until Christmas. They usually contain small gifts or treats that are revealed each day in December leading up to Christmas Day. Introduction

Advent calendars are a popular way to count down the days until Christmas. They usually contain small gifts or treats that are revealed each day in December leading up to Christmas Day. The 12 day advent calendar is a shorter version of the traditional 24-day advent calendar, but many people still wonder when to start it. In this article, we will discuss when to start the 12 day advent calendar in 2024.

What is a 12 Day Advent Calendar?

A 12 day advent calendar is a shorter version of the traditional 24-day advent calendar. Instead of counting down the days until Christmas Day, it counts down the 12 days of Christmas. Each day, a small gift or treat is revealed, just like in a regular advent calendar.

When Does the 12 Days of Christmas Start?

The 12 days of Christmas start on December 25th and end on January 5th. This is also known as the Twelfth Night. The 12 day advent calendar can be started on either December 14th or December 15th, depending on how you count the days.

When Should You Start Your 12 Day Advent Calendar?

It is recommended to start your 12 day advent calendar on December 14th. This will allow you to count down the 12 days of Christmas and end on January 5th. However, if you prefer to start on December 15th, that is also an option. Just make sure to adjust the days accordingly.

What Gifts Should You Include in Your 12 Day Advent Calendar?

The gifts or treats you include in your 12 day advent calendar will depend on your personal preferences. Some popular ideas include small chocolates, candy canes, mini ornaments, and other small trinkets. You can also include small notes or inspirational quotes to make each day feel special.

How to Make Your Own 12 Day Advent Calendar

If you want to make your own 12 day advent calendar, there are many DIY options available. You can use small envelopes, boxes, or bags to hold each day's gift. You can also decorate each one with festive wrapping paper or stickers. There are many online tutorials available to help you create your own unique advent calendar.

Where to Buy a 12 Day Advent Calendar

If you prefer to buy a pre-made 12 day advent calendar, there are many options available online and in stores. Some popular retailers that sell advent calendars include Amazon, Target, and Walmart. You can also find unique and handmade options on websites like Etsy.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the 12 day advent calendar is a fun and festive way to count down the 12 days of Christmas. It can be started on either December 14th or 15th, depending on your preference. Whether you choose to make your own or buy a pre-made one, there are many options available to make each day feel special. Happy holidays!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I start my 12 day advent calendar on December 13th?

A: No, it is recommended to start your 12 day advent calendar on either December 14th or 15th in order to end on January 5th.

Q: What are some unique gift ideas for a 12 day advent calendar?

A: Some unique gift ideas include small succulents, tea bags, mini puzzles, and small candles.
