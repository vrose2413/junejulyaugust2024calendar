What Are the Weather Conditions in Las Vegas in December?

The weather in Las Vegas in December is generally mild, with average temperatures ranging from the low 40s to the mid-50s. It can get chilly at night, so be sure to bring a jacket or sweater. Although it's not the warmest time of year, the cooler weather makes it ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking and golfing.

What Are the Best Outdoor Activities to Do in Las Vegas in December?

One of the best outdoor activities to do in Las Vegas in December is hiking. The cooler temperatures make it easier to tackle some of the more challenging trails, such as the ones at Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area. Golfing is also a popular activity, and there are plenty of courses to choose from. Other outdoor activities include visiting the Hoover Dam and taking a scenic drive through the Valley of Fire State Park.
