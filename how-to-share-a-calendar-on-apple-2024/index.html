How To Share A Calendar On Apple 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Nov 10, 2022 · 4 min read

Sharing a calendar on Apple devices has become increasingly important as we rely more on digital tools to manage our schedules. Knowing how to share a calendar can help you collaborate with colleagues, keep family members in the loop, or simply manage your own schedule more effectively. In this article, we’ll explore the steps to share a calendar on Apple devices in 2024.

Table of Contents

Introduction

Sharing a calendar on Apple devices has become increasingly important as we rely more on digital tools to manage our schedules. Knowing how to share a calendar can help you collaborate with colleagues, keep family members in the loop, or simply manage your own schedule more effectively. In this article, we’ll explore the steps to share a calendar on Apple devices in 2024.

Step 1: Open the Calendar App

The first step to sharing a calendar on Apple is to open the Calendar app. This can be done on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. Once you have the app open, you will see any calendars that are currently synced to your device.

Step 2: Create a New Calendar

If you want to share a specific calendar with someone, you can create a new calendar for that purpose. To do this, click on the “Calendars” button at the bottom of the screen and then click “New Calendar”.

Step 3: Name Your Calendar

After you’ve created a new calendar, give it a name that reflects its purpose. For example, if you’re sharing a calendar with your team at work, you might name it “Work Schedule”.

Step 4: Add Events to Your Calendar

Before you share your calendar, make sure you have added any events that you want to include. This will ensure that everyone who has access to the calendar is up-to-date on all the details.

Step 5: Invite People to Share Your Calendar

To share your calendar, click on the “Calendars” button at the bottom of the screen and then click on the calendar you want to share. Next, click on the “i” button next to the calendar name and then click “Add Person”.

Step 6: Choose How to Share Your Calendar

When you invite someone to share your calendar, you can choose to share it in one of three ways:

Option 1: Share via iCloud

If the person you are sharing with also has an Apple device, you can share the calendar via iCloud. This will allow them to view and edit the calendar in real-time.

Option 2: Share via Email

If the person you are sharing with does not have an Apple device, you can share the calendar via email. They will receive an email with a link to view the calendar in their browser.

Option 3: Share via Public Link

If you want to share the calendar with a wider audience, you can create a public link. This will allow anyone with the link to view the calendar, even if they don’t have an Apple device.

Step 7: Set Permissions

When you share your calendar, you can choose what level of permissions the other person has. For example, you can allow them to view and edit the calendar, or you can limit them to view-only access.

Step 8: Accept the Invitation

If you are invited to share someone else’s calendar, you will receive an email invitation. Click on the link in the email to accept the invitation and gain access to the calendar.

Step 9: View and Edit the Shared Calendar

Once you have access to a shared calendar, you can view and edit it just like you would your own calendar. Any changes you make will be reflected in real-time for everyone who has access to the calendar.

Step 10: Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues when sharing a calendar on Apple devices, try the following troubleshooting steps:

Q: I can’t see the calendar I’m trying to share.

A: Make sure you are signed in to the correct iCloud account and that the calendar is selected in the “Calendars” tab.

Q: I can’t invite someone to share my calendar.

A: Make sure you have the correct email address for the person you are trying to invite and that they are not already sharing a calendar with you.

Q: My changes aren’t showing up on the shared calendar.

A: Make sure you have a stable internet connection and that you are signed in to the correct iCloud account.

Conclusion

Sharing a calendar on Apple devices is a simple and effective way to collaborate with others and manage your own schedule more effectively. By following these steps, you can share a calendar with anyone, anywhere, at any time.
