Rose Bowl Calendar Of Events 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Jan 16, 2023 · 4 min read

The Rose Bowl is a famous stadium located in Pasadena, California. It is known for hosting the annual Rose Bowl Game, which is one of the most prestigious college football games in the United States. However, the stadium also hosts many other events throughout the year. In this article, we will take a look at the Rose Bowl calendar of events for the year 2024.

Table of Contents

Introduction

The Rose Bowl is a famous stadium located in Pasadena, California. It is known for hosting the annual Rose Bowl Game, which is one of the most prestigious college football games in the United States. However, the stadium also hosts many other events throughout the year. In this article, we will take a look at the Rose Bowl calendar of events for the year 2024.

January

In January, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Rose Bowl Flea Market. This is one of the largest flea markets in California, and it features over 2,500 vendors selling everything from vintage clothing to antique furniture. Admission is $10 per person, and parking is free.

Q: What are the hours of the Rose Bowl Flea Market?

A: The Rose Bowl Flea Market is open from 9am to 3pm on the second Sunday of every month.

February

In February, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Pasadena Half Marathon and 5K. This event attracts thousands of runners from all over the world, and it is known for its scenic route that takes runners through historic Pasadena. Registration fees range from $50 to $150 depending on the event and the time of registration.

Q: Can I register for the Pasadena Half Marathon on the day of the event?

A: No, registration for the Pasadena Half Marathon must be done online in advance.

March

In March, the Rose Bowl will host the annual California Beer Festival. This event features over 100 craft beers from breweries all over California, as well as live music and food trucks. Admission is $50 per person, and it includes unlimited beer tastings.

Q: Is the California Beer Festival a family-friendly event?

A: No, the California Beer Festival is a 21+ event, so children are not allowed.

April

In April, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Egg Bowl. This is an Easter egg hunt that is open to children of all ages. The event also features live entertainment, food trucks, and other activities. Admission is $5 per person, and children under 2 years old are free.

Q: Can I bring my own Easter basket to the Egg Bowl?

A: Yes, you can bring your own Easter basket or bag to collect eggs.

May

In May, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Cinco de Mayo Festival. This event celebrates Mexican culture and heritage with live music, traditional food, and dancing. Admission is $20 per person, and children under 12 years old are free.

Q: What time does the Cinco de Mayo Festival start?

A: The Cinco de Mayo Festival starts at 12pm and ends at 8pm.

June

In June, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Pasadena Chalk Festival. This event features hundreds of artists creating beautiful chalk drawings on the pavement around the Rose Bowl. Admission is free, and there are also food trucks and live music.

Q: Can I participate in the Pasadena Chalk Festival as an artist?

A: Yes, anyone can participate in the Pasadena Chalk Festival as an artist. Registration is free, but space is limited.

July

In July, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Fourth of July Celebration. This event features live music, food trucks, and a fireworks show. Admission is $20 per person, and children under 6 years old are free.

Q: What time does the Fourth of July Celebration start?

A: The Fourth of July Celebration starts at 5pm and ends after the fireworks show, which usually starts around 9pm.

August

In August, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Los Angeles Food and Wine Festival. This event features celebrity chefs, wine tastings, and food from some of the best restaurants in Los Angeles. Admission prices vary depending on the event and the time of registration.

Q: Can I buy tickets for the Los Angeles Food and Wine Festival at the door?

A: No, all tickets for the Los Angeles Food and Wine Festival must be purchased in advance online.

September

In September, the Rose Bowl will host the annual Pasadena Greek Fest. This event celebrates Greek culture with food, music, and dancing. Admission is $5 per person, and children under 12 years old are free.

Q: Is there a dress code for the Pasadena Greek Fest?

A: No, there is no dress code for the Pasadena Greek Fest, but comfortable shoes are recommended for dancing.

Conclusion

The Rose Bowl calendar of events for 2024 is packed with a variety of events for people of all ages and interests. From flea markets to marathons to food and wine festivals, there is something for everyone at the Rose Bowl. So mark your calendars and don't miss out on these exciting events!
