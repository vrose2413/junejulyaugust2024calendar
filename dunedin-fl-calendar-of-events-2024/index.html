Dunedin FL Calendar of Events 2024

Introduction

Dunedin, FL is a charming coastal city located in Pinellas County, Florida. It is known for its beautiful beaches, picturesque downtown, and rich cultural heritage. Every year, Dunedin hosts a wide range of events and festivals that attract visitors from all over the world. In this article, we will take a look at the Dunedin FL calendar of events for the year 2024.

January Events

New Year's Day Polar Plunge

Q: What is the New Year's Day Polar Plunge?
A: The New Year's Day Polar Plunge is an annual event where participants jump into the freezing waters of the Gulf of Mexico to celebrate the start of the new year.

This event takes place on January 1st at Dunedin Causeway Beach. It is a fun and unique way to start the year and is open to anyone who is brave enough to take the plunge.

February Events

Dunedin Fine Art Center's Wearable Art Show

Q: What is the Dunedin Fine Art Center's Wearable Art Show?
A: The Dunedin Fine Art Center's Wearable Art Show is an annual event that showcases one-of-a-kind wearable art pieces created by local artists.

The event takes place in mid-February and features a runway show where the pieces are modeled. It is a great way to support local artists and see some truly unique and creative pieces.

March Events

Dunedin Highland Games & Festival

Q: What is the Dunedin Highland Games & Festival?
A: The Dunedin Highland Games & Festival is an annual event that celebrates Scottish culture and heritage.

The event takes place in early March and features traditional Scottish games, music, and food. It is a great way to learn about Scottish culture and have fun with the whole family.

April Events

Dunedin Seafood Festival

Q: What is the Dunedin Seafood Festival?
A: The Dunedin Seafood Festival is an annual event that celebrates the bounty of the sea with delicious seafood dishes and live music.

The event takes place in mid-April and features a wide variety of seafood dishes from local restaurants. It is a great way to enjoy some fresh seafood and listen to some great music.

May Events

Dunedin Food Tour

Q: What is the Dunedin Food Tour?
A: The Dunedin Food Tour is a guided tour that takes visitors on a culinary journey through the city's best restaurants and foodie destinations.

The tour takes place in late May and is a great way to explore the local food scene and learn about the history and culture of the city.

June Events

Dunedin Blue Jays Baseball

Q: What is Dunedin Blue Jays Baseball?
A: Dunedin Blue Jays Baseball is a minor league baseball team that plays their home games at Florida Auto Exchange Stadium in Dunedin, FL.

The season runs from early June to early September and is a great way to enjoy a fun day out with the family.

July Events

Dunedin Orange Festival

Q: What is the Dunedin Orange Festival?
A: The Dunedin Orange Festival is an annual event that celebrates the city's history as a major orange grower in the early 20th century.

The event takes place in mid-July and features live music, food vendors, and plenty of oranges. It is a great way to learn about the city's history and enjoy some tasty treats.

August Events

Dunedin Beer Olympics

Q: What are the Dunedin Beer Olympics?
A: The Dunedin Beer Olympics are a series of drinking games that take place at various bars and restaurants throughout the city.

The event takes place in late August and is a fun way to enjoy some friendly competition and try some great local beers.

September Events

Dunedin International Film Festival

Q: What is the Dunedin International Film Festival?
A: The Dunedin International Film Festival is an annual event that showcases independent films from around the world.

The event takes place in early September and is a great way to see some unique and thought-provoking films.

October Events

Dunedin Wines the Blues

Q: What is Dunedin Wines the Blues?
A: Dunedin Wines the Blues is an annual event that combines the best of wine and blues music.

The event takes place in mid-October and features live music, food vendors, and plenty of wine. It is a great way to unwind and enjoy some great music and wine.

Conclusion

The Dunedin FL calendar of events for 2024 is filled with a wide variety of events and festivals that are sure to appeal to everyone. From the New Year's Day Polar Plunge to Dunedin Wines the Blues, there is something for everyone in this charming coastal city. So mark your calendars and come experience all that Dunedin has to offer!
