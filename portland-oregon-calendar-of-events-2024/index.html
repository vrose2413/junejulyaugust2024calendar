Introduction

Looking for the best events to attend in Portland, Oregon in 2024? Look no further! We have compiled a comprehensive list of events that you won't want to miss. From music festivals to food fairs, there is something for everyone. So, mark your calendar and get ready to have some fun!

January - March

Q: What events are happening in Portland in January 2024?

A: The year kicks off with the Portland International Film Festival, which showcases the best of international and independent cinema. If you're into beer, check out the Winter Ale Fest, where you can sample some of the best local brews.

Q: What can I expect in February?

A: February is all about celebrating love and art. The Portland Art Museum hosts the Love in the Time of War exhibit, which explores the impact of war on human relationships. You can also attend the Portland Jazz Festival, which features world-renowned jazz artists.

Q: What are the best events in March?

A: March is when the city starts to come alive with spring festivals. The St. Patrick's Day Parade is a must-see event, with colorful floats and marching bands. You can also attend the Portland Women's Film Festival, which showcases the work of female filmmakers.

April - June

Q: What's happening in April?

A: April is when the Portland Rose Festival kicks off. This month-long celebration features parades, concerts, and the famous Rose City Classic Dog Show. You can also attend the Portland Tattoo Expo, where you can see some of the best tattoo artists in the country.

Q: What's the best event in May?

A: May is all about food and drink. The Portland Taco Festival is a must-attend event, with dozens of food vendors serving up delicious tacos. You can also check out the Cider Summit, where you can sample over 150 ciders from around the world.

Q: What events are happening in June?

A: June is when the music festivals start. The Waterfront Blues Festival is a four-day event featuring some of the best blues musicians in the world. You can also attend the Pickathon Music Festival, which showcases a diverse range of musical genres.

July - September

Q: What's happening in July?

A: July is all about celebrating Independence Day. The Waterfront Blues Festival hosts a fireworks show that is not to be missed. You can also attend the Oregon Brewers Festival, where you can sample over 80 craft beers.

Q: What's the best event in August?

A: August is when the Oregon State Fair takes place. This family-friendly event features carnival rides, live music, and delicious fair food. You can also attend the Bite of Oregon, where you can sample some of the best food and drink in the state.

Q: What events are happening in September?

A: September is when the city celebrates the arts. The Portland Art Museum hosts the Northwest Arts & Crafts Show, where you can see some of the best local artists. You can also attend the Portland Film Festival, which showcases the work of emerging filmmakers.

October - December

Q: What's happening in October?

A: October is all about celebrating Halloween. The Portland Halloween Bazaar is a must-attend event, with dozens of vendors selling spooky goods. You can also attend the Portland Book Festival, where you can meet some of your favorite authors.

Q: What's the best event in November?

A: November is when the holiday season starts. The Holiday Ale Fest is a must-attend event, with dozens of local breweries serving up holiday-themed beers. You can also attend the Holiday Tree Lighting Ceremony, which takes place at Pioneer Courthouse Square.

Q: What events are happening in December?

A: December is when the city celebrates the holidays in full force. The Christmas Ship Parade is a must-see event, with dozens of boats decked out in holiday lights. You can also attend the Peacock Lane Christmas Lights, where you can see some of the best holiday displays in the city.

Conclusion

There you have it, your ultimate guide to the Portland Oregon Calendar of Events 2024. We hope this article has helped you plan your year and make the most of all the amazing events Portland has to offer. Don't forget to mark your calendar and have some fun!
