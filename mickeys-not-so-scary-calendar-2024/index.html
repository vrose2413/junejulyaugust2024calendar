Introduction

As we all know, Halloween is one of the most exciting times of the year. And what better way to celebrate than by visiting Walt Disney World's Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party? This year, the event will take place on select nights from August 15 to November 1, 2024. Here's everything you need to know about this year's event.

What to Expect

Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party is a family-friendly event that features trick-or-treating, special fireworks, parades, and other Halloween-themed entertainment. Guests are encouraged to dress up in their favorite costumes, but there are some rules to keep in mind. For example, costumes must be appropriate for all ages and may not include weapons or sharp objects.

Question: Can adults wear costumes at Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party?

Yes, adults are welcome to wear costumes to the party. However, there are some guidelines that must be followed, such as no masks for adults.

Entertainment

One of the highlights of Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party is the entertainment. The Boo to You Halloween Parade is a fan favorite, featuring Disney characters dressed in their Halloween costumes. The parade takes place twice per night, and it's a must-see for anyone attending the party. There's also a special stage show called the Hocus Pocus Villain Spelltacular, featuring the Sanderson Sisters from the Disney movie Hocus Pocus.

Question: What time does the Boo to You Halloween Parade start?

The parade takes place twice per night, with the first parade starting at 9:15pm and the second parade at 11:15pm.

Trick-or-Treating

Trick-or-treating is a big part of Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party. Guests can collect candy at various locations throughout the park. Trick-or-treat bags are provided, but guests are welcome to bring their own. Allergy-friendly treats are also available upon request.

Question: Can adults participate in trick-or-treating?

Yes, adults are welcome to participate in trick-or-treating. Just be sure to bring your own trick-or-treat bag!

Special Photo Opportunities

There are plenty of opportunities to take memorable photos at Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party. Disney PhotoPass photographers are stationed throughout the park, and there are special backdrops and props set up for Halloween-themed photos. Don't miss the chance to get your photo taken with some of your favorite Disney characters dressed in their Halloween costumes!

Question: Are the PhotoPass photos included in the price of admission?

No, the PhotoPass photos are not included in the price of admission. However, guests can purchase individual photos or a Memory Maker package that includes all of the digital photos taken during their visit to Walt Disney World.

Conclusion

Mickey's Not So Scary Halloween Party is a fun and festive way to celebrate Halloween with your family and friends. With trick-or-treating, special entertainment, and plenty of photo opportunities, it's no wonder why this event is so popular. Be sure to buy your tickets in advance, as they often sell out quickly!
