What is an Advent Calendar?

An advent calendar is a special calendar used to count the days of Advent in anticipation of Christmas. These calendars typically have 24 windows or doors, one for each day of December leading up to Christmas Eve. Each day, a window is opened to reveal a small gift or chocolate treat. Advent calendars have become increasingly popular over the years and are now available in a wide range of themes and designs.
