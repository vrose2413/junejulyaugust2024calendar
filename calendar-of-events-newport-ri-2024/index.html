Calendar of Events Newport RI 2024

Introduction

Newport, Rhode Island is known for its beautiful beaches, historic mansions, and vibrant community. The city is also known for its year-round calendar of events, which attracts visitors from all over the world. In 2024, Newport is expected to host a variety of events that cater to different interests and ages. In this article, we will explore some of the events that will take place in Newport in 2024.

January

New Year's Day Polar Plunge

Question: What is the New Year's Day Polar Plunge?

Answer: The New Year's Day Polar Plunge is an annual event that takes place at Easton's Beach in Newport. Participants jump into the cold Atlantic Ocean to raise money for a local charity. It's a fun way to start the new year and support a good cause.

This event is open to everyone, and participants are encouraged to dress up in creative costumes. There will be prizes for the best costumes, as well as for the most money raised.

February

Newport Winter Festival

Question: What is the Newport Winter Festival?

Answer: The Newport Winter Festival is a 10-day event that takes place in February. The festival features food, live music, and a variety of winter activities such as ice skating, ice sculpting, and snowboarding. It's a great way to enjoy the winter season and have fun with family and friends.

There are also events for kids, including a children's fair and a magic show. The festival concludes with a chili cook-off, where local restaurants compete for the title of best chili in Newport.

March

Newport St. Patrick's Day Parade

Question: What is the Newport St. Patrick's Day Parade?

Answer: The Newport St. Patrick's Day Parade is a festive event that takes place on the Saturday before St. Patrick's Day. The parade features marching bands, bagpipers, and floats decorated with green and gold. It's a fun way to celebrate Irish culture and heritage.

After the parade, many people head to local bars and restaurants to continue the celebration. Some popular spots include the Fastnet Pub, O'Brien's Pub, and the Celtica Public House.

April

Newport Daffodil Days Festival

Question: What is the Newport Daffodil Days Festival?

Answer: The Newport Daffodil Days Festival is a two-week event that celebrates the arrival of spring. The festival features over one million daffodils planted throughout the city, as well as a variety of events such as a parade, a garden party, and a dog show.

Visitors can take a self-guided tour of the daffodil displays, which are located in public parks, private gardens, and along the waterfront. The festival is a great way to welcome spring and enjoy the beauty of Newport.

May

Newport Waterfront Oyster Festival

Question: What is the Newport Waterfront Oyster Festival?

Answer: The Newport Waterfront Oyster Festival is a two-day event that celebrates the local oyster industry. The festival features fresh oysters from local farms, as well as live music, cooking demonstrations, and craft vendors.

The festival takes place at Bowen's Wharf, which is located in the heart of Newport's waterfront district. It's a great way to taste some of the freshest seafood in New England and enjoy the beautiful views of Newport Harbor.

June

Newport Flower Show

Question: What is the Newport Flower Show?

Answer: The Newport Flower Show is a three-day event that takes place at the historic Rosecliff mansion. The show features floral displays, garden exhibits, and lectures by renowned horticulturists.

The theme for the 2024 show is "Glorious Gardens," and it will celebrate the beauty and diversity of gardens around the world. Visitors can stroll through the gardens of Rosecliff and learn about different gardening techniques and trends.

July

Newport Music Festival

Question: What is the Newport Music Festival?

Answer: The Newport Music Festival is a three-week event that features classical music performances in historic venues throughout Newport. The festival attracts world-renowned musicians and music lovers from around the globe.

Some of the venues for the festival include the Newport Mansions, the Newport Casino, and St. Mary's Church. The festival also includes lectures, receptions, and other special events.

August

Newport Jazz Festival

Question: What is the Newport Jazz Festival?

Answer: The Newport Jazz Festival is a three-day event that features some of the best jazz musicians in the world. The festival takes place at Fort Adams State Park, which is located on the waterfront in Newport.

The festival has a long history, dating back to 1954. Over the years, it has featured performances by legends such as Duke Ellington, Ella Fitzgerald, and Miles Davis. Today, the festival continues to attract top jazz musicians and fans from around the world.

September

Newport International Boat Show

Question: What is the Newport International Boat Show?

Answer: The Newport International Boat Show is a four-day event that showcases the latest boats and marine products from around the world. The show takes place at the Newport Yachting Center and the adjacent Newport Harbor.

The show features hundreds of exhibitors, including boat builders, electronics manufacturers, and marine accessory companies. Visitors can also attend seminars and workshops on topics such as boat maintenance, navigation, and safety.

October

Newport Mansions Wine and Food Festival

Question: What is the Newport Mansions Wine and Food Festival?

Answer: The Newport Mansions Wine and Food Festival is a three-day event that takes place at the historic mansions of Newport. The festival features wine tastings, cooking demonstrations, and seminars by renowned chefs and wine experts.

Some of the mansions that participate in the festival include The Breakers, Marble House, and Rosecliff. The festival is a great way to experience the elegance and grandeur of Newport's mansions while enjoying some of the best food and wine in the world.

Conclusion

As you can see, Newport has a year-round calendar of events that caters to different interests and ages. Whether you're a foodie, a music lover, or a history buff, there's something for everyone in Newport. So mark your calendar for 2024 and start planning your visit to this beautiful city.
