Introduction

Lake of the Ozarks is a popular tourist destination in Missouri, known for its beautiful lake and scenic surroundings. The area is also known for hosting various events throughout the year. As we approach the year 2024, it's time to take a look at the upcoming events that will take place in the Lake of the Ozarks area. In this article, we will explore the Lake of the Ozarks event calendar for 2024.

What are the events that will take place in the Lake of the Ozarks area in 2024?

There are many events that will take place in the Lake of the Ozarks area in 2024. Some of the major events include: 1. Lake of the Ozarks Mardi Gras Pub Crawl – This event takes place on February 17, 2024, and is a popular event among locals and tourists alike. The event includes a pub crawl, live music, and other festivities. 2. Lake of the Ozarks St. Patrick's Day Parade – This event takes place on March 17, 2024, and is a popular event among families. The parade includes floats, marching bands, and other entertainment. 3. Magic Dragon Street Meet Nationals – This event takes place on May 3-5, 2024, and is a car show that features classic cars, hot rods, and other unique vehicles. 4. Lake of the Ozarks Bikefest – This event takes place on September 11-15, 2024, and is a motorcycle rally that attracts bikers from all over the country.

What other events can visitors enjoy in the Lake of the Ozarks area?

Apart from the major events mentioned above, visitors can also enjoy other events like: 1. Lake of the Ozarks Air Show – This event takes place in June 2024 and features various aircraft displays and aerial performances. 2. Lake of the Ozarks Jazz Festival – This event takes place in August 2024 and features live jazz music from various artists. 3. Lake of the Ozarks Fall Festival – This event takes place in October 2024 and features food vendors, live music, and other entertainment.

How can visitors find more information about these events?

Visitors can find more information about these events on the official Lake of the Ozarks website. The website provides details about the events, including dates, times, and locations. Visitors can also purchase tickets and make reservations for events through the website.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Lake of the Ozarks area is a great place to visit for those looking for fun and entertainment. With a variety of events taking place throughout the year, visitors will never be bored. Whether you're interested in cars, motorcycles, music, or food, there's an event for everyone in the Lake of the Ozarks area. So, mark your calendars and start planning your trip to the Lake of the Ozarks area in 2024!
