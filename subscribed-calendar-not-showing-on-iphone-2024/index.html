Subscribed Calendar Not Showing On Iphone 2024: What To Do?

Written by Mable Stanley Feb 17, 2023 · 3 min read

With the increasing use of smartphones, it has become essential to keep our schedules and tasks organized. One way to do that is by subscribing to calendars. However, some iPhone users have recently reported that their subscribed calendars are not showing on their devices. This can be frustrating, especially if you rely on your calendar to keep your life organized. In this article, we will discuss the possible reasons why subscribed calendars may not be showing on iPhone 2024 and what you can do to fix the issue.

Table of Contents

Introduction

With the increasing use of smartphones, it has become essential to keep our schedules and tasks organized. One way to do that is by subscribing to calendars. However, some iPhone users have recently reported that their subscribed calendars are not showing on their devices. This can be frustrating, especially if you rely on your calendar to keep your life organized. In this article, we will discuss the possible reasons why subscribed calendars may not be showing on iPhone 2024 and what you can do to fix the issue.

Possible Reasons for Subscribed Calendar Not Showing on iPhone

There are several reasons why your subscribed calendar may not be showing on your iPhone. Some of the common reasons include:

1. Syncing Issues

One of the possible reasons why your subscribed calendar is not showing on your iPhone is syncing issues. If your iPhone is not syncing with your calendar, you may not see the subscribed calendar on your device.

2. Incorrect Settings

Another reason why your subscribed calendar may not be showing on your iPhone is incorrect settings. If your settings are not configured correctly, you may not see the subscribed calendar on your device.

3. Subscription Issues

There may be issues with the subscription itself. If the subscription is not active or has been canceled, you may not see the subscribed calendar on your device.

What to Do When Subscribed Calendar is not Showing on iPhone

If your subscribed calendar is not showing on your iPhone, there are several things you can do to fix the issue. Some of the solutions include:

1. Check Your Syncing Settings

The first thing you should do is check your syncing settings. Make sure that your iPhone is set to sync with your calendar and that the subscribed calendar is selected for syncing.

2. Check Your Calendar Settings

Check your calendar settings to ensure that the subscribed calendar is selected to display on your iPhone. To do this, go to Settings > Calendar > Accounts and select the subscribed calendar, then turn on the toggle for "Show on Home Screen" and "Show Notifications."

3. Check Your Subscription

If you have recently subscribed to a calendar, check your subscription to ensure that it is active and not canceled. You can do this by going to Settings > Calendar > Accounts and selecting the subscribed calendar. If the subscription is not active, you may need to renew it.

4. Restart Your iPhone

If none of the above solutions work, try restarting your iPhone. Sometimes, a simple restart can fix syncing and display issues.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I subscribe to a calendar on my iPhone?

A: Yes, you can subscribe to a calendar on your iPhone. To do this, go to Settings > Calendar > Accounts > Add Account > Other > Add Subscribed Calendar. Enter the URL of the calendar and click "Next."

Q: Can I subscribe to multiple calendars on my iPhone?

A: Yes, you can subscribe to multiple calendars on your iPhone. To do this, repeat the steps above for each calendar you want to subscribe to.

Q: Why is my subscribed calendar not syncing with my iPhone?

A: Your subscribed calendar may not be syncing with your iPhone due to syncing issues or incorrect settings. Try checking your syncing and calendar settings, and if that doesn't work, try restarting your iPhone.

Conclusion

Having a subscribed calendar can be a great way to keep your life organized. However, if your subscribed calendar is not showing on your iPhone, it can be frustrating. By following the solutions discussed in this article, you can fix the issue and ensure that your subscribed calendar is always up-to-date on your iPhone.
