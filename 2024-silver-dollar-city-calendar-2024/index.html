2024 Silver Dollar City Calendar: Plan Your Trip Now

Written by Pauline Lafleur Aug 25, 2022 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you’re looking for a fun, family-friendly vacation destination for 2024, look no further than Silver Dollar City. This theme park, located in Branson, Missouri, has something for everyone, from thrilling roller coasters to live entertainment and delicious food. But before you pack your bags, make sure you check out the 2024 Silver Dollar City calendar to plan your trip around all the exciting events and activities.

What Is Silver Dollar City?

Silver Dollar City is a theme park that celebrates the culture and history of the Ozarks. It features over 40 rides and attractions, including roller coasters, water rides, and family-friendly experiences. In addition, the park hosts daily live entertainment shows, craft demonstrations, and delicious food options.

What Is the 2024 Silver Dollar City Calendar?

The 2024 Silver Dollar City calendar is a schedule of events and activities that will take place throughout the year. It includes special events like the Bluegrass and BBQ Festival, the National Harvest and Cowboy Festival, and the Old Time Christmas Festival, as well as daily shows and attractions. By checking the calendar, you can plan your trip to Silver Dollar City around your favorite events and experiences.

Events and Activities

Bluegrass and BBQ Festival

The Bluegrass and BBQ Festival is a week-long celebration of music and food. It features some of the best bluegrass bands from around the country, as well as delicious BBQ dishes from Silver Dollar City’s culinary team. In addition to the music and food, there are also craft demonstrations and other activities for the whole family.

National Harvest and Cowboy Festival

The National Harvest and Cowboy Festival is a month-long celebration of the fall season. It features over 125 visiting craftsmen, wild west shows, and a barn dance. There are also special harvest-themed foods and drinks, as well as pumpkin-themed activities for kids.

Old Time Christmas Festival

The Old Time Christmas Festival is a six-week celebration of the holiday season. It features over 6.5 million lights, a Christmas parade, and holiday shows and performances. There are also seasonal foods and drinks, as well as special events like a living nativity and a holiday light parade.

Tips for Visiting Silver Dollar City

Plan Ahead

Before you visit Silver Dollar City, make sure you check the 2024 calendar and plan your trip around the events and activities you want to experience. You should also purchase your tickets in advance to save time and money.

Stay Hydrated

Silver Dollar City can get hot and humid in the summer months, so make sure you bring plenty of water and stay hydrated throughout the day. You can also bring a refillable water bottle to save money and reduce waste.

Wear Comfortable Shoes

With over 40 rides and attractions, you’ll be doing a lot of walking at Silver Dollar City. Make sure you wear comfortable shoes that can handle the terrain and keep your feet happy all day long.

FAQs

What Are the Hours of Operation for Silver Dollar City?

The hours of operation for Silver Dollar City vary depending on the season and the day of the week. You can check the park’s website or the 2024 calendar for specific hours.

What Is the Best Time of Year to Visit Silver Dollar City?

The best time of year to visit Silver Dollar City depends on your interests and preferences. If you love bluegrass music and BBQ, the Bluegrass and BBQ Festival is a great time to visit. If you’re a fan of the fall season and cowboy culture, the National Harvest and Cowboy Festival is a must-see. And if you love Christmas lights and holiday cheer, the Old Time Christmas Festival is the perfect time to visit.

What Should I Bring to Silver Dollar City?

You should bring comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, a refillable water bottle, and a camera to capture all the memories. You may also want to bring a small bag or backpack to carry your belongings throughout the day.
