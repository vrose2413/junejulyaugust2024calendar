How To Delete A Calendar Event On Iphone 2024

Written by Juan Stafford May 02, 2023 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you are an iPhone user, you know how important the calendar app can be in helping you manage your schedule. However, there may be times when you need to delete a calendar event. In this article, we will guide you on how to delete a calendar event on your iPhone in the year 2024.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open the Calendar App

The first step is to open the calendar app on your iPhone. This can be done by tapping on the calendar icon on your home screen.

Step 2: Locate the Event

Once you have opened the calendar app, locate the event that you want to delete. You can do this by scrolling through the calendar or using the search function to find the event.

Step 3: Tap on the Event

Once you have found the event, tap on it to open it. This will take you to the event details page.

Step 4: Tap on the Edit Button

On the event details page, tap on the edit button located in the top right corner of the screen. This will allow you to make changes to the event.

Step 5: Scroll Down and Tap on Delete Event

Scroll down the page until you see the option to delete the event. Tap on delete event and confirm that you want to delete the event when prompted.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I delete multiple events at once?

A: Yes, you can delete multiple events at once by selecting the events and tapping on the delete button.

Q: Will deleting an event from my iPhone calendar also delete it from my other devices?

A: If you have your iPhone calendar synced with your other devices, deleting an event from your iPhone will also delete it from your other devices.

Tips and Tricks

- You can also delete an event by swiping left on the event in the calendar view and tapping on delete. - If you accidentally delete an event, you can restore it by going to the Recently Deleted folder in the calendar app and tapping on the event to restore it.

Conclusion

Deleting a calendar event on your iPhone is a simple process that can help you manage your schedule better. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily delete any unwanted events from your calendar.
