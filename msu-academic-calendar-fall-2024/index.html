When Does the Fall Semester Start?

The fall semester at MSU starts on August 26, 2024. This is the first day of classes, and students should make sure they are prepared to attend their first classes on this day.

There are several important dates to keep in mind for the fall semester of 2024 at MSU. These include:

  • August 26, 2024: First day of classes
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day (No classes)
  • October 14-15, 2024: Fall break (No classes)
  • November 28-29, 2024: Thanksgiving break (No classes)
  • December 13-19, 2024: Final exams

The registration deadline for the fall semester at MSU is August 1, 2024. Students should make sure they are registered for their classes before this deadline to avoid any late fees or other penalties.

The exam schedules for the fall semester of 2024 at MSU are as follows:

  • December 15-16, 2024: Tuesday/Thursday classes
  • December 17-19, 2024: Friday classes and other classes as needed

What Are the Graduation Requirements?

Students who plan to graduate in the fall semester of 2024 must meet the following requirements:
