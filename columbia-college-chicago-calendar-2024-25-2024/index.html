Introduction

Columbia College Chicago is a private arts and media college located in Chicago, Illinois. It offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields such as music, theatre, dance, film, television, and journalism. In this article, we will discuss the college's academic calendar for the year 2024-25.

When does the academic year start and end?

The academic year at Columbia College Chicago for 2024-25 starts on August 26, 2024, and ends on May 10, 2025. This period includes fall, spring, and summer semesters.

What are the important dates to remember?

Here are some important dates to remember for the academic year 2024-25: - Fall semester starts on August 26, 2024, and ends on December 17, 2024. - Spring semester starts on January 13, 2025, and ends on May 10, 2025. - Summer session starts on May 19, 2025, and ends on August 8, 2025. - Thanksgiving break is from November 27 to November 30, 2024. - Winter break is from December 18, 2024, to January 12, 2025. - Spring break is from March 14 to March 22, 2025.

What are the registration deadlines?

The registration deadlines for the academic year 2024-25 are: - Fall semester: August 19, 2024. - Spring semester: January 6, 2025. - Summer session: May 12, 2025.

Can students take classes during the summer session?

Yes, students can take classes during the summer session. The summer session offers a variety of courses for undergraduate and graduate students. It is a great way to catch up on credits or get ahead in your studies.

What is the grading system at Columbia College Chicago?

Columbia College Chicago uses a letter grading system. The grades range from A to F, with A being the highest and F being the lowest. In addition, students may also receive a grade of W (withdrawal) or I (incomplete).

How many credits do students need to graduate?

Undergraduate students need to complete a minimum of 120 credits to graduate. Graduate students need to complete a minimum of 30 credits to graduate. However, some programs may require more credits.

Are there any important events during the academic year?

Yes, there are several important events during the academic year at Columbia College Chicago. What resources are available to students during the academic year?

Columbia College Chicago offers a variety of resources to students during the academic year. In conclusion, the academic calendar for Columbia College Chicago for the year 2024-25 includes fall, spring, and summer semesters.
