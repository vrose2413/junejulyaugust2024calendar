How To Connect Outlook Calendar To Slack In 2024

Written by Ben Javu Apr 04, 2023 · 2 min read

Are you tired of constantly switching between your Outlook calendar and your Slack workspace? Well, the good news is that you can now connect the two and streamline your scheduling. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the process of connecting your Outlook calendar to Slack in 2024.

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you tired of constantly switching between your Outlook calendar and your Slack workspace? Well, the good news is that you can now connect the two and streamline your scheduling. In this tutorial, we will guide you through the process of connecting your Outlook calendar to Slack in 2024.

Step 1: Install the Outlook Calendar app for Slack

The first step is to ensure that you have the Outlook Calendar app installed in your Slack workspace. To do this, click on the Apps button in your Slack sidebar, search for "Outlook Calendar," and then click on the Install button.

Step 2: Authorize the app

Once you have installed the app, you will need to authorize it to access your Outlook calendar. Click on the "Authorize" button and follow the prompts to give the app the necessary permissions.

Step 3: Connect your calendar

After authorizing the app, you will be prompted to connect your Outlook calendar. Click on the "Connect" button and follow the prompts to sign in to your Outlook account and allow the app to access your calendar.

Step 4: Customize your settings

Once you have connected your calendar, you can customize the settings to personalize your experience. For example, you can choose which calendar events to display in Slack, set reminders for upcoming events, and even create new events directly from Slack.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need to have an Office 365 account to connect my Outlook calendar to Slack?

A: Yes, you will need to have an Office 365 account to use the Outlook Calendar app for Slack.

Q: Can I connect multiple calendars to Slack?

A: Yes, you can connect multiple Outlook calendars to Slack and customize the settings for each one.

Conclusion

Connecting your Outlook calendar to Slack can save you time and improve your productivity. By following these simple steps, you can easily integrate your scheduling and communication tools and stay on top of your busy schedule.
