Marin County Criminal Court Calendar 2024

Introduction

As the year 2023 comes to an end, it's time to start preparing for the upcoming year. For those who are involved in the Marin County criminal justice system, it's important to be aware of the court calendar for 2024. This article will provide an overview of what to expect in the upcoming year.

What is the Marin County Criminal Court Calendar?

The Marin County Criminal Court Calendar is a schedule of court sessions that are held throughout the year. It outlines the dates and times of court sessions, including arraignments, trials, and sentencing hearings. The calendar is updated regularly to reflect any changes that may occur.

When is the Marin County Criminal Court Calendar for 2024 released?

The Marin County Criminal Court Calendar for 2024 is typically released in late December of the previous year. This allows attorneys, defendants, and other parties involved in the criminal justice system to plan accordingly.

What types of cases are heard in the Marin County Criminal Court?

The Marin County Criminal Court hears a wide range of criminal cases, including misdemeanors and felonies. These cases may involve charges such as drug offenses, DUIs, theft, assault, and more. The court also handles cases involving probation violations and other criminal matters.

How can I find the Marin County Criminal Court Calendar?

The Marin County Criminal Court Calendar is available online through the Marin County Superior Court website. It can also be obtained in person at the courthouse.

What should I do if my court date conflicts with my schedule?

If your court date conflicts with your schedule, you should contact your attorney as soon as possible. They may be able to request a continuance or reschedule the hearing for a more convenient time.

What are my options if I am unable to attend my court date?

If you are unable to attend your court date, you should contact your attorney immediately. They may be able to request a continuance or represent you in your absence. However, it's important to note that missing a court date without proper notification can result in serious consequences.

Conclusion

Being aware of the Marin County Criminal Court Calendar for 2024 is essential for those involved in the criminal justice system. Whether you're an attorney, defendant, or witness, it's important to stay informed and prepared for your court date. By utilizing the resources available, you can ensure that your case is handled in an efficient and timely manner.
