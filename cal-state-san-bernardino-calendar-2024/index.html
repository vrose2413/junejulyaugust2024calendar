Cal State San Bernardino Calendar 2024: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Ben Javu Nov 02, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you're a student or faculty member at California State University, San Bernardino, you'll want to know what's on the calendar for 2024. In this article, we'll break down all the important dates and events you need to know to stay on top of your schedule.

When Does the Academic Year Start?

The academic year at Cal State San Bernardino typically starts in late August or early September, and the 2024-2025 academic year is no different. The first day of classes for the fall semester is August 26, 2024.

When Are the Holidays?

As with any academic calendar, there are several holidays throughout the year. In 2024, Labor Day falls on September 2, Thanksgiving break runs from November 27 to November 29, and winter break runs from December 20 to January 5.

What About Finals Week?

Finals week is always a stressful time for students, but it's important to know when it's happening so you can plan accordingly. In 2024, finals week for the fall semester runs from December 16 to December 20. The spring semester finals week runs from May 12 to May 16, 2025.

What Are Some Other Important Dates?

There are a few other important dates to keep in mind. The last day to add classes for the fall semester is September 6, 2024, and the last day to drop classes without a "W" is September 13. The last day to drop classes with a "W" is November 1, and the last day to withdraw from the university is November 29.

Question and Answer:

Q: What happens if I miss the deadline to drop a class without a "W"?
A: If you miss the deadline to drop a class without a "W," you'll still be enrolled in the class and will receive a grade at the end of the semester.

When Is Commencement?

For graduating seniors, commencement is the most important date on the calendar. In 2024, commencement ceremonies will be held on June 15 and June 16.

What About Summer Sessions?

If you're planning on taking classes during the summer, you'll want to know when the summer sessions start. In 2024, the first summer session starts on June 3, and the second summer session starts on July 8.

Question and Answer:

Q: How many units can I take during the summer sessions?
A: During the summer sessions, you can take up to 7 units per session.

Conclusion

Whether you're a student or faculty member at Cal State San Bernardino, it's important to stay on top of the calendar to make sure you don't miss any important dates or events. With this guide, you should have all the information you need to plan your 2024 schedule with ease.
