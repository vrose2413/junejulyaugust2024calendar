Important Dates

The Spring 2024 semester at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University begins on January 8th, 2024 and ends on May 3rd, 2024. There are several important dates to keep in mind throughout the semester:

  • January 7th, 2024 - Last day to drop/add courses without a "W" grade
  • January 14th, 2024 - Last day to drop courses and receive a partial refund
  • February 28th - March 8th, 2024 - Spring Break
  • April 15th, 2024 - Last day to withdraw from courses
  • May 3rd, 2024 - Last day of classes

Registration Deadlines

If you miss a registration deadline, don't panic. Late registration is available until January 21st, 2024, but it comes with a $100 late fee. If you miss the late registration deadline, you may still be able to add courses with the approval of your academic advisor.

If you need to withdraw from a course, you must do so by April 15th, 2024. Withdrawing from a course will result in a "W" grade on your transcript, but it will not affect your GPA. However, it's important to keep in mind that withdrawing from a course may affect your progress towards graduation.
