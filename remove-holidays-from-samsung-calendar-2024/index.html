Remove Holidays from Samsung Calendar 2024

Introduction

If you are tired of seeing holidays in your Samsung Calendar 2024, don't worry, you can easily remove them. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to remove holidays from your Samsung Calendar 2024.

Why Remove Holidays from Samsung Calendar 2024?

While holidays are important to keep track of, they may not be relevant for everyone. By removing holidays from your Samsung Calendar 2024, you can declutter your calendar and focus on events that matter to you.

How to Remove Holidays from Samsung Calendar 2024?

Follow these simple steps to remove holidays from your Samsung Calendar 2024:

  1. Open the Samsung Calendar app on your device.
  2. Tap on the three dots in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Scroll down and select "Holidays".
  5. Toggle the switch next to each holiday to turn them off.
  6. Tap "Done" when finished.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still add holidays to my Samsung Calendar 2024 after removing them?

A: Yes, you can still add holidays to your Samsung Calendar 2024 after removing them. Simply follow the same steps as above and toggle the switch back on for the holidays you want to add.

Q: Will removing holidays from my Samsung Calendar 2024 affect my device's performance?

A: No, removing holidays from your Samsung Calendar 2024 will not affect your device's performance. It will only remove them from your calendar view.

Conclusion

By following the simple steps outlined in this article, you can easily remove holidays from your Samsung Calendar 2024. This will help you declutter your calendar and keep track of events that matter to you.
