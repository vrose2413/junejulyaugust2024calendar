Freedom Cash Back Calendar 2024: The Ultimate Guide

Written by Pauline Lafleur May 30, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you tired of missing out on cash back rewards? Look no further than the Freedom Cash Back Calendar for 2024. This calendar provides you with a schedule of rotating bonus categories that earn you 5% cash back on up to $1,500 in purchases each quarter. In this article, we’ll dive into the details of the 2024 calendar and how you can maximize your rewards.

What is the Freedom Cash Back Calendar?

The Freedom Cash Back Calendar is a schedule of rotating bonus categories that earn you 5% cash back on up to $1,500 in purchases each quarter. These categories change every three months, so you have the opportunity to earn cash back on a variety of purchases throughout the year. The calendar is available to all Chase Freedom cardholders.

How does it work?

Each quarter, the Freedom Cash Back Calendar features different bonus categories that earn you 5% cash back on up to $1,500 in purchases. To activate these bonus categories, simply log into your account and activate the offer. Once activated, you’ll earn 5% cash back on eligible purchases made with your Chase Freedom card.

What are the bonus categories for 2024?

The bonus categories for 2024 are as follows: - Q1 (January - March): Gas stations, internet, cable, and phone services - Q2 (April - June): Grocery stores, home improvement stores, and select streaming services - Q3 (July - September): Restaurants and select streaming services - Q4 (October - December): Department stores, PayPal, and Chase Pay

Maximizing Your Rewards

To maximize your rewards with the Freedom Cash Back Calendar, it’s important to plan ahead and make purchases within the bonus categories each quarter. Here are some tips for maximizing your rewards: - Take advantage of the 5% cash back by making purchases within the bonus categories each quarter. - If you have other credit cards, compare the rewards offered and use the card that will give you the highest cash back for each purchase. - If you have a large purchase coming up, consider timing it so that it falls within a bonus category. - Pay your balance in full each month to avoid interest charges.

Question and Answer

Q: Is there a limit to the amount of cash back I can earn?

A: Yes, there is a limit of $75 in cash back rewards per quarter (5% on $1,500 in purchases).

Q: Can I use my Chase Freedom card outside of the bonus categories?

A: Yes, you can still use your Chase Freedom card for purchases outside of the bonus categories. However, these purchases will only earn you 1% cash back.

Q: Do I need to activate the bonus categories each quarter?

A: Yes, you need to activate the bonus categories each quarter to earn the 5% cash back. You can activate the offer online or through the Chase mobile app.

Conclusion

The Freedom Cash Back Calendar for 2024 is a great way to earn cash back on purchases you’re already making. By planning ahead and making purchases within the bonus categories each quarter, you can maximize your rewards and save money. So, don’t miss out on this opportunity to earn cash back – activate your bonus categories today!
