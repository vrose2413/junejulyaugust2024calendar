Introduction

The University of Kansas (KU) academic calendar for fall 2024 is out, and students can have a glimpse of what to expect. KU is a public research university located in Lawrence, Kansas, United States, and it offers various degree programs, including undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs.

When Does the Fall 2024 Semester Start?

The fall 2024 semester at the University of Kansas will start on Monday, August 26, 2024. This is the first day of classes for the semester, and students are expected to attend.

What are the Key Dates for the Fall 2024 Semester?

The fall 2024 semester at the University of Kansas will run from August 26, 2024, to December 13, 2024. There will be various key dates during the semester, including:
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day (no classes)
  • October 11-12, 2024: Fall break (no classes)
  • November 27-29, 2024: Thanksgiving break (no classes)
  • December 9, 2024: Last day of classes

What are the Registration Deadlines?

Registration deadlines for the fall 2024 semester at the University of Kansas are as follows:
  • April 1, 2024: Graduate students
  • April 15, 2024: Undergraduate students

What are the Tuition and Fees for the Fall 2024 Semester?

Tuition and fees for the fall 2024 semester at the University of Kansas will be announced later. However, students can expect the tuition and fees to be similar to the fall 2023 semester.

Academic Requirements

What is the Minimum GPA Requirement?

The minimum GPA requirement to maintain good academic standing at the University of Kansas is 2.0. However, certain programs may have higher GPA requirements.

How Many Credits do I Need to Graduate?

Students need to earn a minimum of 120 credits to graduate from the University of Kansas. However, certain programs may require more credits.

Question and Answer

Q: What if I Miss the Registration Deadline?

A: If you miss the registration deadline, you may be able to add classes during the first week of the semester. However, it is best to register before the deadline to avoid any problems.

Q: What if I Need to Drop a Class?

A: If you need to drop a class, you can do so before the drop deadline, which is usually around mid-semester. However, you should consult with your advisor before dropping a class to ensure that it will not affect your academic progress.

Q: What if I Need to Withdraw from the Semester?

A: If you need to withdraw from the semester, you can do so before the withdrawal deadline, which is usually around the eighth week of the semester. However, withdrawing from the semester may affect your financial aid and academic progress, so you should consult with your advisor before making a decision.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the University of Kansas academic calendar for fall 2024 provides students with important dates and deadlines that they need to keep in mind. By following the academic requirements and attending classes regularly, students can have a successful semester and make progress towards their degree.
