How To Make Google Calendar Pretty 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Jan 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of looking at the bland and dull Google Calendar? Do you want to give it a fresh and vibrant look? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to make Google Calendar pretty in 2024.

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you tired of looking at the bland and dull Google Calendar? Do you want to give it a fresh and vibrant look? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to make Google Calendar pretty in 2024.

Step 1: Choose a Theme

The first step to making your calendar look pretty is by selecting a theme. To do this, go to the settings option, click on the General tab, and select the theme you like. There are several options available, so choose the one that suits your style and preferences.

Question: Can I create my own theme?

Yes, you can create your own theme! To do this, go to the settings option, click on the General tab, and select the "Create New Theme" option. From here, you can customize your calendar by selecting different colors, images, and fonts.

Step 2: Add Events with Color Codes

Now that you have selected a theme, it's time to add events to your calendar. You can make your calendar look organized and pretty by assigning different colors to each event. For instance, you can assign pink to personal events, green to work events, and blue to birthdays.

Question: How do I assign colors to events?

To assign colors to events, click on the event you want to edit, click on the "Edit Event" option, and select the color you want to assign to that event.

Step 3: Use Images

You can use images to make your calendar look visually appealing. To add an image, click on the event you want to edit, click on the "Edit Event" option, and select the "Add Image" option. You can choose an image from your computer or use the URL of the image.

Question: Can I add images to my theme?

Yes, you can add images to your theme. To do this, go to the settings option, click on the General tab, and select the "Customize" option. From here, you can upload images and use them as a background or header image.

Step 4: Use Different Fonts

You can also make your calendar look pretty by using different fonts. You can choose from a variety of fonts available in Google Calendar or upload your own custom font.

Question: How do I upload my own custom font?

To upload your own custom font, go to the settings option, click on the General tab, and select the "Customize" option. From here, you can upload your own font and use it in your theme.

Step 5: Add Holidays and Birthdays

Adding holidays and birthdays can make your calendar look festive and pretty. You can add holidays by going to the settings option, clicking on the "Add Calendar" option, and selecting "Holidays". Similarly, you can add birthdays by clicking on the day, selecting "Create Event", and choosing the "Birthday" option.

Question: Can I customize the holidays and birthdays?

Yes, you can customize the holidays and birthdays. To do this, go to the settings option, click on the "Add Calendar" option, and select "Browse Interesting Calendars". From here, you can select the holiday or birthday calendar you want and customize it according to your preferences.

Conclusion

There you have it! Follow these simple steps, and you will have a pretty Google Calendar in no time. Remember to choose a theme, add events with color codes, use images and different fonts, and add holidays and birthdays. Happy scheduling!
