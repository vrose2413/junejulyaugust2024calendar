Oklahoma State Fair 2024 Calendar: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Ben Javu Jun 17, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

The Oklahoma State Fair is an annual event that takes place in Oklahoma City. It is the largest event in the state, attracting more than a million visitors every year. In this article, we will provide you with all the information you need to know about the Oklahoma State Fair 2024 calendar.

What is Oklahoma State Fair?

The Oklahoma State Fair is a 11-day event that celebrates the best of Oklahoma. It features a variety of attractions, including live music, food, carnival rides, animal shows, and more. The fair has been a tradition in Oklahoma since 1907 and has grown to become one of the largest state fairs in the country.

When is the Oklahoma State Fair 2024?

The Oklahoma State Fair 2024 will take place from Thursday, September 12 to Sunday, September 22, 2024.

Where is the Oklahoma State Fair held?

The Oklahoma State Fair is held at the State Fair Park in Oklahoma City. The address is 3001 General Pershing Boulevard, Oklahoma City, OK 73107.

What are the hours of the Oklahoma State Fair?

The fairgrounds open at 8:00 am and close at midnight during the fair. However, some areas of the fair may open earlier or close later.

Attractions

What are the main attractions at the Oklahoma State Fair?

The Oklahoma State Fair features a variety of attractions, including: - Live music: The fair hosts concerts by famous musicians every night. - Carnival rides: There are over 60 rides to choose from, including roller coasters, Ferris wheels, and more. - Food: The fair offers a wide variety of food options, from traditional fair food like corn dogs and funnel cakes to more exotic options. - Animal shows: The fair has a variety of animal shows, including horse shows, livestock shows, and a petting zoo. - Exhibits: The fair has a variety of exhibits showcasing the best of Oklahoma, including art, crafts, and photography.

What are some new attractions for 2024?

The Oklahoma State Fair is always adding new attractions, so be sure to check the website for the latest updates. Some new attractions for 2024 include: - A new roller coaster: The fair is adding a new roller coaster to its lineup of carnival rides. - Food trucks: The fair is expanding its food options with the addition of food trucks.

Tickets and Admission

How much are tickets to the Oklahoma State Fair?

Ticket prices vary depending on the day of the week and the time of day. Prices range from $10 to $15 for adults and $5 to $10 for children.

Where can I buy tickets to the Oklahoma State Fair?

Tickets can be purchased online at the fair's website or at the gate.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes, there are several discounts available, including: - Military discount: Active duty military personnel and veterans can get free admission on certain days. - Senior discount: Seniors over the age of 60 can get a discount on admission. - Group discount: Groups of 20 or more can get a discount on admission.

Conclusion

The Oklahoma State Fair is a must-visit event for anyone in Oklahoma. With a variety of attractions and events, there is something for everyone. Make sure to mark your calendar for the Oklahoma State Fair 2024 and don't miss out on all the fun!
