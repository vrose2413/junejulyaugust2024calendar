When does the academic year begin?

The academic year 2024-25 at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities will begin on August 26, 2024. This date marks the start of the fall semester, which will run until December 20, 2024.

What are the important dates to remember?

There are several important dates to remember in the academic calendar. Here are some of the key dates:

  • August 26, 2024: First day of fall semester
  • September 2, 2024: Labor Day (no classes)
  • November 28-29, 2024: Thanksgiving Break
  • December 16-20, 2024: Final exams

What are the holidays observed by the University?

The University of Minnesota Twin Cities observes several holidays throughout the academic year. These holidays include Labor Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, and Memorial Day. It is important for students and staff to note these holidays and plan accordingly.

When is spring break?

Spring break at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities is scheduled for March 9-13, 2025. This break allows students to take a breather from their studies and recharge for the remainder of the semester.

When is the last day of the academic year?

The last day of the academic year 2024-25 at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities is May 16, 2025. This marks the end of the spring semester and the completion of the academic year.

What are the enrollment deadlines?

Enrollment deadlines are crucial for students to keep in mind. The deadline for fall semester enrollment is August 1, 2024. The deadline for spring semester enrollment is January 1, 2025.

What are the graduation dates?

The University of Minnesota Twin Cities has two graduation dates: May and December. The graduation date for May 2025 is May 17, 2025. The graduation date for December 2024 is December 20, 2024.
