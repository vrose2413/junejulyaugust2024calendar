What is Hudsonville Christian School?

Hudsonville Christian School is a private, Christian school located in Hudsonville, Michigan. The school serves students from preschool through eighth grade and is committed to providing an excellent education while nurturing the spiritual growth of each student.

When Does the 2024-2025 Academic Year Begin?

The 2024-2025 academic year at Hudsonville Christian School begins on Tuesday, September 3, 2024. This first day of school will be a full day of classes, and students are expected to arrive at school promptly at 8:00 am.

The 2024-2025 school year at Hudsonville Christian School will run for a total of 180 days, just like previous academic years.

The calendar includes several major holidays and breaks, including Thanksgiving Break (November 27-29), Christmas Break (December 20-January 5), and Spring Break (March 28-April 5).
