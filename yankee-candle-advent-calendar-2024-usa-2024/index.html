What is an Advent Calendar?

An advent calendar is a special calendar used to count down the days until Christmas. It typically starts on December 1st and ends on December 24th. Each day, a new door is opened, revealing a surprise treat or gift. Advent calendars have been around for over a century and have become a beloved holiday tradition in many households.
