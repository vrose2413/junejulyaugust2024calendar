How To Delete Events From Google Calendar On Iphone 2024?

Written by Pauline Lafleur May 14, 2022 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Google Calendar is a convenient tool to schedule and manage your events. However, there might be times when you want to delete an event from your calendar. In this article, we will guide you on how to delete events from Google Calendar on iPhone 2024.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open Google Calendar App

To start, open the Google Calendar app on your iPhone 2024. You can easily find the app on your home screen or in the app drawer.

Step 2: Locate the Event

Once you have opened the app, look for the event that you want to delete. You can either scroll through your calendar or use the search bar to find the event.

Step 3: Tap on the Event

After you have located the event, tap on it. This will open the event details page.

Step 4: Tap on the "Delete" Button

On the event details page, you will see a "Delete" button at the bottom of the screen. Tap on it to delete the event.

Step 5: Confirm Deletion

A pop-up message will appear asking you to confirm the deletion. Tap on "Delete" to confirm.

FAQs

1. Can I recover a deleted event from Google Calendar?

No, once you have deleted an event from Google Calendar, it cannot be recovered.

2. Can I delete multiple events at once?

Yes, you can delete multiple events at once by selecting them and tapping on the "Delete" button.

3. Will deleting an event on Google Calendar also delete it from my other devices?

Yes, if you have synced your Google Calendar across multiple devices, deleting an event on one device will also delete it from your other devices.

Conclusion

Deleting events from Google Calendar on iPhone 2024 is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Whether you want to clear your calendar or remove an event that is no longer relevant, following this guide will help you achieve your goal. Remember to confirm your deletion before proceeding, as deleted events cannot be recovered.
