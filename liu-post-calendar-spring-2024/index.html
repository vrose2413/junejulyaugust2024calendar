Introduction

As we gear up for the new academic year, it's important to stay informed about the key dates and deadlines for Liu Post's spring 2024 semester. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or staff member, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to stay on top of your schedule and make the most of your time at Liu Post.

Important Dates

The spring 2024 semester at Liu Post will begin on January 22, 2024, and end on May 17, 2024. There will be no classes on January 21, 2024, in observance of Martin Luther King Jr. Day. Spring break will take place from March 11 to March 17, 2024.

Registration Deadlines

Registration for the spring 2024 semester begins on November 1, 2023, and ends on January 21, 2024. Students are encouraged to register as early as possible to secure their preferred courses and class times.

Tuition and Fees

Tuition for the spring 2024 semester is $19,980 for full-time undergraduate students and $23,520 for full-time graduate students. Part-time students will be charged on a per-credit basis. In addition to tuition, students will be responsible for fees such as the student activity fee, technology fee, and health fee.

Academic Calendar

The academic calendar for the spring 2024 semester includes important dates such as the last day to add or drop classes, the last day to withdraw from classes with a W grade, and the final exam schedule. These dates can vary depending on the specific course and program, so it's important to stay in touch with your professors and academic advisors.

Q: When is the last day to add or drop classes?

A: The last day to add or drop classes for the spring 2024 semester is February 3, 2024.

Q: When is the final exam schedule released?

A: The final exam schedule for the spring 2024 semester will be released on April 1, 2024.

Campus Events

In addition to classes and coursework, Liu Post offers a variety of campus events and activities for students, faculty, and staff. From guest lectures and workshops to cultural events and recreational activities, there's always something happening on campus.

Q: What are some upcoming campus events for the spring 2024 semester?

A: Some upcoming campus events for the spring 2024 semester include the Spring Fling festival, the International Food Festival, and the Spring Concert featuring a special guest performer.

Campus Resources

Liu Post provides a range of resources to support the academic and personal success of its students. From the Writing Center and Tutoring Services to the Career Services Center and Counseling Center, there are many resources available to help students achieve their goals.

Q: How can I make an appointment with the Counseling Center?

A: To make an appointment with the Counseling Center, students can call 516-299-3057 or stop by the office in the Winnick Student Life Center.

Conclusion

By staying informed and engaged with the academic calendar, campus events, and resources at Liu Post, students, faculty, and staff can make the most of their time at the university. Whether you're a first-year student or a seasoned faculty member, there's always something new to learn and experience at Liu Post.
