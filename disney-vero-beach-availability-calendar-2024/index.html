What is the Disney Vero Beach Resort?

The Disney Vero Beach Resort is a beachfront hotel located in Vero Beach, Florida. It's a popular vacation spot for families who want to enjoy the sun, sand, and surf. The resort is part of the Disney Vacation Club, which means it's owned by Disney and offers exclusive perks to members.

What is the Availability Calendar?

The availability calendar is a tool that helps you plan your trip to the Disney Vero Beach Resort. It shows you when rooms are available, and when special events are happening at the resort. This way, you can plan your trip around the events you want to attend.

How Can I Use the Availability Calendar?

The availability calendar is easy to use. Simply visit the Disney Vero Beach Resort website and click on the "Availability Calendar" tab. Question and Answer

Q: Can I cancel my reservation if something comes up?

A: Yes. You can cancel your reservation up to 30 days before your stay without penalty. If you cancel within 30 days of your stay, you will be charged a fee.

Q: Is the Disney Vero Beach Resort only for Disney Vacation Club members?

A: No. While Disney Vacation Club members do get exclusive perks, anyone can book a stay at the Disney Vero Beach Resort.

Q: Can I bring my pet to the resort?

A: No. The Disney Vero Beach Resort does not allow pets, except for service animals.
