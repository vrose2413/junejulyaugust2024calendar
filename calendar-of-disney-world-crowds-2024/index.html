What is the Calendar of Disney World Crowds?

The Calendar of Disney World Crowds is a tool that helps visitors plan their trip to Disney World. The calendar shows which days are expected to be busy and which days are expected to be less crowded. This helps visitors plan their itinerary and avoid long waiting times.

How is the Calendar Prepared?

The calendar is prepared based on historical data and trends. The park authorities keep track of the number of visitors each day and use this information to predict the crowds for the upcoming year. They also take into account holidays, special events, and school breaks to prepare the calendar.
