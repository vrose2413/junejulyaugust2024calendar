Table of Contents

Introduction

Google Calendar and iCalendar are two popular calendar applications used by many people worldwide. Google Calendar is a web-based calendar service provided by Google, while iCalendar is a calendar application developed by Apple. In this article, we will show you how to add Google Calendar to iCalendar in 2024.

Step 1: Get the iCalendar URL from Google Calendar

The first step is to get the iCalendar URL from your Google Calendar. You can do this by following these simple steps:

  1. Open Google Calendar on your computer or mobile device.
  2. Click on the three dots next to the calendar you want to add to iCalendar.
  3. Select "Settings and sharing" from the drop-down menu.
  4. Scroll down to the "Integrate calendar" section and copy the iCalendar URL.

Step 2: Add the iCalendar URL to iCalendar

Once you have the iCalendar URL from Google Calendar, you can add it to iCalendar by following these steps:

  1. Open iCalendar on your Mac computer.
  2. Click on "File" in the menu bar and select "New Calendar Subscription".
  3. Paste the iCalendar URL you copied from Google Calendar in the "Calendar URL" field.
  4. Click on "Subscribe" and enter a name and color for the calendar.
  5. Click on "OK" to add the Google Calendar to iCalendar.

Step 3: Sync the Calendar

After adding the Google Calendar to iCalendar, you need to sync the calendar to ensure that any changes made in one calendar are reflected in the other. Here's how to do it:

  1. Click on the "Calendar" menu in iCalendar and select "Preferences".
  2. Click on the "Accounts" tab and select the Google Calendar you just added.
  3. Check the box next to "Refresh Calendars" and select how often you want iCalendar to sync with Google Calendar.
  4. Click on "Save" to sync the calendars.

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I add multiple Google Calendars to iCalendar?

A: Yes, you can add multiple Google Calendars to iCalendar by following the same steps for each calendar.

Q: Can I edit events in Google Calendar from iCalendar?

A: No, you cannot edit events in Google Calendar from iCalendar. You can only view the events and make changes in iCalendar.

Q: Can I add iCalendar to my Google Calendar?

A: Yes, you can add iCalendar to your Google Calendar by following the steps in reverse. Instead of getting the iCalendar URL from Google Calendar, you need to get the Google Calendar URL from iCalendar and add it to Google Calendar.

Conclusion

Adding Google Calendar to iCalendar is a simple process that can help you stay organized and keep track of all your events in one place. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily add any Google Calendar to iCalendar in 2024.
