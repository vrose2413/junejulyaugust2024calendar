Introduction

Phoenix, Arizona, is a vibrant city that is home to a variety of events throughout the year. Whether you're a local or a visitor, there's always something to do in Phoenix. If you're looking for a calendar of events for 2024, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be exploring some of the top events happening in Phoenix throughout the year.

What are some of the top events happening in Phoenix in 2024?

There are many events happening in Phoenix in 2024, but some of the top events include:

  • The Waste Management Phoenix Open
  • The Arizona Renaissance Festival
  • The Scottsdale Arabian Horse Show
  • The Phoenix Pride Festival
  • The Arizona State Fair

What is the Waste Management Phoenix Open?

The Waste Management Phoenix Open is a PGA Tour golf tournament that takes place annually at the TPC Scottsdale golf course. It's known for its rowdy crowds and the famous 16th hole, which is surrounded by grandstands and can hold up to 20,000 spectators. The tournament is one of the most attended events on the PGA Tour and takes place in late January or early February.

What is the Arizona Renaissance Festival?

The Arizona Renaissance Festival is a medieval-themed festival that takes place in February and March. The festival includes jousting, live entertainment, and a marketplace filled with artisans selling handmade goods. It's a fun event for the whole family and a great way to experience the culture of the Renaissance era.

What is the Scottsdale Arabian Horse Show?

The Scottsdale Arabian Horse Show is one of the largest Arabian horse shows in the world. It takes place annually in February and features over 2,400 horses from around the world. The event includes competitions, demonstrations, and a marketplace for horse-related goods. It's a must-see event for horse lovers.

What is the Phoenix Pride Festival?

The Phoenix Pride Festival is an LGBTQ+ event that takes place annually in April. The festival includes live entertainment, food and drink vendors, and a marketplace. It's a celebration of diversity and a great way to show support for the LGBTQ+ community.

What is the Arizona State Fair?

The Arizona State Fair is an annual event that takes place in October. The fair includes carnival rides, live entertainment, and a variety of food vendors. It's a fun event for the whole family and a great way to experience the culture of Arizona.

Conclusion

Phoenix, Arizona, is a city that is always buzzing with events. From the Waste Management Phoenix Open to the Arizona State Fair, there's always something to do in Phoenix. If you're looking for a calendar of events for 2024, be sure to check out these top events and experience all that Phoenix has to offer.
