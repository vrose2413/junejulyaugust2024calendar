Introduction

Hartford Public Schools have released their academic calendar for the year 2024-2025. This calendar includes important dates such as the first day of school, holidays, early release days, and the last day of school. It is important for parents, students, and teachers to be aware of these dates in order to plan their schedules accordingly.

What are the key dates?

The first day of school for the 2024-2025 academic year will be August 26, 2024. The last day of school will be June 12, 2025. There will be several holidays throughout the year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, and Memorial Day. There will also be early release days throughout the year.

What are the benefits of knowing the calendar?

Knowing the academic calendar in advance can help parents and students plan their vacations and other activities. Teachers can also plan their lessons and assignments around the holidays and other important dates. Additionally, being aware of early release days can help parents make arrangements for their children's care on those days.

Calendar Details

The academic calendar for the 2024-2025 year includes 180 instructional days. There will be a total of 10 early release days throughout the year. The calendar also includes a winter break from December 23, 2024, to January 1, 2025, and a spring break from April 14, 2025, to April 18, 2025.

What are the early release days?

Early release days are days when students are released from school early. This can be for various reasons such as teacher professional development or parent-teacher conferences. The early release days for the 2024-2025 academic year are September 13, 2019, October 11, 2019, November 8, 2019, December 6, 2019, January 24, 2020, February 14, 2020, March 13, 2020, April 10, 2020, May 8, 2020, and June 12, 2020.

Conclusion

Knowing the academic calendar for the 2024-2025 year is important for parents, students, and teachers. It allows for better planning of vacations, activities, and lessons. The early release days provide an opportunity for teacher professional development and parent-teacher conferences. Overall, being aware of the calendar can help make the academic year run smoother for everyone involved.

Where can I find the full calendar?

The full calendar can be found on the Hartford Public Schools website or by contacting your child's school.
