What is an Advent Calendar?

For those who are not familiar with advent calendars, they are a special calendar used to count down the days until Christmas. Advent calendars typically have 24 pockets or doors, one for each day leading up to Christmas Eve. Each day, a door is opened, revealing a small gift or treat inside.

Why do people use advent calendars?

Advent calendars are a fun and festive way to count down to Christmas. They help build excitement and anticipation for the holiday season, and they also serve as a reminder of the true meaning of Christmas.
