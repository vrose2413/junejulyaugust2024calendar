Calendly Limit Number Of Participants 2024: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Mable Stanley Nov 30, 2022 · 2 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Calendly, the popular meeting scheduling app, has announced that starting in 2024, it will be limiting the number of participants in meetings. This has caused quite a stir among users who rely on the app for scheduling and managing their meetings. In this article, we will explore what this change means for Calendly users and answer some of the most common questions.

What is Calendly?

Calendly is an online scheduling tool that allows users to schedule meetings and appointments without the hassle of back-and-forth emails. It syncs with users' calendars and provides a link that they can share with others to book a meeting.

What is the New Limit on Participants?

Starting in 2024, Calendly will limit the number of participants in meetings to 10. This means that users will no longer be able to schedule meetings with more than 10 participants using the app.

Why is Calendly Limiting the Number of Participants?

Calendly has stated that the reason for this change is to improve the user experience for all users. By limiting the number of participants, Calendly hopes to make the app more efficient and reliable for everyone.

What Does This Mean for Calendly Users?

Will Users Have to Pay More?

No, users will not have to pay more for this change. The new limit on participants will apply to all Calendly users, regardless of their subscription plan.

Will Existing Meetings be Affected?

No, existing meetings with more than 10 participants will not be affected. This change will only apply to new meetings scheduled after the new limit goes into effect.

What if Users Need to Schedule Meetings with More than 10 Participants?

Users who need to schedule meetings with more than 10 participants will need to find an alternative scheduling tool. There are many other scheduling apps available that allow for larger meetings.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Calendly's new limit on the number of participants in meetings is a significant change that will affect many users. However, by limiting the number of participants, Calendly hopes to improve the app's user experience for everyone. If you need to schedule meetings with more than 10 participants, you will need to find an alternative scheduling tool. Overall, this change is a reminder that technology is always evolving, and users must be prepared to adapt.
