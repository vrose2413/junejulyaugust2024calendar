What is a Calendar?

A calendar is a system of organizing days for social, religious, commercial or administrative purposes. It is a way of keeping track of time and events. Calendars can be divided into different categories such as lunar, solar, and lunisolar. The Gregorian calendar, which is widely used today, is a solar calendar that consists of 365 days in a year.

Why Use a Calendar?

A calendar is an essential tool for keeping track of important dates such as birthdays, anniversaries, and appointments. It also helps in planning vacations, scheduling meetings, and managing time efficiently. With a calendar, you can easily plan your activities and avoid conflicts.
