What is a Food Bank?

A food bank is a non-profit organization that collects and distributes food to those in need. These organizations work to eliminate hunger by providing food to individuals and families who may not have the resources to purchase it themselves.

Why is the Food Bank Calendar Important?

The food bank calendar is important because it provides individuals with the dates and times of food distributions. This information allows people to plan and prepare for when they can receive food assistance. It also helps food banks to efficiently distribute their resources and serve as many people as possible.
