Important Dates

The academic calendar for Thomas More University in 2024 will begin on August 26th and end on May 10th. There will be a total of four semesters throughout the year, with each semester lasting for 16 weeks. The fall semester will begin on August 26th and end on December 13th, while the spring semester will begin on January 13th and end on May 10th.

When is Spring Break?

Spring break will take place from March 9th to March 15th. During this time, there will be no classes held and the university will be closed. This is a great opportunity for students to take a break from their studies and recharge their batteries.

When are Finals?

Final exams will be held from May 6th to May 10th. This is an important time for students to focus and study for their exams. It is important to note that there will be no classes held during finals week.

Registration

Registration for the fall semester will begin on April 1st and end on August 25th. Registration for the spring semester will begin on November 1st and end on January 12th. It is important for students to register for their classes as early as possible to ensure that they get the classes they need.

What if I Miss the Registration Deadline?

If you miss the registration deadline, you may still be able to register for classes. However, you will need to speak to your academic advisor to see if there are any available classes and if you will be able to get into them.

Holidays

Thomas More University will be closed on certain holidays throughout the year. These holidays include Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, and Memorial Day.

What if I Have Classes on a Holiday?

If you have classes scheduled on a holiday, your professor may choose to cancel the class or reschedule it for a different day. It is important to check with your professor to see what their policy is on classes that fall on holidays.
