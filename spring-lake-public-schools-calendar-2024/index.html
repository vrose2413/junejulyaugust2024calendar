Spring Lake Public Schools Calendar 2024: Everything You Need To Know

Are you a parent or student in the Spring Lake Public Schools district? As the year 2023 comes to a close, it's time to start planning for the upcoming academic year. In this article, we will discuss the Spring Lake Public Schools calendar for 2024 and provide answers to some common questions.

When Does the 2024 School Year Begin and End?

The Spring Lake Public Schools calendar for 2024 will start on Tuesday, September 3, 2024, and end on Friday, June 14, 2025. This means that students will have a total of 182 school days, including holidays and breaks.

What Are the Major Holidays and Breaks?

The Spring Lake Public Schools district observes several major holidays and breaks throughout the academic year. These include: - Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024 - Winter Break: December 23, 2024 - January 3, 2025 - Mid-Winter Break: February 17-21, 2025 - Spring Break: April 4-8, 2025

Will There Be Early Release Days?

Yes, the Spring Lake Public Schools district schedules early release days throughout the academic year. These are typically scheduled on Wednesdays and give students the opportunity to participate in after-school activities or receive additional support from teachers.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation dates for the Spring Lake Public Schools district are determined by each individual school. In general, high school graduation ceremonies are held in mid-June, while middle school promotion ceremonies are held in late May or early June.

How Can I Stay Up-to-Date with the School Calendar?

The Spring Lake Public Schools district maintains an online calendar that is regularly updated with important dates and events. You can access the calendar on the district's website or through the school's mobile app.

What Should I Do If I Have Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions or concerns about the Spring Lake Public Schools calendar for 2024, you should contact your child's school or the district office. They will be able to provide you with additional information and support.

Conclusion

Planning for the academic year ahead can be stressful, but with the information provided in this article, you can start preparing for the Spring Lake Public Schools calendar for 2024. Remember to mark important dates on your own calendar and stay in communication with your child's school to ensure a successful academic year.
