Introduction

Duke University is known for its academic excellence and outstanding research programs. As a student or faculty member, it is important to stay updated about the academic calendar. The academic calendar for the year 2024-25 has been released by Duke University, and it is important to know the important dates to plan your academic year efficiently.

What is the Duke Academic Calendar 24-25 2024?

The Duke Academic Calendar 24-25 2024 is a schedule of important dates and deadlines for the academic year. It includes the start and end dates of the academic year, breaks, holidays, and exam schedules. It is a useful tool for students, faculty, and staff to plan their academic year effectively.

When does the Academic Year start and end?

The academic year for Duke University starts on August 27, 2024, and ends on May 10, 2025. The Fall semester starts on August 27, 2024, and ends on December 13, 2024. The Spring semester starts on January 8, 2025, and ends on May 10, 2025.

What are the breaks and holidays?

The academic calendar includes several breaks and holidays. The Fall Break is from October 14, 2024, to October 15, 2024. Thanksgiving Break is from November 27, 2024, to November 29, 2024. Winter Break is from December 14, 2024, to January 7, 2025. Spring Break is from March 10, 2025, to March 14, 2025. Good Friday is on April 18, 2025.

What is the Exam Schedule?

The exam schedule for the academic year 2024-25 is yet to be released by Duke University. However, in general, the Fall semester exams are conducted from December 16, 2024, to December 21, 2024. The Spring semester exams are conducted from May 2, 2025, to May 10, 2025.

FAQs

How can I access the Duke Academic Calendar 24-25 2024?

The Duke Academic Calendar 24-25 2024 is available on the official website of Duke University. You can access it by visiting the academic calendar section of the website.

Will the dates in the academic calendar change?

The dates in the academic calendar are subject to change. In case of any changes, Duke University will issue a revised academic calendar.

If you miss an important deadline, you should reach out to your professor or academic advisor. They will guide you on the next steps to take.
