Introduction

Keeping your neighborhood and city clean is a vital part of maintaining a healthy and safe environment. One of the ways to ensure cleanliness in El Paso is by following the trash pickup schedule. For the year 2024, the city of El Paso has released a trash pickup calendar that every resident must be aware of.

What is the El Paso Trash Pickup Calendar?

The El Paso Trash Pickup Calendar is a schedule that outlines the days and times when the city's trash collection service will pick up your garbage, recycling, and bulky items. This calendar is an essential tool for every resident in El Paso, as it helps to keep the city clean and organized.

When Will the 2024 El Paso Trash Pickup Calendar be Released?

The 2024 El Paso Trash Pickup Calendar will be released in December 2023. The city usually releases the calendar a few weeks before the beginning of the new year to give residents enough time to prepare for the new schedule.

Important Dates and Information

What Days Will the Trash be Picked Up?

The trash pickup schedule in El Paso varies depending on the area you live in. However, the city generally follows a Monday through Friday schedule for garbage and recycling collection. Bulky item collection occurs on a designated day of the week, depending on your area.

What Should I Do if I Miss the Trash Pickup?

If you miss your trash pickup day, you can contact the El Paso Environmental Services Department to schedule a special pickup. However, this service may come at an additional cost.

How Should I Prepare My Trash for Pickup?

To ensure that your trash is picked up on schedule, you should always follow these guidelines:

  • Place your garbage cans and recycling bins on the curb by 6:00 AM on your scheduled pickup day.
  • Do not overfill your garbage cans or recycling bins, as this can cause spillage and littering.
  • Separate your garbage and recycling items properly to avoid contamination.
  • Make sure your bulky items are placed on the curb on the designated collection day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Should I Do if My Trash Pickup Falls on a Holiday?

If your trash pickup day falls on a holiday, the city of El Paso may adjust the schedule. You should check the city's website or contact the Environmental Services Department for more information.

Can I Get a Larger Garbage Can?

Yes, you can request a larger garbage can from the city of El Paso. However, this service may come at an additional cost.

Can I Recycle Glass and Plastic Bags?

No, you cannot recycle glass or plastic bags in El Paso. These items should be placed in the trash.

What Should I Do if I Have Hazardous Waste to Dispose of?

If you have hazardous waste to dispose of, you should not place it in your regular trash. The city of El Paso offers a hazardous waste disposal program that you can participate in. You can contact the Environmental Services Department for more information.

Conclusion

The 2024 El Paso Trash Pickup Calendar is an essential tool for every resident in the city. By following the schedule and guidelines outlined in this article, you can help keep your neighborhood and city clean and organized. If you have any questions or concerns about the trash pickup schedule, you can contact the El Paso Environmental Services Department for assistance.
