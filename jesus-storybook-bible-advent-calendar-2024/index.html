What is the Jesus Storybook Bible Advent Calendar?

The Jesus Storybook Bible Advent Calendar is a unique way to count down the days to Christmas. It is based on the best-selling book, "The Jesus Storybook Bible," by Sally Lloyd-Jones. This advent calendar contains 25 days of readings, each with a corresponding ornament. Each day, you will read a short passage from the Bible and hang the corresponding ornament on your tree. By the end of the month, you will have a beautifully decorated tree and a deeper understanding of the story of God's love for us.

Unlike other advent calendars that focus only on the Christmas story, the Jesus Storybook Bible Advent Calendar takes you on a journey from creation to the birth of Jesus.
