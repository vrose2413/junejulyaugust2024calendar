What is a Funko Pop Advent Calendar?

A Funko Pop Advent Calendar is a holiday-themed box that includes 24 small Funko Pop figures. Each figure is hidden behind a numbered door that corresponds to a specific day in December leading up to Christmas. The concept of an Advent calendar dates back to the 19th century and is still a popular tradition today.
