How To Sync Macbook Calendar With Google Calendar 2024

Written by Pauline Lafleur Apr 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Introduction

Are you tired of manually entering events in your MacBook calendar and Google calendar separately? Well, worry no more! In this tutorial, we will guide you on how to sync your MacBook calendar with Google calendar in 2024. With this, you can easily manage all your events in one place.

Step 1: Set up Google Calendar

Before syncing, you need to make sure that your Google Calendar is set up correctly. Log in to your Google account and click on the "Calendar" tab. From there, you can create new calendars, add events, and customize your settings.

Question: How can I create a new calendar in Google Calendar?

To create a new calendar, click on the "Create new calendar" button on the left-hand side of the screen. You can then name your calendar, choose your time zone, and set other preferences.

Step 2: Enable iCloud Calendar

To sync your MacBook calendar with Google calendar, you need to enable iCloud calendar first. Go to "System Preferences" on your MacBook and click on "iCloud." Check the box next to "Calendars" to enable iCloud Calendar.

Question: How can I check if iCloud Calendar is enabled?

To check if iCloud Calendar is enabled, go to your MacBook's Calendar app and click on "Calendar" at the top. If "iCloud" is checked, then it's enabled.

Step 3: Add Google Account to Calendar

Next, you need to add your Google account to your MacBook's Calendar app. Go to "Calendar"> "Preferences"> "Accounts" and click on the "+" button. Select "Google" from the dropdown menu and enter your Google account information.

Question: What if I have multiple Google accounts?

If you have multiple Google accounts, you can add them all to your MacBook's Calendar app. Just follow the same steps for each account.

Step 4: Choose Which Calendars to Sync

Once you've added your Google account, you can choose which calendars to sync. Go to "Calendar"> "Preferences"> "Accounts" and select your Google account. Check the boxes next to the calendars you want to sync.

Question: Can I sync all my Google calendars with my MacBook?

Yes, you can sync all your Google calendars with your MacBook by checking the box next to "All calendars."

Step 5: Sync Your Calendars

Now that you've set up everything, it's time to sync your calendars. Go to "Calendar"> "Preferences"> "Accounts" and click on "Refresh Calendars." This will sync all your MacBook and Google events in real-time.

Question: Will all my future events be synced as well?

Yes, all your future events will be synced automatically. You don't have to manually add them to both calendars.

Conclusion

Syncing your MacBook calendar with Google calendar is a simple process that can save you time and effort. With just a few clicks, you can manage all your events in one place and never miss an important appointment again. Follow these steps, and you'll be syncing like a pro in no time!
