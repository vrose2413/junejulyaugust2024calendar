Introduction

The Beaufort County School District is a public school district located in Beaufort County, South Carolina. As a parent, teacher, or student, planning your school year is critical to ensure you don't miss important dates. In this article, we will cover the Beaufort County School Calendar for 2024, including when school starts, holidays, and other essential dates.

When Does School Start?

The Beaufort County School District generally starts its school year in August. For the year 2024, the first day of school will be on August 19. This date may vary based on any changes made by the district.

What are the Holidays for 2024?

The Beaufort County School District observes various holidays throughout the year. In 2024, students will have the following holidays: - Labor Day (September 2) - Fall Break (October 14-15) - Thanksgiving Break (November 27-29) - Winter Break (December 23-January 3) - Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 20) - Spring Break (April 1-5) - Memorial Day (May 27) It's important to note that the above dates may be subject to change based on district decisions.

What are the Other Essential Dates?

Apart from the holidays, there are other essential dates that parents, teachers, and students need to keep in mind. Some of these dates include: - First day of school (August 19) - End of the first quarter (October 18) - End of the second quarter (December 20) - End of the third quarter (March 14) - End of the school year (May 31)

FAQs

Q: Will there be any make-up days?

A: Yes. In case of any school cancellation due to inclement weather or other reasons, the district has designated make-up days. These dates may vary based on the district's decision.

Q: Can I subscribe to the school calendar?

A: Yes. You can subscribe to the Beaufort County School District calendar on their website. This will ensure that you receive updates and reminders of important dates via email or other means.

Q: Are there any changes to the school hours?

A: At the moment, there are no changes to the school hours for 2024. However, it's important to keep checking the district's website for any updates or changes.

Conclusion

Planning ahead is essential, especially for parents, teachers, and students. The Beaufort County School District calendar for 2024 provides an overview of important dates, including holidays, make-up days, and the end of the school year. By keeping these dates in mind, you can plan your school year accordingly and ensure that you don't miss any critical events.
