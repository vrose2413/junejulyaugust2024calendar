What is Moon Void of Course?

Moon Void of Course (MVoC) refers to a period of time when the moon is transitioning from one zodiac sign to another and is not making any major aspects to other planets. During this time, astrologers believe that it is not an appropriate time to start new projects or make important decisions as the energy is considered unpredictable and unstable.

What is a Moon Void of Course Calendar?

A Moon Void of Course Calendar is a tool used by astrologers to track the dates and times of MVoC periods throughout the year. This calendar helps people to plan their activities and avoid important decisions during MVoC periods.

What Should You Do During Moon Void of Course?

Astrologers suggest that during MVoC periods, it is best to focus on routine activities such as cleaning, organizing, and finishing up old projects.
