Introduction

The University of Edinburgh is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the United Kingdom. It was founded in 1582 and has a long history of academic excellence. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various faculties.

What is the University of Edinburgh Calendar?

The University of Edinburgh Calendar is a document that contains important information about the academic year. It includes details about term dates, holidays, exam periods, and other key events.

When is the University of Edinburgh Calendar Published?

The University of Edinburgh Calendar is usually published in advance of the academic year. For example, the calendar for the 2024-25 academic year will be published in the summer of 2023.

What's New in the 2024-25 Calendar?

The University of Edinburgh is constantly updating its course offerings and academic calendar to ensure that students receive the best possible education. Some of the changes that students can expect to see in the 2024-25 calendar include: - New undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various faculties - Revised term dates and exam periods - Updated academic regulations and policies

FAQs

When Does the Academic Year Start?

The academic year at the University of Edinburgh usually starts in September. For the 2024-25 academic year, the first term will run from Monday, September 16th, 2024 to Friday, December 13th, 2024.

Are There Any Holidays During the Academic Year?

Yes, there are several holidays during the academic year. The university is closed on Christmas Day, Boxing Day, and New Year's Day. There are also Easter holidays and a mid-semester break in the second term.

When Are the Exam Periods?

The exam periods are usually in December and May. For the 2024-25 academic year, the first exam period will run from Monday, December 16th, 2024 to Friday, December 20th, 2024. The second exam period will run from Monday, May 5th, 2025 to Friday, May 16th, 2025.

Conclusion

The University of Edinburgh Calendar is an important document for students and staff. It provides key information about the academic year, including term dates, holidays, and exam periods.
