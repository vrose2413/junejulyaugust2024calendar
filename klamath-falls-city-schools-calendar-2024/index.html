Introduction

Klamath Falls City Schools is one of the leading educational institutions in Oregon, catering to students from kindergarten to 12th grade. The school calendar plays a crucial role in planning the academic year, and the Klamath Falls City Schools calendar for 2024 is highly anticipated.

What is Klamath Falls City Schools Calendar?

The Klamath Falls City Schools Calendar is a document that outlines the academic year for the students, faculty, and staff of the school district. It includes important dates such as the first and last day of school, holidays, exam schedules, and other significant events.

Why is the 2024 Calendar Significant?

The 2024 calendar is significant as it marks the beginning of a new decade and a fresh start for the school district. Additionally, it provides a glimpse into the future and helps students, faculty, and staff plan their schedules accordingly.

Important Dates in the Klamath Falls City Schools Calendar 2024

When Does the School Year Begin and End?

The school year for Klamath Falls City Schools in 2024 will begin on Monday, August 26th, and end on Friday, June 7th. This is the standard academic year for the school district.

What are the Major Holidays in the Calendar?

The Klamath Falls City Schools calendar for 2024 includes the major holidays such as Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents Day, Memorial Day, and Independence Day.

When are the Exams Scheduled?

The exam schedule for Klamath Falls City Schools in 2024 is yet to be announced. However, it typically takes place in the last week of January and the first week of June.

FAQs

What is the Length of the School Day?

The length of the school day varies by grade level. For elementary students, the school day begins at 8:00 am and ends at 2:30 pm. For middle and high school students, the school day begins at 8:30 am and ends at 3:30 pm.

What is the Dress Code for Students?

Klamath Falls City Schools has a strict dress code policy that all students must adhere to. The dress code includes guidelines on what students can and cannot wear, such as no revealing clothing, no offensive graphics, and no hats indoors.

What Extracurricular Activities are Available?

Klamath Falls City Schools offers a wide range of extracurricular activities for its students, including sports teams, music programs, drama clubs, and academic clubs. Students are encouraged to participate in these activities to develop their skills and interests outside of the classroom.
