If you're a Binghamton University student, you're probably already thinking ahead to the spring 2024 semester. One of the most important things to keep in mind is the academic calendar. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the Binghamton academic calendar for spring 2024.

What is the Binghamton Academic Calendar?

The Binghamton academic calendar is a schedule of important dates and deadlines for the university's academic year. This includes dates for the start and end of the semester, holidays, registration periods, and more.

When does Spring Semester 2024 begin?

According to the Binghamton academic calendar, the spring semester for 2024 will begin on Monday, January 22nd. This is the first day of classes for the semester.

Important Dates

When is Spring Break?

Spring Break for the 2024 semester will take place from March 16th through March 24th. This is a great time to take a break from classes and recharge.

When is the Last Day of Classes?

The last day of classes for the spring 2024 semester is Friday, May 10th. This is an important date to keep in mind as you plan your final projects and exams.

When is Commencement?

Commencement for the spring 2024 semester will take place on Sunday, May 19th. This is a special day for graduating students and their families, so make sure to mark it on your calendar.

When is Registration for Spring 2024?

Registration for the spring 2024 semester will take place from November 6th through November 17th. This is an important time to make sure you're registered for the classes you need to take.

When is the Deadline to Drop a Class?

The deadline to drop a class for the spring 2024 semester is March 8th. After this date, you will not be able to drop a class without special permission from the university.
