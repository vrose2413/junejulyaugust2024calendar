Question: What are some of the biggest music festivals in Austin in 2024?

South by Southwest (SXSW)

SXSW is one of the most popular music festivals in the world, attracting thousands of musicians and music lovers every year. In 2024, the festival is scheduled to take place from March 8 to March 17. The festival features a wide range of music genres, as well as film and interactive media.

ACL is another popular music festival that takes place in Austin every year. The festival is scheduled to take place from October 4 to October 13 in 2024. The festival features a mix of mainstream and indie artists, covering a wide range of music genres.

Old Settler's Music Festival is a roots and Americana music festival that takes place in April every year. The festival is scheduled to take place from April 18 to April 21 in 2024. The festival features a mix of established and up-and-coming artists, as well as workshops and camping.
