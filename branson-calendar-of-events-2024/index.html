Branson Calendar Of Events 2024: Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Mable Stanley Oct 27, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you planning to visit Branson in 2024 but don't know where to start? Look no further! We've got you covered with this ultimate guide to Branson's calendar of events for 2024. From music festivals to sporting events, we've rounded up the top events happening in Branson next year.

Q: When is the best time to visit Branson?

A: While there is no bad time to visit Branson, the best time to visit is during the summer months, especially from May to September. During this time, the weather is warm, and several outdoor events take place.

Top Events in Branson 2024

1. Branson Music Fest

Branson Music Fest is an annual event that celebrates the best of country, bluegrass, and gospel music. The event takes place in May and features performances from some of the biggest names in the music industry.

2. Memorial Day Weekend

Memorial Day Weekend is a time to honor the brave men and women who have served our country. Branson hosts several events, including a parade, a flag-raising ceremony, and a fireworks display.

3. Branson Landing's Annual Summer Concert Series

Branson Landing's Annual Summer Concert Series is a free event that takes place every Saturday from June to August. The event features live music from local and regional artists.

4. Fourth of July Celebration

The Fourth of July is a big deal in Branson. The city hosts several events, including a parade, a fireworks display, and a concert featuring some of the biggest names in music.

5. BransonFest

BransonFest is a three-day event that takes place in August and celebrates the best of Branson. The event features live music, food vendors, and a craft fair.

Q: What is the dress code for events in Branson?

A: The dress code for events in Branson varies depending on the event. For music festivals and concerts, casual attire is acceptable. However, for more formal events, such as galas or fundraisers, formal attire is required.

Q: Are there any family-friendly events in Branson?

A: Yes, there are several family-friendly events in Branson, including the Ozark Mountain Christmas Festival and the Silver Dollar City Harvest Festival. These events feature activities for children, such as holiday-themed crafts, carnival rides, and live entertainment.

Conclusion

Branson is a fantastic destination for music lovers, sports enthusiasts, and families. With so many events happening throughout the year, there is always something to see and do. Plan your visit to Branson in 2024 and experience the best of what this vibrant city has to offer.
