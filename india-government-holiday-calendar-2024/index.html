What are the Holidays in 2024?

National Holidays

There are three national holidays in 2024:

  • Republic Day - January 26th
  • Independence Day - August 15th
  • Gandhi Jayanti - October 2nd

Regional Holidays

There are several regional holidays in India. These holidays vary from state to state. Some of the important regional holidays in 2024 are:

  • Bihu - April 14th (Assam)
  • Rath Yatra - July 4th (Odisha)
  • Bakrid - September 2nd (Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Telangana)
  • Onam - September 7th (Kerala)

Religious Holidays

India is a country with diverse religions. The holiday calendar includes holidays for all major religions. Some of the important religious holidays in 2024 are:

  • Mahashivratri - February 21st
  • Guru Nanak Jayanti - November 19th
  • Christmas - December 25th
