SDA General Conference 2024 Calendar 2024

The Seventh-day Adventist (SDA) General Conference is a worldwide organization that coordinates the work of the SDA Church. Every five years, the General Conference Session is held to elect leaders, discuss important issues, and plan for the future. In 2024, the General Conference Session will be held in Indianapolis, Indiana, USA.

Date and Venue

The 2024 General Conference Session will be held from June 23 to July 3, 2024, at the Indiana Convention Center in Indianapolis, Indiana. The convention center is a state-of-the-art facility that can accommodate large events like the General Conference Session.

Program

Plenary Sessions

The plenary sessions are the main business meetings of the General Conference Session. Delegates from around the world gather to hear reports, discuss issues, and make decisions that affect the entire church. These sessions are open to all attendees.

Worship Services

Worship services are an important part of the General Conference Session. They provide an opportunity for attendees to come together in prayer, praise, and reflection. Guest speakers and musicians are invited to lead these services.

Seminars and Workshops

Seminars and workshops cover a wide range of topics, from theology to evangelism to health. These sessions are designed to provide practical training and resources for church leaders and members.

Accommodations

There are several hotels near the Indiana Convention Center that offer special rates for General Conference Session attendees. It is recommended to book early to ensure availability.

Transportation

Indianapolis is easily accessible by air, as it is served by the Indianapolis International Airport. The convention center is located in the heart of downtown, making it easy to get around on foot or by public transportation.

FAQs

Who can attend the General Conference Session?

Delegates, church leaders, and members of the SDA Church are welcome to attend the General Conference Session. However, some sessions may be restricted to certain groups.

What is the dress code for the General Conference Session?

The dress code is business casual. However, attendees are encouraged to dress modestly and respectfully.

What languages are spoken at the General Conference Session?

The official languages of the General Conference Session are English, French, Spanish, and Portuguese. Interpretation services are provided for these languages.

How can I register for the General Conference Session?

Registration information will be available on the General Conference website closer to the event. Check the website regularly for updates.

The 2024 General Conference Session promises to be an exciting and important event for the SDA Church. It is a time for delegates, church leaders, and members to come together to discuss the future of the church and to be inspired by worship, learning, and fellowship. We hope to see you there!
