What is a Dog Advent Calendar?

If you're not familiar with advent calendars, they are special calendars that count down the days until Christmas. Each day, you open a door to reveal a small treat or gift. Dog advent calendars are no different, except the treats are specially designed for your furry friend.

What's Inside the Trader Joe's Dog Advent Calendar?

The Trader Joe's dog advent calendar features 24 different treats, one for each day leading up to Christmas. The treats are made with high-quality ingredients and are designed to be both tasty and healthy for your dog.

Are the Treats Suitable for All Dogs?

The treats in the Trader Joe's dog advent calendar are suitable for most dogs, but it's always a good idea to check the ingredients list if your dog has any allergies or dietary restrictions. If you're unsure, you can always consult with your veterinarian before giving your dog any new treats.
