Introduction

It's that time of year again! The Linn County Fair & Expo is almost here, and we can't wait to share with you everything that's in store for 2024. This year's event promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with an incredible lineup of attractions, shows, and activities for the whole family to enjoy. Whether you're a lifelong resident or just passing through, the Linn County Fair & Expo is an experience you won't want to miss.

What is the Linn County Fair & Expo?

The Linn County Fair & Expo is an annual event that celebrates the best of Linn County, Oregon. It's a week-long festival that brings together local businesses, organizations, and residents to showcase their talents, products, and services. The fair features a wide range of attractions, including live music, carnival rides, food vendors, livestock shows, and more. It's a great opportunity to connect with the community and have some fun!

When and Where is the Linn County Fair & Expo?

The Linn County Fair & Expo takes place in the heart of Linn County, Oregon. The event runs from July 17th to July 23rd, 2024, and is held at the Linn County Fairgrounds. The fairgrounds are located at 3700 Knox Butte Rd E, Albany, OR 97322.

What's New in 2024?

We're always looking for ways to improve the Linn County Fair & Expo, and this year is no exception. Here are just a few of the exciting new features you can expect in 2024:

  • A brand new carnival with more rides and attractions than ever before
  • A larger petting zoo, featuring a wide variety of animals
  • Expanded food options, including vegan and gluten-free options
  • More live music, with performances from both local and national artists

FAQs

Q: How much does it cost to attend the Linn County Fair & Expo?

A: Admission to the fair is $10 for adults, $5 for children ages 6-12, and free for children 5 and under. Parking is free.

Q: What are the hours of the Linn County Fair & Expo?

A: The fair is open from 10am to 10pm daily, with the exception of the final day, when it closes at 6pm.

Q: Can I bring my own food and drinks to the fair?

A: Outside food and drinks are not allowed on the fairgrounds. However, there are plenty of food vendors that offer a variety of options to suit all tastes and dietary needs.

Q: Is the Linn County Fair & Expo accessible for people with disabilities?

A: Yes! The fairgrounds are fully accessible, with wheelchair ramps and accessible restrooms available throughout the grounds.

Q: Are pets allowed at the Linn County Fair & Expo?

A: Only service animals are allowed on the fairgrounds. For the safety of your pets and other fairgoers, we ask that you leave your pets at home.

Conclusion

The Linn County Fair & Expo is always a highlight of the summer, and 2024 promises to be no exception. With new attractions, delicious food, and live entertainment, there's something for everyone to enjoy. We hope to see you there!
