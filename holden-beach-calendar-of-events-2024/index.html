Introduction

Are you looking for a perfect beach getaway in 2024? Look no further than Holden Beach, North Carolina! With its pristine beaches, charming small-town vibe, and rich calendar of events, Holden Beach is the ultimate vacation destination. In this article, we’ll give you a sneak peek into the exciting events lined up for 2024 in Holden Beach.

What’s on the Calendar?

The Holden Beach calendar of events is packed with activities for visitors of all ages. From music festivals to sporting events, there’s always something happening in this coastal town. Here are some of the highlights you can expect in 2024:

1. Beach Music Festival

Are you a fan of beach music? Then you won’t want to miss the Holden Beach Music Festival, which takes place in June 2024. This festival features live music from local and regional artists, as well as food and drinks from local vendors. It’s the perfect way to kick off your summer vacation!

2. Independence Day Celebration

Looking for a patriotic way to celebrate the Fourth of July? Holden Beach’s Independence Day Celebration is just what you need. This event features a parade, live music, food trucks, and a spectacular fireworks display over the ocean. It’s a great way to spend the day with family and friends.

3. North Carolina Oyster Festival

Love seafood? Then you won’t want to miss the North Carolina Oyster Festival in October 2024. This festival celebrates the state’s famous oysters with live music, arts and crafts vendors, and of course, plenty of oysters to sample. It’s a must-visit for foodies!

Question and Answer

Q: Are the events free to attend?

A: Most of the events on the Holden Beach calendar are free to attend. However, some events may require tickets or a small fee for entry.

Q: Is Holden Beach a good destination for families?

A: Absolutely! Holden Beach is a family-friendly destination with plenty of activities for visitors of all ages. From beach days to mini-golf, there’s something for everyone.

Q: What’s the weather like in Holden Beach in 2024?

A: While we can’t predict the weather with certainty, Holden Beach typically has mild winters and hot summers. We recommend checking the weather forecast before your trip.

Conclusion

Holden Beach is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a relaxing beach vacation with plenty of activities. With so many exciting events on the calendar for 2024, there’s never been a better time to plan your trip. We hope this guide has given you a taste of what Holden Beach has to offer – we can’t wait to see you there!
