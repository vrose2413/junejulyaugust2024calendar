When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for students in Dekalb County will be August 5th, 2024. The last day of school will be May 23rd, 2025. This means that students will have a total of 180 days of instruction.

Fall Break

What About Inclement Weather?

In the event of inclement weather, the school district may cancel school or delay the start of school. Parents and students should stay informed about any cancellations or delays through the school district's website or local news outlets.

What Happens on Early Release Days?

On early release days, students will be dismissed from school early. The exact time of dismissal will depend on the school, but most schools will dismiss students 2-3 hours earlier than usual. Parents should make arrangements to pick up their children or arrange for them to be picked up by a designated adult.

If you have more questions about the Dekalb County Schools 2024-25 calendar, you can visit the school district's website or contact your child's school directly. The 2024-25 school year in Dekalb County will be filled with important dates and events.
