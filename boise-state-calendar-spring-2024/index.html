Introduction

The Boise State University academic calendar for Spring 2024 is now available for students, parents, and faculty members to view. Important Dates

The Spring 2024 semester begins on January 8, 2024, and ends on May 10, 2024. There are several important dates to keep in mind throughout the semester. January 15 is the last day to add or drop classes without a "W" grade. February 19-20 is Presidents' Day weekend, which is a university holiday. March 11-15 is Spring Break. April 19-22 is the week of final exams.

Registration

Registration for Spring 2024 classes begins on November 6, 2023. Students should meet with their academic advisors to plan their schedules and ensure they are on track to graduate. It is important to register as early as possible to secure the classes you need and to avoid late registration fees.

Graduation

The Spring 2024 graduation ceremony will be held on May 11, 2024. Students who plan to graduate in the spring must apply for graduation by March 1, 2024. The ceremony is a celebration of students' academic achievements and marks the beginning of their professional careers.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I take online classes during Spring 2024?

A: Yes, Boise State University offers online classes for students who prefer to learn remotely. You can search for online classes in the course catalog and register for them during the regular registration period.

Q: When is the last day to drop a class?

A: The last day to drop a class without a "W" grade is January 15, 2024. After that date, you can still drop a class, but you will receive a "W" grade on your transcript, which may affect your GPA.

Q: How do I apply for graduation?

A: You can apply for graduation through your student portal. The application deadline for Spring 2024 graduation is March 1, 2024. Make sure to meet with your academic advisor to ensure you have completed all the necessary requirements for graduation.
