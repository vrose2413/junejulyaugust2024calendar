Introduction

Why Connect Google Calendar to Notion?

By connecting Google Calendar to Notion, you can keep track of your events and tasks in one place. This can help you stay organized and save time by avoiding the need to switch between multiple apps.

Can You Connect Google Calendar to Notion?

Yes, you can connect Google Calendar to Notion using third-party integrations such as Zapier or Automate.io. These integrations allow you to automatically create tasks or events in Notion based on your Google Calendar events, or vice versa.

How to Connect Google Calendar to Notion?

To connect Google Calendar to Notion, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Integration Platform

There are several integration platforms available, such as Zapier, Automate.io, or Integromat. Choose the one that suits your needs and budget.

Step 2: Create Your Account

Sign up for an account on your chosen integration platform.

Step 3: Connect Google Calendar and Notion

Follow the instructions on your integration platform to connect Google Calendar and Notion.

Step 4: Set Up Your Automation

Create your automation workflow based on your preferences. For example, you can create a task in Notion whenever you have a new event in Google Calendar.

Step 5: Test and Launch Your Automation

Test your automation and make sure it works as expected. Then, launch it and enjoy the benefits of having your Google Calendar events and tasks in Notion!

Conclusion

Connecting Google Calendar to Notion can help you stay organized and save time. By using third-party integrations, you can automate the process and enjoy the benefits of having your events and tasks in one place. Try it out and see how it can improve your productivity!
