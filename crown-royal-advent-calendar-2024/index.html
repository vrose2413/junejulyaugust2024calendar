If you're a whiskey lover, then you know that Crown Royal is one of the most popular brands on the market. And if you're looking for a unique way to enjoy this delicious spirit during the holiday season, then you need to get your hands on a Crown Royal Advent Calendar.

What is a Crown Royal Advent Calendar?

A Crown Royal Advent Calendar is a special holiday-themed box that contains 24 mini bottles of Crown Royal whiskey. Each bottle is a different flavor, giving you the chance to try a variety of the brand's offerings.

What flavors are included in the 2024 Crown Royal Advent Calendar?

While the official list of flavors for the 2024 Crown Royal Advent Calendar hasn't been released yet, you can expect it to include some of the brand's most popular offerings, such as:

  • Crown Royal Reserve
  • Crown Royal Northern Harvest Rye
  • Crown Royal Black
  • Crown Royal Vanilla

Why Should You Get a Crown Royal Advent Calendar?

If you're a whiskey lover, then there are plenty of reasons why you should get a Crown Royal Advent Calendar. Here are just a few:

How much does a Crown Royal Advent Calendar cost?

The cost of a Crown Royal Advent Calendar varies depending on where you buy it and what year it is. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $100 for one.

Where Can You Buy a Crown Royal Advent Calendar?

Crown Royal Advent Calendars can be bought at most liquor stores and online retailers. However, they tend to sell out quickly, so it's best to get yours early in the holiday season.
