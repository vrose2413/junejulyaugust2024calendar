About Barry University

Barry University is a private Catholic university located in Miami Shores, Florida. It was founded in 1940 and offers over 100 undergraduate and graduate programs. The university has a diverse student body and is committed to providing a personalized education experience.

Overview of Fall 2024 Calendar

The Fall 2024 semester at Barry University will begin on August 26th and end on December 13th. There will be a total of 15 weeks of classes, with a one-week break in November for the Thanksgiving holiday.

Important Dates

Here are some important dates to keep in mind for the Fall 2024 semester:

  • August 26th: First day of classes
  • September 2nd: Labor Day (university closed)
  • November 25th-29th: Thanksgiving Break
  • December 13th: Last day of classes

Registration Information

Registration for Fall 2024 classes will open on April 1st, 2024. Students are encouraged to register as early as possible to ensure that they get the classes they need. The university offers online registration through its student portal, which can be accessed through the Barry University website.

Tuition and Fees

The cost of tuition for the Fall 2024 semester at Barry University will vary depending on the program and number of credits taken. The university offers financial aid and scholarships to eligible students. It is important to check with the financial aid office for more information on available options.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I access the Fall 2024 calendar?

A: The Fall 2024 calendar can be found on the Barry University website under the academic calendar section. It is also available through the student portal.

Q: What should I do if I need to drop a class?

A: Students can drop classes through the student portal up until the deadline, which is usually around the midpoint of the semester. It is important to check with the registrar's office for the exact deadline and any applicable fees.

Q: What happens if I miss a class?

A: It is important to attend all classes, as attendance policies vary by professor. If a student misses a class, they should contact the professor and make arrangements to catch up on any missed material.

A: Barry University offers a variety of clubs and organizations for students to get involved in. Students can also participate in intramural sports, community service projects, and campus events. Q: What resources are available for academic support?

A: Barry University offers a variety of resources for academic support, including tutoring services, writing centers, and study groups. Students can also speak with their professors or academic advisors for additional support.

Conclusion

The Fall 2024 semester at Barry University offers a variety of opportunities for students to learn and grow. With a diverse student body, personalized education experience, and a wide range of academic and extracurricular activities available, students are sure to have a memorable and enriching semester.
