How To Remove Working Location From Google Calendar 2024?

Written by Mable Stanley Apr 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Google Calendar is a popular tool that helps individuals and businesses keep track of their schedules. It allows you to add events, set reminders, and even share your calendar with others. However, if you’re someone who travels frequently or does not want to reveal your working location, you may want to remove the working location from your Google Calendar. In this article, we’ll show you how to remove the working location from Google Calendar 2024.

Why Remove Working Location from Google Calendar?

There can be many reasons why you may want to remove the working location from your Google Calendar. For instance, you may not want to reveal your location to others for privacy reasons. Or, if you’re someone who travels frequently, you may not want to clutter your calendar with different locations. Whatever your reason may be, removing the working location from your Google Calendar is easy to do.

Step-by-Step Guide to Remove Working Location from Google Calendar

Here are the steps to remove working location from your Google Calendar:

Step 1:

Open your Google Calendar and click on the event that you want to remove the working location from.

Step 2:

Click on the “Edit Event” button.

Step 3:

Scroll down to the “Event Details” section.

Step 4:

Click on the “Location” field and delete the working location.

Step 5:

Click on the “Save” button to save the changes.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I Remove Working Location from Multiple Events at Once?

A: Yes, you can remove the working location from multiple events at once. Here’s how:

1. Open your Google Calendar and click on the “Settings” icon.

2. Click on “Settings” from the dropdown menu.

3. Under “Settings for my calendars” section, click on the calendar that you want to remove the working location from.

4. Scroll down to the “Event settings” section.

5. Check the box next to “Remove location from all events”.

6. Click on the “Save” button to save the changes.

Q: Will Removing Working Location Affect My Calendar Notifications?

A: No, removing the working location from your Google Calendar will not affect your calendar notifications. You will still receive notifications for your events as usual.

Conclusion

Removing the working location from your Google Calendar is a simple process. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily remove the working location from your events. Whether you’re someone who values their privacy or just wants to keep their calendar clutter-free, removing the working location is a great solution. So, go ahead and give it a try!
