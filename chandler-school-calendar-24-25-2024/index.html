Introduction

As we approach the academic year 2023-2024, parents and students alike are curious about the upcoming school calendar in Chandler, Arizona. When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for the 2024 academic year is on August 5, 2024, while the last day is on May 28, 2025. This school year will run for a total of 175 school days, with a total of 10 days allocated for breaks and holidays.

What Are the Important Dates to Remember?

Parents and students should mark their calendars for important dates such as the first day of school, holidays, and teacher in-service days. The Chandler School District will have the following holidays in the 2024-2025 academic year: Labor Day, Thanksgiving Break, Winter Break, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Spring Break, and Memorial Day.

What Are the Other Activities Planned for the School Year?

Aside from the usual academic activities, the Chandler School District has a variety of other activities planned for the 2024-2025 academic year. These activities include sports events, musicals, and other extracurricular activities. Parents and students should check the school district website regularly for updates on these activities.

What Are the Teacher In-Service Days?

Teacher in-service days are days when teachers and staff members attend workshops or training sessions. These days are set aside for professional development, and students will not have classes on these days. For the 2024-2025 academic year, the Chandler School District has set aside six teacher in-service days.

What Is the Winter Break Schedule?

The Winter Break for the 2024-2025 academic year will begin on December 20, 2024, and end on January 3, 2025. Students and staff members will have a total of 10 days off during this period. Classes will resume on January 6, 2025.

What Is the Spring Break Schedule?

The Spring Break for the 2024-2025 academic year will begin on March 9, 2025, and end on March 16, 2025. Students and staff members will have a total of 8 days off during this period. Classes will resume on March 17, 2025.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

Graduation dates for the 2024-2025 academic year have not yet been announced. However, parents and students can expect high school graduations to take place in late May or early June 2025.

The Chandler School Calendar 24-25 2024 is packed with exciting activities and important dates.
