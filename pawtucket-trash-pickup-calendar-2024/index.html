Pawtucket Trash Pickup Calendar 2024

Introduction

Are you a Pawtucket resident wondering when your trash will be picked up in 2024? Look no further, as we have the latest calendar information for you!

When will the trash pickup start?

The Pawtucket trash pickup calendar for 2024 will begin on Monday, January 3rd. Make sure to have your trash out by 7am on your scheduled pickup day to ensure it gets picked up.

How often will the trash be picked up?

Trash will be picked up on a weekly basis, every Monday throughout the year. This excludes holidays, in which case the pickup will be delayed to the next day.

Important Dates

Here are some important dates to remember for the Pawtucket trash pickup calendar in 2024:

  • New Year's Day - January 1st (no trash pickup)
  • Martin Luther King Day - January 17th (trash pickup delayed to Tuesday, January 18th)
  • Memorial Day - May 27th (trash pickup delayed to Tuesday, May 28th)
  • Independence Day - July 4th (trash pickup delayed to Tuesday, July 5th)
  • Labor Day - September 2nd (trash pickup delayed to Tuesday, September 3rd)
  • Thanksgiving Day - November 28th (trash pickup delayed to Friday, November 29th)
  • Christmas Day - December 25th (trash pickup delayed to Wednesday, December 26th)

Recycling and Yard Waste

Recycling and yard waste will also be picked up on a weekly basis, but on different days depending on your location. Make sure to check the Pawtucket city website for specific information on your pickup day and guidelines for recycling and yard waste disposal.

Conclusion

Knowing the Pawtucket trash pickup calendar for 2024 is important to ensure that your trash is properly disposed of and doesn't accumulate in your home. Make note of the important dates and guidelines for recycling and yard waste, and have a clean and healthy year!

Any questions?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the Pawtucket trash pickup calendar for 2024, feel free to contact the Pawtucket city department of sanitation for more information.

Happy recycling!
