Introduction

As we approach the academic year of 2024-2025, Frisco Independent School District has released its calendar to help parents, students, and teachers plan accordingly. This article will provide you with all the necessary information regarding the Frisco ISD calendar for the year 2024-2025.

What are the Important Dates?

The Frisco ISD calendar for 2024-2025 will consist of 176 instructional days, which are divided into two semesters. The first semester will commence on August 19, 2024, and the second semester will begin on January 9, 2025.

The calendar also includes the following important dates:

  • Labor Day: September 2, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024
  • Winter Break: December 23, 2024 - January 6, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 10-14, 2025
  • Memorial Day: May 27, 2025

What are the School Hours?

The school hours for Frisco ISD for the academic year of 2024-2025 will be from 8:25 a.m. to 3:40 p.m. for elementary schools, and 9:10 a.m. to 4:20 p.m. for middle and high schools.

It is important to note that each school may have its own schedule, so it is recommended to check with your child's school for any specific changes.

What are the Graduation Dates?

The graduation dates for the Frisco ISD academic year of 2024-2025 have not been released yet. However, they are usually scheduled towards the end of May or early June.

It is important to check with your child's school for the exact date and time of their graduation.

Are There any Professional Development Days?

Yes, the Frisco ISD calendar for 2024-2025 includes professional development days for teachers. These days are scheduled on October 11, 2019, and February 17, 2025.

On these days, there will be no school for students.

What About Bad Weather Days?

In case of bad weather, Frisco ISD has built-in make-up days in the calendar. If there are any cancellations due to inclement weather, the make-up days will be added to the end of the semester.

It is important to keep an eye on the Frisco ISD website or local news channels for any weather-related announcements.

Conclusion

The Frisco ISD calendar for 2024-2025 provides parents, students, and teachers with all the necessary information regarding the upcoming academic year. It is important to keep track of the important dates, school hours, and any changes that may occur throughout the year.
