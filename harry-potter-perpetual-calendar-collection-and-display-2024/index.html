What is a Perpetual Calendar?

A perpetual calendar is a type of calendar that can be used year after year. Unlike traditional calendars, it doesn't have specific dates, but instead has numbered cubes or tiles that can be adjusted to display the correct day, month, and year. This means that you can use the Harry Potter Perpetual Calendar Collection and Display 2024 for years to come.

What Scenes are Included?

The calendar features 12 iconic scenes from the Harry Potter series, one for each month. Some of the scenes include Harry receiving his Hogwarts letter, the Hogwarts Express, and the Battle of Hogwarts.

How Does the Display Stand Work?

The display stand is designed to showcase your favorite scene from the calendar. Simply place the numbered cubes or tiles in the correct order to display your chosen scene, and place it on the stand. The stand also includes a storage compartment for the unused cubes or tiles.
