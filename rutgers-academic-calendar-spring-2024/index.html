When Does the Spring Semester Start?

The spring semester at Rutgers University typically begins in late January or early February. For the spring 2024 semester, classes are set to begin on January 30th, 2024. Make sure to mark this date on your calendar!

When Are Spring Break and Other Holidays?

Spring break is a much-needed respite for students after a busy semester. For the spring 2024 semester, spring break will run from March 16th to March 24th. Additionally, Rutgers observes several holidays throughout the semester, including Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 21st), Presidents' Day (February 18th), and Memorial Day (May 27th).

When Are Final Exams?

Final exams are a crucial part of the academic calendar, and it's important to know when they're scheduled. For the spring 2024 semester, final exams will take place from May 7th to May 14th. Make sure to plan accordingly and start studying early!

What Are Some Important Deadlines?

There are several important deadlines to keep in mind throughout the semester. Here are a few key dates to remember:

  • February 1st: Last day to add a course without permission
  • February 8th: Last day to drop a course without a "W" grade
  • March 29th: Last day to withdraw from a course without a "W" grade
  • April 19th: Last day to withdraw from a course with a "W" grade
  • May 15th: Last day of classes

Can I Take Summer Courses?

If you're interested in taking summer courses at Rutgers, you're in luck! The university offers a wide range of summer courses to help you stay on track with your academic goals. Registration for summer courses typically opens in early March.

What If I Need Help?

If you're struggling with anything related to the academic calendar or your coursework, don't hesitate to reach out for help. Rutgers offers a wide range of resources for students, including academic advising, tutoring services, and counseling services.
