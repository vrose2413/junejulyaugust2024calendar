Introduction

The Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 is the latest addition to Longines' Master Collection series. This watch features a unique annual calendar complication, which displays the date, day, and month, and only requires adjustment once a year. This timepiece is a perfect blend of style and function, making it an ideal choice for watch enthusiasts and collectors.

Design and Features

The Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 has a classic and elegant design. The watch features a stainless steel case with a diameter of 42mm and a thickness of 13mm. The silver dial has a sunray finish and is adorned with applied hour markers and hands coated with Super-LumiNova, making it easy to read in low-light conditions.

The watch is powered by the Longines L897 automatic movement, which has a power reserve of 64 hours. The annual calendar complication is located at the 3 o'clock position and is displayed through three windows. The watch also features a sapphire crystal case back, which allows the wearer to admire the movement.

Question and Answer

Q: What is an annual calendar complication?

An annual calendar complication is a watch feature that displays the date, day, and month and requires adjustment only once a year. It is different from a perpetual calendar complication, which can adjust for leap years and only requires adjustment once every few hundred years.

Q: How do you adjust the annual calendar?

The Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 has an easy-to-use adjustment system. Simply use a pusher located on the side of the case to adjust the date, day, and month. The watch will automatically adjust for months with 30 or 31 days, but will need manual adjustment for February.

Performance and Durability

The Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 is a highly reliable and durable watch. It has a water resistance of up to 30 meters, making it suitable for everyday wear. The stainless steel case and bracelet are scratch-resistant and will maintain their shine for years to come.

Conclusion

The Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 is a beautiful and functional watch that is perfect for any watch enthusiast or collector. Its annual calendar complication is a unique feature that adds to the watch's appeal. The watch is also highly reliable and durable, ensuring that it will last for years to come. If you are looking for a watch that combines style and function, the Longines Master Collection Annual Calendar 2024 is an excellent choice.
