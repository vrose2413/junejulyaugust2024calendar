An Overview of Klein ISD Calendar 2024-2025

As we approach the year 2024, it's important to stay up-to-date with the latest information regarding the Klein Independent School District's calendar. The school district has announced their calendar for the 2024-2025 academic year, which includes important dates such as the first day of school, holidays, and breaks. According to the calendar, the first day of school for students will be on August 19, 2024, and the last day of school will be on May 29, 2025. The school year will consist of 174 instructional days, which will be divided into two semesters.

What Are the Important Dates to Keep in Mind?

To help you plan your schedule, here are some of the important dates to keep in mind: - August 19, 2024: First Day of School - September 2, 2024: Labor Day Holiday - October 14-15, 2024: Fall Break - November 25-29, 2024: Thanksgiving Break - December 20, 2024 - January 6, 2025: Winter Break - March 10-14, 2025: Spring Break - April 18-21, 2025: Easter Break - May 29, 2025: Last Day of School

What Are Some Changes from the Previous Year?

Compared to the previous year's calendar, there are a few changes to keep in mind. For example, the winter break is slightly shorter, with students returning to school on January 7, 2025, instead of January 8, 2024. Additionally, the spring break is slightly longer, with students off from March 10-14, 2025, instead of March 11-15, 2024.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Happens If There are School Closures Due to Inclement Weather?

If there are school closures due to inclement weather, the Klein ISD will notify parents and students through their website, social media, and local news outlets. The district will also provide updates on when schools will reopen.

What Are the School Hours for Klein ISD?

The school hours for Klein ISD vary depending on the grade level. Elementary school hours are typically from 8:00 am to 3:30 pm, while middle and high school hours are typically from 8:40 am to 4:10 pm. However, it's best to check with your specific school for their exact hours.

When Will Report Cards Be Distributed?

Report cards will be distributed at the end of each semester. For the 2024-2025 academic year, the first semester ends on December 20, 2024, and the second semester ends on May 29, 2025. Report cards will be distributed shortly after the end of each semester.

Conclusion

By staying up-to-date with the Klein ISD calendar for the 2024-2025 academic year, you can plan your schedule accordingly and ensure that you don't miss any important dates. Whether you're a student, parent, or teacher, it's important to stay informed and prepared for the upcoming school year. If you have any further questions, be sure to reach out to your specific school or the Klein ISD district office for more information.
