How To Automatically Copy Events From One Google Calendar To Another In 2024

Written by Ben Javu Apr 17, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Are you tired of manually copying events from one Google calendar to another? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing how to automatically copy events from one Google calendar to another in 2024.

Step 1: Create a New Google Calendar

The first step to automatically copying events is to create a new Google calendar that will act as the destination for your copied events. To do this, simply click on the "+" sign next to "Other calendars" and select "Create new calendar."

Step 2: Get the iCal URL of Your Source Calendar

Next, you'll need to get the iCal URL of the calendar that you want to copy events from. To do this, go to the settings of your source calendar and scroll down to the "Integrate calendar" section. Click on the "Secret address in iCal format" link and copy the URL that is displayed.

Step 3: Set up a New Google Calendar Event

Now that you have your iCal URL, it's time to set up a new Google calendar event that will automatically copy events from your source calendar to your destination calendar. To do this, create a new event in your destination calendar and click on the "Edit event" button.

Step 4: Add the iCal URL to Your Event

In the event details section, scroll down to the "Guests" section and click on the "Add guests" button. In the "Enter an email address" field, paste the iCal URL that you copied earlier and click on the "Add" button.

Step 5: Save Your Event

Finally, save your event and your Google calendar will now automatically copy events from your source calendar to your destination calendar.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I copy events from multiple source calendars to one destination calendar?

A: Yes, you can copy events from multiple source calendars to one destination calendar by following the same steps outlined above for each source calendar.

Q: Will events be deleted from my source calendar after they are copied to my destination calendar?

A: No, events will not be deleted from your source calendar after they are copied to your destination calendar. However, any changes made to the copied events in your destination calendar will not be reflected in your source calendar.

Q: Can I choose which events to copy from my source calendar?

A: No, you cannot choose which events to copy from your source calendar. All events from your source calendar will be copied to your destination calendar.
