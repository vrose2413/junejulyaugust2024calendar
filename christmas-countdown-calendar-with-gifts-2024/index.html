Introduction

These calendars have small gifts or treats for each day leading up to Christmas. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best Christmas countdown calendars with gifts for 2024.

What is an Advent Calendar?

An Advent calendar is a special calendar used to count the days of Advent in anticipation of Christmas. Each day, a small gift or treat is revealed. The calendar typically starts on December 1st and ends on December 24th.

What are the Different Types of Advent Calendars?

There are many different types of advent calendars available, from traditional paper calendars to more modern ones with little doors or drawers. Some advent calendars also have themes, such as beauty products or chocolates.

FAQs

Q: When should I buy an Advent Calendar?
