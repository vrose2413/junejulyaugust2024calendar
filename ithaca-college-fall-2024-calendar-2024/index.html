Introduction

Important Dates

The Fall 2024 semester at Ithaca College will officially begin on Monday, August 26th. This is the first day of classes for undergraduate and graduate students. Labor Day, which falls on Monday, September 2nd, will be observed as a holiday and classes will not be held. Thanksgiving Break will begin on Wednesday, November 27th and classes will resume on Monday, December 2nd. The last day of classes will be on Friday, December 13th and final exams will be held from Monday, December 16th to Friday, December 20th.

Q: When should I plan to move in for the Fall 2024 semester?

A: Move-in day for the Fall 2024 semester has not yet been announced. Typically, move-in day for new students is a few days before the first day of classes and returning students move in shortly after. Keep an eye on your email and the Ithaca College website for more information as it becomes available.

Q: When is the last day to add or drop a class?

A: The last day to add a class for the Fall 2024 semester is Friday, September 6th. The last day to drop a class without a grade of W is Friday, September 13th. The last day to drop a class with a grade of W is Friday, November 8th.

Exam Periods

The Fall 2024 exam period will take place from Monday, December 16th to Friday, December 20th. During this time, no classes will be held and students will take their final exams. It is important to note that students should plan their travel accordingly and make sure they are available to take their exams during this time.

Q: What happens if I have three exams in one day?

A: If you have three exams scheduled on the same day, you can request to reschedule one of them. However, this is not guaranteed and it is important to communicate with your professors ahead of time if you think this may be an issue for you.

Q: What if I have an exam scheduled during a holiday or break?

A: If you have an exam scheduled during a holiday or break, such as Thanksgiving Break, your exam will still take place as scheduled. It is important to plan your travel accordingly and make sure you are available to take your exam.

Conclusion

The Fall 2024 academic calendar at Ithaca College includes important dates such as the first day of classes, holidays, and exam periods. It is important for students, faculty, and staff to plan accordingly and make sure they are aware of these dates.
