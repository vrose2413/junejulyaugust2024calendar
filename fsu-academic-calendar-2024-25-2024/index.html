FSU Academic Calendar 2024-25

As the 2023 academic year comes to a close, students and faculty members of Florida State University (FSU) are already looking ahead to the next academic year. What is the FSU Academic Calendar?

The FSU academic calendar is a schedule of important dates and events for the academic year. It includes start and end dates for each semester, holidays, exam periods, and other important deadlines.

When Does the 2024-25 Academic Year Begin and End?

The 2024-25 academic year at FSU begins on August 26, 2024, and ends on May 3, 2025. This schedule includes both fall and spring semesters, as well as winter and spring breaks.

What are the Key Dates to Remember?

Some of the most important dates to remember for the 2024-25 academic year include:

  • Fall semester begins: August 26, 2024
  • Thanksgiving Break: November 27-29, 2024
  • Final exams for fall semester: December 9-13, 2024
  • Spring semester begins: January 6, 2025
  • Spring Break: March 9-16, 2025
  • Final exams for spring semester: April 28-May 3, 2025

What are Some Important Deadlines to Keep in Mind?

Along with start and end dates, there are also several important deadlines that students and faculty members should be aware of. These include:

  • Last day to add a class: September 2, 2024
  • Last day to drop a class without a W: September 6, 2024
  • Last day to drop a class with a W: November 4, 2024
  • Last day to apply for graduation: January 24, 2025
  • Last day to withdraw from a class: April 11, 2025

What are Some Tips for Staying on Track?

With so many important dates and deadlines to remember, it can be easy to feel overwhelmed. However, there are several tips that can help you stay on track:

What Are Some Common FAQs About the Academic Calendar?

Here are some common questions and answers about the FSU academic calendar:

Q: What happens if a holiday falls on a weekend?

A: If a holiday falls on a Saturday or Sunday, it will be observed on the following Monday.

Q: Does the academic calendar apply to all FSU campuses?

A: Yes, the academic calendar applies to all FSU campuses, including the Tallahassee campus, Panama City campus, and Sarasota campus.

Q: Can the academic calendar change?

A: Yes, the academic calendar is subject to change. Students and faculty members should regularly check the FSU website for updates and changes.

Q: When will the next academic calendar be released?
