Introduction

Milwaukee Academy of Science, commonly known as MAS, is a charter school located in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. The school follows a science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) curriculum. This article provides a detailed overview of the Milwaukee Academy of Science Calendar 2024.

When does the academic year start?

The academic year at Milwaukee Academy of Science starts in late August. The exact date for the start of the 2024 academic year will be announced closer to the date.

How many days are in the academic year?

The academic year at MAS comprises 180 instructional days. This excludes weekends, holidays, and teacher workdays.

Are there any holidays during the academic year?

Yes, Milwaukee Academy of Science observes several holidays during the academic year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Memorial Day. The exact dates for holidays in 2024 will be released in the MAS Calendar 2024.

First Semester

When does the first semester start?

The first semester of the academic year usually starts in late August and ends in mid-December.

What subjects are covered in the first semester?

The first semester covers a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, English, social studies, and electives.

What are electives?

Electives are courses that students choose to take in addition to their core classes. Electives at MAS include art, music, foreign languages, and physical education.

Second Semester

When does the second semester start?

The second semester usually starts in early January and ends in late May.

What subjects are covered in the second semester?

The second semester covers the same subjects as the first semester, but in greater depth.

Are there any exams during the academic year?

Yes, students take mid-term and final exams during the academic year. The exact dates for exams will be released in the MAS Calendar 2024.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Milwaukee Academy of Science Calendar 2024 provides a detailed timeline for the academic year. The academic year comprises 180 instructional days and is divided into two semesters. Students take mid-term and final exams during the year, and there are several holidays observed. Overall, the MAS Calendar 2024 provides a comprehensive guide for students, teachers, and parents.
