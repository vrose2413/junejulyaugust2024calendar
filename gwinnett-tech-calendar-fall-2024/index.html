Gwinnett Tech Calendar Fall 2024

Written by Ben Javu Oct 09, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

Gwinnett Technical College is a public, two-year college located in Lawrenceville, Georgia. The college offers more than 140 associate degree, diploma, and certificate programs. In this article, we will be discussing the Gwinnett Tech Calendar for the Fall 2024 semester.

When does the Fall 2024 semester start?

The Fall 2024 semester at Gwinnett Technical College will start on August 19, 2024. Students are advised to check the college's website for the most up-to-date information regarding the start of the semester.

What are the important dates for the Fall 2024 semester?

Here are some important dates to keep in mind for the Fall 2024 semester at Gwinnett Tech: - August 19, 2024: First day of classes - September 6, 2024: Labor Day Holiday (college closed) - October 11-12, 2024: Fall Break (no classes) - November 11, 2024: Veterans Day Holiday (college closed) - November 25-29, 2024: Thanksgiving Break (college closed) - December 12-18, 2024: Final Exams

Academic Programs

Gwinnett Tech offers a wide range of academic programs, including: - Business and Computer Technology - Health Sciences - Industrial and Engineering Technology - Public Safety and Legal Studies - Arts and Sciences - Continuing Education

What are some popular programs at Gwinnett Tech?

Some popular programs at Gwinnett Tech include: - Nursing (Associate Degree) - Radiologic Technology (Associate Degree) - Dental Hygiene (Associate Degree) - Computer Programming (Associate Degree) - Culinary Arts (Diploma) - Automotive Technology (Diploma)

Student Life

Gwinnett Tech offers many opportunities for students to get involved on campus. Students can join clubs and organizations, attend events and workshops, and participate in community service projects.

What clubs and organizations are available?

There are many clubs and organizations available at Gwinnett Tech, including: - Student Government Association - Phi Theta Kappa (Honor Society) - SkillsUSA - National Technical Honor Society - Environmental Club - Christian Student Fellowship

What events and workshops are available?

Gwinnett Tech offers a variety of events and workshops throughout the year, including: - Career Fairs - Transfer Fairs - Leadership Workshops - Financial Literacy Workshops - Diversity and Inclusion Workshops - Health and Wellness Events

Admissions and Financial Aid

Gwinnett Tech has an open admissions policy, which means that all students who meet the basic requirements are welcome to apply. The college also offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans.

What are the basic requirements for admission?

To be admitted to Gwinnett Tech, students must: - Be a high school graduate or have a GED - Submit an application for admission - Submit official transcripts from all previously attended colleges - Take the ACCUPLACER or ACT/SAT placement test

What types of financial aid are available?

Gwinnett Tech offers several types of financial aid, including: - Scholarships (merit-based and need-based) - Grants (Federal Pell Grant, Georgia HOPE Grant, etc.) - Loans (Federal Direct Loans, PLUS Loans, etc.) - Work Study (part-time employment on campus)

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Fall 2024 semester at Gwinnett Technical College will start on August 19, 2024. Students should be aware of the important dates for the semester and take advantage of the many academic programs, student life opportunities, and financial aid options available at the college. If you have any questions or need additional information, please visit the Gwinnett Tech website or contact the college's admissions office.
