Table of Contents

What are Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Fredericksburg Trade Days is a famous outdoor market event that takes place four times a year in Fredericksburg, Texas. This event showcases a variety of merchandise, including antiques, collectibles, handcrafted items, and unique gifts.

When is Fredericksburg Trade Days in 2024?

The 2024 Fredericksburg Trade Days are scheduled to take place on the following dates:

  • March 15-17, 2024
  • June 21-23, 2024
  • September 20-22, 2024
  • November 29- December 1, 2024

What can you expect at Fredericksburg Trade Days?

At Fredericksburg Trade Days, you can expect to find a wide variety of vendors selling everything from handmade crafts to vintage clothing. You can also enjoy live music, local food, and a relaxed atmosphere. This event is perfect for shoppers looking for unique finds and a fun day out.

Planning Your Visit

What should you wear to Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Since this is an outdoor event, it's important to dress appropriately for the weather. Wear comfortable shoes since you'll be walking around a lot. Bring a hat, sunscreen, and sunglasses for sun protection. If you're visiting in the fall or winter, make sure to bring a jacket or sweater.

How much does it cost to attend Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Admission to Fredericksburg Trade Days is free, but you'll need to pay for parking. Parking costs $5 per vehicle.

What should you bring to Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Make sure to bring cash since some vendors might not accept credit cards. Bring a tote bag or two to carry your purchases. If you're visiting during the summer, bring a refillable water bottle to stay hydrated.

FAQs

Are pets allowed at Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Pets are welcome at Fredericksburg Trade Days, but they must be on a leash at all times.

Can you bring your own food to Fredericksburg Trade Days?

Yes, you can bring your own food and drinks to Fredericksburg Trade Days. However, there are also plenty of food vendors on site if you prefer to buy your meals.

Is Fredericksburg Trade Days wheelchair accessible?

Yes, Fredericksburg Trade Days is wheelchair accessible. However, keep in mind that the event is held outdoors on a large property, so there may be some uneven terrain.

Conclusion

If you're looking for a fun and unique shopping experience, Fredericksburg Trade Days is the perfect event for you. With a variety of vendors, live music, and delicious food, there's something for everyone. Make sure to plan your visit ahead of time, dress appropriately, and bring cash for purchases. We hope to see you at Fredericksburg Trade Days in 2024!
