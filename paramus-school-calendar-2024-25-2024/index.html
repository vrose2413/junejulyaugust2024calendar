Introduction

The Paramus School District has released its calendar for the 2024-25 academic year. This calendar includes important dates such as the first and last day of school, holidays, and teacher workdays. When Does School Start and End?

The first day of school for students in the Paramus School District will be on September 4, 2024. The last day of school will be on June 19, 2025.

What are the Holidays?

The calendar includes several holidays throughout the school year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, and Independence Day. Students will also have a winter break from December 23, 2024, to January 1, 2025.

What are Teacher Workdays?

There are several teacher workdays throughout the school year, which are days when teachers will be at school but students will not. These workdays are typically used for professional development or planning. The teacher workdays for the 2024-25 academic year are listed on the calendar.

Are There Any Early Release Days?

Yes, there will be early release days throughout the school year. These days are used for parent-teacher conferences or other school events. The early release dates for the 2024-25 academic year are listed on the calendar.

What About Snow Days?

In the event of inclement weather, the Paramus School District may close schools. The district has built in five snow days into the calendar. If more than five days are needed, the calendar will be adjusted accordingly.

What is the Graduation Date?

The graduation date for the Class of 2025 will be on June 20, 2025. The location and time of the graduation ceremony will be announced at a later date.

What is the Spring Break?

The spring break for the 2024-25 academic year will be from April 14-21, 2025. During this time, students will have a week off from school.

What is the Summer School Schedule?

The summer school schedule for the Paramus School District has not yet been released. It is typically announced in the spring.

The Paramus School District's calendar for the 2024-25 academic year includes important dates such as the first and last day of school, holidays, teacher workdays, and early release days.
