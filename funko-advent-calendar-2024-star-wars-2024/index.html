Introduction

Are you a fan of Star Wars and collectibles? Then you're in for a treat! Funko, the popular toy company, is releasing a Star Wars-themed advent calendar for 2024. This calendar is packed with 24 mini-figures, including fan-favorite characters from the franchise. What is a Funko Advent Calendar?

A Funko Advent Calendar is a special edition calendar that counts down the days until Christmas. Each day, you get to open a compartment and find a mini-figure inside. These mini-figures are highly collectible and often feature characters from popular franchises like Star Wars, Marvel, and DC Comics.

What can we expect from the Star Wars Edition?

The Star Wars Edition of the Funko Advent Calendar will feature 24 mini-figures from the Star Wars universe. These mini-figures will be exclusive to the calendar and cannot be found anywhere else. You can expect to see fan-favorites like Darth Vader, Luke Skywalker, and Princess Leia.

How much will it cost?

The price of the Funko Advent Calendar 2024: Star Wars Edition has not been announced yet. However, based on previous years' prices, we can estimate that it will cost around $50 to $60.

When will it be released?

The Funko Advent Calendar 2024: Star Wars Edition will be released in November 2024, just in time for the holiday season.

Where can I buy it?

The Funko Advent Calendar 2024: Star Wars Edition will be available at most major retailers, including Amazon, Walmart, and Target. You can also purchase it directly from the Funko website.

Why should I buy it?

If you're a Star Wars fan and love collecting mini-figures, then the Funko Advent Calendar 2024: Star Wars Edition is a must-buy. Not only do you get 24 exclusive mini-figures, but you also get to count down the days until Christmas in a fun and unique way.

Q: Can I buy the mini-figures separately?

The Funko Advent Calendar 2024: Star Wars Edition is an exciting addition to any Star Wars fan's collection. With 24 exclusive mini-figures and a fun countdown to Christmas, it's a must-buy for collectors and fans alike.
