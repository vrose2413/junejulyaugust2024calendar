Introduction

Are you ready for the festive season? If you're a Marvel fan, then you'll love the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024. This advent calendar features 24 unique Marvel-themed Funko Pop! figures, one for every day leading up to Christmas. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024.

What is a Funko Pop! Figure?

Funko Pop! figures are collectible vinyl toys that depict characters from various pop culture franchises. These figures are known for their oversized heads and cute, chibi-style designs. Funko Pop! figures have become incredibly popular among collectors and fans alike.

What's Included in the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024?

The Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 includes 24 unique Marvel-themed Funko Pop! figures. The figures are packaged in a festive advent calendar box, with each day featuring a different figure. The figures include characters from the Marvel Cinematic Universe, as well as characters from the comics.

How Much Does the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 Cost?

The Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 is expected to cost around $70. This is in line with previous Funko Pop! advent calendars, which have also been priced around $70.

Where Can I Buy the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024?

The Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 will be available at various retailers, including Amazon, Walmart, and Target. It's recommended that you pre-order the calendar to ensure that you get one, as they tend to sell out quickly.

When Will the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 Be Released?

The Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 is expected to be released in October 2024, just in time for the holiday season.

Why Should You Buy the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024?

If you're a Marvel fan or a Funko Pop! collector, then the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 is a must-have. This advent calendar is a fun and unique way to count down to Christmas, and it features 24 exclusive Marvel-themed Funko Pop! figures that you won't be able to get anywhere else.

What Are Some of the Characters Included in the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024?

Some of the characters included in the Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 are Iron Man, Captain America, Spider-Man, and Thor. There are also some lesser-known characters from the comics, such as Squirrel Girl and Moon Knight.

How Can You Display Your Marvel Funko Pop! Figures?

There are many ways to display your Marvel Funko Pop! figures. You can use a display case, a shelf, or even a pegboard. Some collectors like to arrange their figures by franchise, while others prefer to display them by character.

What Are Some Tips for Collecting Funko Pop! Figures?

If you're new to collecting Funko Pop! figures, here are some tips to get you started: - Focus on a specific franchise or character to start with - Be mindful of the value of certain figures - Keep your figures in good condition by storing them properly - Don't be afraid to trade or sell figures to complete your collection

The Marvel Funko Advent Calendar 2024 is a fun and unique way to celebrate the festive season for Marvel fans and Funko Pop! collectors. With 24 exclusive Marvel-themed Funko Pop! figures, this advent calendar is sure to bring joy to any fan. Pre-order yours now to ensure that you don't miss out!
