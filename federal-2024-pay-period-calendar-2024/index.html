Introduction

What is a Pay Period Calendar?

A pay period calendar is a schedule that shows when federal employees will receive their paychecks. It outlines the start and end dates of each pay period and the corresponding pay dates. Federal employees can use this calendar to plan their finances and budget effectively.

2024 Pay Period Calendar

The 2024 pay period calendar for federal employees consists of 26 pay periods. The first pay period starts on January 6, 2024, and ends on January 19, 2024. The last pay period starts on December 22, 2024, and ends on January 4, 2025.

Pay Periods and Holidays

Most pay periods consist of 80 hours of work, and federal employees are paid on a biweekly basis. However, some pay periods may have an extra day due to holidays, which can affect the pay date. For example, if a holiday falls on a Monday, the pay date may be moved to Friday of the previous week.

FAQs

Q: How do I know my pay period?

A: Your pay period will depend on your agency and position. You can check with your agency's human resources department or refer to the pay period calendar for your specific agency.

Q: Can the pay period calendar change?

A: Yes, the pay period calendar can change due to holidays or other factors. It is important to keep an eye on any updates or changes to the calendar.

Q: How do I calculate my pay?

A: To calculate your pay, you can use the hourly rate or salary provided in your employment contract and multiply it by the number of hours worked in each pay period. You can also refer to the pay tables provided by the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) for your specific position and location.

Conclusion

The federal 2024 pay period calendar is an essential tool for federal employees to plan their finances and budget effectively. By understanding the calendar and how it works, employees can ensure that they receive their paychecks on time and avoid any financial difficulties.
