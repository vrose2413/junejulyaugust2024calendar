Mundelein High School Calendar 2024-25: Everything You Need To Know

Introduction

As we approach the year 2024, many parents and students are already curious about the upcoming academic calendar for Mundelein High School. Planning ahead is crucial, especially when it comes to school events, holidays, and breaks. In this article, we will provide you with all the necessary information regarding the Mundelein High School Calendar for 2024-25.

When Does the School Year Begin?

The Mundelein High School Calendar for 2024-25 will begin on August 19, 2024. This is the first day of school for all students, including freshmen, sophomores, juniors, and seniors.

What Are the Scheduled Holidays and Breaks?

There will be several holidays and breaks during the 2024-25 academic year. These include Labor Day on September 2, 2024, Thanksgiving Break from November 27-29, 2024, Winter Break from December 23, 2024 to January 3, 2025, and Spring Break from March 23-27, 2025.

What Are the Scheduled Half-Days?

There will be several half-days scheduled throughout the academic year. These include October 4, 2019, January 17, 2020, March 6, 2020, and May 29, 2020.

When Is the Last Day of School?

The last day of school for Mundelein High School students will be on May 29, 2025. This is the last day of the academic year for all students, including seniors.

What Are the Important Dates to Remember?

There are several important dates to remember during the 2024-25 academic year. These include Homecoming Week from September 16-20, 2024, Parent-Teacher Conferences from October 24-25, 2024, and Prom on April 26, 2025.

What Are the Graduation Dates?

The graduation dates for Mundelein High School are scheduled for May 24, 2025.

What Are the Bell Schedules?

The bell schedules for Mundelein High School will be announced closer to the start of the academic year. Typically, there are four different bell schedules for each day of the week.

What Are the Extracurricular Activities?

Mundelein High School offers a variety of extracurricular activities for students to participate in. These include sports teams, music and drama productions, clubs, and volunteer opportunities.

What Are the School Policies?

Mundelein High School has several policies in place to ensure the safety and well-being of all students. These policies include dress code, attendance, academic honesty, and bullying prevention.

Conclusion

Planning ahead for the Mundelein High School Calendar for 2024-25 is essential for all parents and students. With this information, you can prepare for holidays, breaks, important dates, bell schedules, extracurricular activities, and school policies. We hope you found this article helpful and informative. Good luck with the upcoming academic year!
