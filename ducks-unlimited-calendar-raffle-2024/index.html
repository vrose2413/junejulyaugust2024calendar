Introduction

As we enter the year 2023, many hunting enthusiasts are already looking forward to the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle in 2024. This annual event is highly anticipated by hunters and conservationists alike, as it offers a chance to win amazing prizes while supporting the important work of Ducks Unlimited. In this article, we will take a closer look at what the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle is all about, how it works, and what you can expect in 2024.

What is Ducks Unlimited?

Ducks Unlimited is a non-profit organization dedicated to the conservation of wetlands and associated habitats for the benefit of North America's waterfowl, other wildlife, and people. Founded in 1937, the organization has grown to become the world's largest and most effective private waterfowl and wetlands conservation organization.

What is the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle?

The Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle is an annual fundraising event that supports the conservation work of Ducks Unlimited. The raffle offers participants the chance to win amazing prizes, including guns, hunting gear, and other outdoor equipment. Each year, the raffle features a different set of prizes, and all proceeds from ticket sales go directly towards wetlands conservation.

How the Raffle Works

When Does the Raffle Take Place?

The Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle typically runs from January 1st to December 31st of each year. Each day, a winning ticket is drawn, and the winner receives the prize associated with that day.

How Do I Enter the Raffle?

To enter the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle, you must purchase a raffle ticket. Tickets can be purchased from a Ducks Unlimited volunteer, online, or by phone. Each ticket costs $100, and includes entry into the raffle for the entire year.

What Are the Prizes?

The prizes for the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle vary from year to year, but typically include guns, hunting gear, and other outdoor equipment. In some cases, winners may have the option to choose a cash prize instead of the designated prize.

How Are Winners Notified?

Winners of the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle are notified by phone or email within 24 hours of the drawing. Winners are also posted on the Ducks Unlimited website and social media channels.

What to Expect in 2024

What Prizes Will be Available?

The prizes for the 2024 Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle have not yet been announced. However, past raffles have included a wide range of prizes, such as shotguns, rifles, hunting trips, and outdoor gear.

How Can I Get Involved?

If you're interested in supporting the work of Ducks Unlimited and potentially winning some amazing prizes, there are several ways to get involved. You can purchase a raffle ticket, become a Ducks Unlimited member, or volunteer with your local chapter.

Questions and Answers

Q: What Happens to the Proceeds from the Raffle?

A: All proceeds from the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle go towards wetlands conservation. This includes habitat restoration, waterfowl research, and education and outreach programs.

Q: Can Anyone Enter the Raffle?

A: Yes, anyone can enter the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle as long as they are 18 years of age or older and reside in the United States.

Q: How Many Tickets Are Sold Each Year?

A: The number of tickets sold for the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle varies from year to year, but typically ranges from 10,000 to 20,000 tickets.

Q: Is the Raffle Legal?

A: Yes, the Ducks Unlimited Calendar Raffle is legal and fully compliant with all federal and state laws and regulations governing raffles and charitable gaming.
