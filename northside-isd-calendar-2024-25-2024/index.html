Introduction

Northside Independent School District (NISD) is the fourth-largest school district in Texas, serving over 100,000 students across 120 campuses. As a parent or student in NISD, it's important to stay informed about the district's academic calendar to plan ahead and make the most of the school year.

When is the NISD Calendar Released?

The NISD calendar for 2024-25 was released on the district's website in May 2023. This allows families to plan ahead for the upcoming school year, including vacations and other important events.

What are the Key Dates in the NISD Calendar 2024-25?

The first day of school in NISD for the 2024-25 academic year is August 26, 2024, and the last day of school is June 6, 2025. There will be a total of 176 instructional days, including holidays and breaks.

What Holidays and Breaks are Included in the NISD Calendar 2024-25?

The NISD calendar includes several holidays and breaks throughout the academic year, including Labor Day, Thanksgiving break, winter break, spring break, and Memorial Day. Additionally, there are early release days and teacher workdays throughout the year.

What are the Graduation Dates for NISD in 2024-25?

The graduation dates for NISD high schools in 2024-25 will be announced closer to the end of the school year. Typically, graduation ceremonies take place in late May or early June.

How Can I Stay Up-to-Date on NISD Calendar Changes?

It's important to note that the NISD calendar is subject to change due to inclement weather or other unforeseen circumstances. To stay up-to-date on calendar changes, parents and students can check the district's website or social media pages, sign up for text or email alerts, or contact their school directly.

What Should I Do if My Child Needs to Miss School?

If your child needs to miss school due to illness or other reasons, it's important to contact their school as soon as possible to report the absence. In some cases, students may be able to complete assignments and coursework online or receive makeup work upon their return to school.

What Resources are Available to Help My Child Succeed in NISD?

NISD offers a variety of resources and support systems to help students succeed academically and personally. This includes tutoring services, counseling services, extracurricular activities, and more. Parents and students can contact their school or visit the district's website for more information.

How Can I Get Involved in NISD?

NISD encourages parents and community members to get involved in their local schools through volunteer opportunities, school events, and other initiatives. For more information on getting involved in NISD, parents and community members can contact their local school or visit the district's website.

What Should I Do if I Have Questions or Concerns about NISD?

If you have questions or concerns about NISD, there are several resources available to you. Parents and students can contact their local school or district office, attend school board meetings, or reach out to the district's customer service team for assistance.

Conclusion

Staying informed about the NISD calendar and other important district information is key to helping your child succeed in school. By planning ahead and utilizing the resources available to you, you can ensure that your child has a successful and rewarding academic year.
