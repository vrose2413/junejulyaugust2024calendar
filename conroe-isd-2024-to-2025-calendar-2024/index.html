Introduction

As the year 2023 comes to a close, many students, parents, and teachers are already looking ahead to the next academic year. One of the most important things to keep in mind is the school calendar. In this article, we will discuss the Conroe Independent School District's (CISD) 2024 to 2025 calendar.

What is Conroe ISD?

Conroe ISD is a school district located in Montgomery County, Texas. It serves over 63,000 students and has 60 campuses, including 47 elementary schools, 13 middle schools, and 10 high schools. The district is known for its high academic standards and extracurricular activities.

When does the 2024-2025 school year start and end?

The first day of school for the 2024-2025 academic year is August 19, 2024, and the last day of school is May 29, 2025. The district follows a traditional school year calendar, with holidays and breaks throughout.

Important Dates

What are the major holidays during the school year?

The CISD 2024-2025 calendar includes the following major holidays: - Labor Day: September 2, 2024 - Thanksgiving Break: November 25-29, 2024 - Winter Break: December 23, 2024-January 6, 2025 - Martin Luther King Jr. Day: January 20, 2025 - Spring Break: March 9-15, 2025 - Memorial Day: May 26, 2025

Are there any early release or late start days?

Yes, there are several early release and late start days throughout the school year. These days are typically used for staff development or teacher workdays. Parents and students should check the CISD calendar for specific dates and times.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if there is inclement weather?

If there is inclement weather, the district may cancel or delay classes. In the event of a cancellation or delay, the district will notify parents and students through its website, social media, and local news outlets.

What is the attendance policy?

The CISD attendance policy requires students to attend school for at least 90% of the school year. Parents and students should review the district's policy for more information on excused and unexcused absences.

Can students participate in extracurricular activities?

Yes, students in CISD are encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and clubs. However, students must maintain a certain GPA and attendance record to remain eligible.

Conclusion

In summary, the Conroe ISD 2024-2025 calendar includes important dates such as holidays, early release and late start days, and the first and last day of school. Parents and students should review the calendar carefully and plan accordingly for the upcoming school year. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to contact your child's school or the district office.
