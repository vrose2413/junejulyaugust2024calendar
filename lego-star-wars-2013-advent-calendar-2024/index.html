Introduction

The Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar has been a fan-favorite since its launch in 2011. Each year, the calendar features a new set of mini-builds and figures, counting down to Christmas. In 2013, Lego released its Star Wars Advent Calendar, and it has been one of the most sought-after calendars for Star Wars fans. In this article, we will review the Lego Star Wars 2013 Advent Calendar and see how it fares in 2024.

What's in the Box?

The 2013 Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar comes with 24 mini-builds and figures. Each day, you can open a new door to reveal a surprise. The mini-builds are all Star Wars-themed and include iconic characters, ships, and vehicles from the movies. Some of the mini-builds include a Millennium Falcon, an AT-AT, and a Death Star. The figures include popular characters like Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, and Princess Leia.

Building Experience

The building experience for the Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar is quite enjoyable. Each mini-build is relatively easy to put together and takes around 10-15 minutes to complete. The instructions are clear and easy to follow, making it a great activity to do with your family or friends during the holiday season.

Playability

The mini-builds and figures in the Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar are not only great for display but also for play. The mini-builds are small enough to be used as part of a larger Star Wars playset, and the figures can be used in action scenes or for display. The set also comes with a fold-out playmat, which is perfect for creating your own Star Wars scenes.

Price and Availability

The Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar is a limited edition set and is only available during the holiday season. In 2013, the set retailed for $39.99. However, due to its popularity, the set can now be quite expensive, with some resellers selling it for over $100.

Q&A

Q: Is the Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar worth the price?

A: It depends on how much you love Star Wars and Lego. The set is quite expensive, but it comes with 24 mini-builds and figures, making it a great value for money. If you're a fan of Star Wars and Lego, then it's definitely worth it.

Q: Can I still buy the Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar?

A: As mentioned earlier, the set is a limited edition and is only available during the holiday season. However, you may be able to find it on reseller sites like eBay or Amazon. Just be prepared to pay a premium.

Q: Can the mini-builds be used in other Lego sets?

A: Yes, the mini-builds are compatible with other Lego sets. They are small enough to be used as part of a larger Star Wars playset or even as standalone models.

Conclusion

The Lego Star Wars 2013 Advent Calendar is a great set for Star Wars and Lego fans. The mini-builds and figures are well-designed and offer great value for money. The building experience is enjoyable, and the set is perfect for display or play. However, due to its limited availability, the set can be quite expensive. If you can find it at a reasonable price, then it's definitely worth adding to your collection.
