Introduction

Google Calendar is one of the most popular digital calendars that people use to keep track of their schedules and events. One of the key features of the Google Calendar is the ability to show holidays from different countries. However, some users may find that the calendar does not show holidays for the year 2024. Why Google Calendar May Not be Showing Holidays for 2024

There could be several reasons why Google Calendar is not showing holidays for the year 2024. One possible reason could be that the holidays have not been added to the calendar yet. Google usually adds holidays for the upcoming year a few weeks before the end of the current year. Therefore, it is possible that the holidays for 2024 have not been added to the calendar yet.

Another possible reason could be that the user has not selected the correct country or region in their calendar settings. Google Calendar allows users to choose their country or region, and the calendar will show holidays for that specific location. If the user has not selected the correct country or region, then Google Calendar may not show holidays for 2024.

What to Do if Google Calendar is Not Showing Holidays for 2024

If Google Calendar is not showing holidays for 2024, there are a few things that users can do to fix the issue. Here are some possible solutions:

Check for Updates

The first thing to do is to check if there are any updates available for Google Calendar. Users can do this by going to the Google Play Store or the App Store and checking for updates. If there is an update available, users should download and install it. The update may include the holidays for 2024.

Check Calendar Settings

Users should also check their calendar settings to ensure that they have selected the correct country or region. To do this, users should open Google Calendar, click on the three horizontal lines in the top left corner, and select "Settings". From there, users should select "General" and then "Country/Region". Users should make sure that the correct country or region is selected.

Add Holidays Manually

If the holidays for 2024 are still not showing up, users can add them manually to their calendar. To do this, users should open Google Calendar, click on the plus sign in the bottom right corner, and select "Event". From there, users should enter the name of the holiday, date, and any other relevant information. Users can also select the appropriate color for the holiday to make it stand out on their calendar.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Google Calendar not showing holidays for 2024 can be a frustrating issue for users. However, there are several possible solutions that users can try to fix the problem. Checking for updates, checking calendar settings, and adding holidays manually are some possible solutions that users can try. By following these steps, users can ensure that their calendar is up-to-date and accurate.
