When Does the School Year Start and End?

The first day of school for the 2024-2025 academic year in Dekalb County is August 5th, 2024. The last day of school is May 23rd, 2025. This is a slightly shorter school year than in previous years, due to changes in state regulations and holidays.

What Are the Major Holidays?

As always, the school calendar includes major holidays like Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Spring Break. In 2024, Thanksgiving Break will run from November 25th to 29th. Winter Break will run from December 23rd to January 3rd. Spring Break will be from April 1st to 5th.

What About Teacher Workdays and Staff Development Days?

In addition to major holidays, the Dekalb County Public Schools Calendar includes several teacher workdays and staff development days. These days are important for teachers and staff to plan curriculum, attend training sessions, and collaborate with colleagues. In 2024, there will be a total of 11 teacher workdays and staff development days spread throughout the school year.

What Are the Testing Dates?

Standardized testing is an important part of the school year, and the Dekalb County Public Schools Calendar includes dates for these tests. In 2024, the Georgia Milestones Assessment System (GMAS) will be administered to students in grades 3-8 from April 15th to April 26th. High school students will take end-of-course exams in various subjects throughout the month of May.

What About Graduation Dates?

Graduation is a major milestone for high school seniors, and the Dekalb County Public Schools Calendar includes dates for graduation ceremonies. In 2024, high school graduation ceremonies will be held from May 20th to May 23rd.

What Are Some Important Dates to Remember?

While the school calendar includes all major holidays, testing dates, and graduation ceremonies, there are a few other important dates to remember. One of these is Open House, which will be held on August 1st, 2024. This is an opportunity for parents and students to meet their teachers, tour the school, and learn about expectations for the upcoming year. Another important date is Parent-Teacher Conferences, which will be held on October 17th and March 20th.
