Best Calendar Apps For College Students 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Jul 31, 2022 · 3 min read

Q: Can I use these calendar apps on my laptop?

Table of Contents

Introduction:

As the world becomes more digitalized, the use of traditional paper calendars is decreasing. Nowadays, people prefer to use digital calendars on their smartphones or laptops. As a college student, you have a lot of work to do, and it can be hard to keep track of everything. That's why we have compiled a list of the best calendar apps for college students in 2024.

What are the benefits of using calendar apps?

Calendar apps are very beneficial for college students. They help you keep track of your class schedules, homework deadlines, and extracurricular activities. With a calendar app, you can easily manage your time and ensure that you don't miss any important deadlines.

What are the best calendar apps for college students in 2024?

There are many calendar apps available in the market, but not all of them are suitable for college students. After extensive research, we have shortlisted the following apps:

1. Google Calendar:

Google Calendar is a free app that is available on both Android and iOS devices. It is a very popular app among college students because of its user-friendly interface and easy integration with other Google apps. You can easily schedule your classes, assignments, and extracurricular activities in Google Calendar.

2. Any.do:

Any.do is another great calendar app that is available on both Android and iOS devices. It has a simple and intuitive interface that allows you to schedule your tasks quickly. You can also create to-do lists and set reminders for important deadlines.

3. Fantastical:

Fantastical is a paid app that is available only on iOS devices. It is a very powerful app that allows you to schedule your tasks with natural language. You can also integrate it with other apps like Google Maps and Uber to easily plan your day.

4. Trello:

Trello is a free app that is available on both Android and iOS devices. It is a project management app that allows you to organize your tasks on a board. You can easily move tasks from one list to another and set deadlines for each task.

5. Wunderlist:

Wunderlist is a free app that is available on both Android and iOS devices. It is a to-do list app that allows you to create lists and set reminders for each task. You can also share your lists with your classmates to collaborate on group projects.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, these five calendar apps are the best options for college students in 2024. They are all user-friendly, easy to use, and offer unique features to help you manage your time effectively. By using one of these apps, you can ensure that you stay on top of your coursework and extracurricular activities. So, download one of these apps today and start managing your time like a pro!

Question and Answer:

Q: Can I use these calendar apps on my laptop?

A: Yes, all of these calendar apps have web versions that you can access on your laptop.

Q: Are these calendar apps free?

A: Most of these apps have free versions, but some of them have paid versions with more advanced features.

Q: Can I share my calendar with my classmates?

A: Yes, most of these apps allow you to share your calendar with others. This can be very helpful when working on group projects.

Q: Which app should I choose?

A: It depends on your personal preferences and needs. If you want a simple app, Any.do or Wunderlist might be the best options. If you want a powerful app with natural language input, Fantastical might be the best choice. However, if you use other Google apps, then Google Calendar might be the most convenient option.
