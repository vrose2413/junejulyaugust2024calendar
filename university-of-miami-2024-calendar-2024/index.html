The University of Miami has released its academic calendar for the year 2024. This calendar includes important dates such as the start and end of each semester, holidays, and exam periods. In this article, we will go through the calendar in detail and answer some common questions about it.

Semesters

The 2024 academic year at the University of Miami is divided into two semesters. The first semester, also known as the fall semester, begins on August 26, 2024, and ends on December 11, 2024. The second semester, also known as the spring semester, begins on January 13, 2025, and ends on May 8, 2025.

Each semester is further divided into two sessions - the first half of the semester and the second half of the semester. The first half of the fall semester runs from August 26 to October 15, while the second half of the fall semester runs from October 18 to December 11. The first half of the spring semester runs from January 13 to March 4, while the second half of the spring semester runs from March 7 to May 8.

Question: When does the fall semester start?

The fall semester starts on August 26, 2024.

Question: When does the spring semester end?

The spring semester ends on May 8, 2025.

Holidays

The University of Miami recognizes several holidays throughout the academic year. These holidays include Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, and Memorial Day, among others. Classes are not held on these holidays, and the university is closed.

Question: Is the university closed on Memorial Day?

Yes, the university is closed on Memorial Day.

Exam Periods

The exam periods for the fall and spring semesters are held at the end of each semester. The fall exam period runs from December 13 to December 20, while the spring exam period runs from May 10 to May 17. During the exam period, students are required to attend exams for each of their classes.

Question: When is the fall exam period?

The fall exam period runs from December 13 to December 20.

Question: What happens during the exam period?

During the exam period, students are required to attend exams for each of their classes.

The University of Miami 2024 calendar provides students, faculty, and staff with important dates for the academic year.
