How To Add Google Calendar Widget To Iphone 2024

Written by Mable Stanley Nov 12, 2022 · 3 min read

Do you want to keep track of your schedule without opening the Google Calendar app on your iPhone? One way to do this is by adding a Google Calendar widget to your iPhone. In this article, we will show you how to do it step by step.

Do you want to keep track of your schedule without opening the Google Calendar app on your iPhone? One way to do this is by adding a Google Calendar widget to your iPhone. In this article, we will show you how to do it step by step.

Step 1: Check your iPhone's compatibility

Before you start, make sure that your iPhone is running on iOS 14 or later. You can check this by going to Settings > General > About > Software Version. If you are not on iOS 14 or later, you need to update your iPhone first.

Step 2: Add the Google Calendar app

If you haven't already, download and install the Google Calendar app from the App Store. Once installed, open the app and sign in to your Google account.

Step 3: Add the Google Calendar widget to your home screen

Press and hold the home screen on your iPhone until the apps start to jiggle. Tap the "+" icon in the top left corner to add a new widget. Scroll down until you find the Google Calendar widget and select the size you want to use.

Step 4: Configure the widget settings

After adding the widget, you can customize its settings. Press and hold the widget until the "Edit Widget" option appears. Tap on it to access the widget settings. Here, you can choose which calendar to display, the date range, and the event title and time format.

Step 5: Enjoy your new Google Calendar widget

Once you have configured the widget to your liking, you can exit the settings and enjoy your new Google Calendar widget on your iPhone home screen. Now you can easily see your upcoming events without opening the Google Calendar app.

FAQs

Q: Can I add multiple Google Calendar widgets to my iPhone home screen?

A: Yes, you can add multiple Google Calendar widgets to your iPhone home screen. Simply repeat the steps above to add another widget of a different size or with different settings.

Q: Can I change the color of the Google Calendar widget?

A: Unfortunately, you cannot change the color of the Google Calendar widget on your iPhone home screen. It will always be the same color as the Google Calendar app icon.

Q: Can I delete the Google Calendar widget from my iPhone home screen?

A: Yes, you can delete the Google Calendar widget from your iPhone home screen. Press and hold the widget until the "Remove Widget" option appears. Tap on it to remove the widget from your home screen.

Conclusion

Adding a Google Calendar widget to your iPhone home screen is a simple and effective way to keep track of your schedule. By following the steps above, you can easily set up and customize your Google Calendar widget to your liking. Enjoy the convenience of seeing your upcoming events at a glance!
