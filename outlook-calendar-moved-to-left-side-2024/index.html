Outlook Calendar Moved To Left Side 2024: Everything You Need To Know

Written by Mable Stanley Feb 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

If you're an avid Outlook user, you might have noticed that the calendar has been moved to the left side for the year 2024. This change has been made to enhance user experience and make it easier for users to navigate through the calendar. In this article, we will discuss what this change means and how it affects you as an Outlook user.

What Does This Change Mean?

The Outlook calendar has been moved to the left side for the year 2024. This means that instead of the calendar being located on the right side of your screen, it will now be located on the left side. This change has been made to enhance user experience and make it easier for users to navigate through the calendar.

Why Was This Change Made?

This change was made to improve user experience. By moving the calendar to the left side, users can easily view upcoming events and appointments without having to scroll through the entire calendar. This change also makes it easier for users to navigate through the calendar and find the information they need quickly.

How Does This Change Affect You?

As an Outlook user, this change should not have a significant impact on your experience. You will still be able to access your calendar, view upcoming events, and create new appointments as you normally would. The only difference is that the calendar will now be located on the left side of your screen instead of the right.

Do You Need to Make Any Changes?

No, you do not need to make any changes to your Outlook account. This change has been made on the server-side, so it will automatically apply to all users. However, if you prefer the old layout, you can switch back to it by going to the “View” tab and selecting “Calendar” on the right side of your screen.

Benefits of the New Layout

The new layout has several benefits. First, it makes it easier for users to view upcoming events and appointments without having to scroll through the entire calendar. Second, it makes it easier for users to navigate through the calendar and find the information they need quickly. Third, it provides a more modern and streamlined user interface.

What Are Some Tips for Using the New Layout?

Here are some tips for using the new layout:
  • Use the left panel to view your calendar by day, week, or month.
  • Use the middle panel to view your appointments and events.
  • Use the right panel to view your to-do list and upcoming tasks.
  • Customize the layout by dragging and dropping panels to the desired location.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the Outlook calendar has been moved to the left side for the year 2024 to enhance user experience. This change should not have a significant impact on your experience as an Outlook user. However, if you prefer the old layout, you can switch back to it by going to the “View” tab and selecting “Calendar” on the right side of your screen. Overall, the new layout provides several benefits and a more modern user interface for users.
