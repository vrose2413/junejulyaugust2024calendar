Los Angeles County School Calendar 2024

Introduction

It's that time of the year again when parents and students start preparing for the upcoming school year. One of the most important things to consider is the school calendar. In this article, we will discuss the Los Angeles County school calendar for the year 2024.

When does the school year begin?

The first day of school for Los Angeles County schools in 2024 will be on Monday, August 19th. This is the same date as the previous year and is subject to change due to unforeseen circumstances.

When does the school year end?

The last day of school for Los Angeles County schools in 2024 will be on Friday, June 7th. This is also the same date as the previous year and is subject to change due to unforeseen circumstances.

What are the holidays during the school year?

There are several holidays during the school year that students and teachers can look forward to. These holidays include Labor Day, Thanksgiving Break, Winter Break, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, President's Day, Spring Break, and Memorial Day.

What are the school breaks?

Aside from the holidays mentioned above, there are also school breaks that students can enjoy. These breaks include Thanksgiving Break, Winter Break, and Spring Break. Thanksgiving Break will be from November 25th to November 29th, Winter Break will be from December 23rd to January 10th, and Spring Break will be from April 1st to April 5th.

What are the teacher workdays?

There are several teacher workdays throughout the school year when students do not have to attend school. These days are usually used for teacher training and planning. The teacher workdays for the 2024 school year have not been announced yet.

What about snow days?

Los Angeles County generally does not experience snow, so snow days are not a concern. However, in the event of severe weather, the school district may cancel classes to ensure the safety of students and staff.

Conclusion

The Los Angeles County school calendar for the year 2024 has been announced, and it's time to start preparing for the upcoming school year. Make sure to mark your calendars for the first and last day of school, as well as the holidays and breaks throughout the year. Stay safe and have a great school year!
