Introduction

With the new year comes the new calendar on your iPhone, but what if you don't want to see the holidays cluttering up your schedule? In this article, we'll show you how to remove holidays from your iPhone calendar in 2024.

Step 1: Open your Calendar App

First, open the calendar app on your iPhone. This can be done by tapping the icon on your home screen or by searching for it using the search feature.

Step 2: Tap on Calendars

Once you're in the calendar app, tap on "Calendars" at the bottom of the screen. This will bring up a list of all the calendars that are currently synced to your device.

Step 3: Uncheck the Holiday Calendar

Scroll down until you see the "Holiday" calendar for the year 2024. To remove this calendar from your view, simply tap the circle to the left of the calendar name to uncheck it. This will remove all of the holidays from your calendar view.

Step 4: Repeat for Other Calendars

If you have other holiday calendars synced to your device, repeat the same process for each calendar to remove them from your view. This will ensure that your calendar is free from any unnecessary clutter.

Question and Answer

Q: Will removing the holiday calendar delete any events I have planned on those days?

A: No, removing the holiday calendar will only remove the holiday events from your view. Any events that you have planned on those days will still be visible on your calendar.

Q: Can I restore the holiday calendar if I change my mind?

A: Yes, you can restore the holiday calendar at any time by going back to the "Calendars" section and checking the circle to the left of the calendar name.

Q: Will removing the holiday calendar affect my ability to add new events?

A: No, removing the holiday calendar will not affect your ability to add new events to your calendar.

Conclusion

Removing the holiday calendar from your iPhone calendar in 2024 is a quick and easy process that will help to keep your schedule organized. By following the simple steps outlined in this article, you can enjoy a clutter-free calendar that is tailored to your needs.
