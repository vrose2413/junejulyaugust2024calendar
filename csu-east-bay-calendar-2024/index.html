Introduction

As we approach the year 2024, it's important for students and faculty of California State University East Bay to know what to expect in terms of the academic calendar. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about the CSU East Bay Calendar for 2024.

When Does the Academic Year Begin?

The CSU East Bay academic year for 2024 begins on Monday, August 26th. This marks the official start of fall semester, which runs until December 13th. The spring semester then begins on January 21st, 2025, and finishes on May 16th.

What About Winter and Summer Sessions?

Winter session at CSU East Bay runs from December 23rd, 2024 to January 17th, 2025. Summer session, on the other hand, is split into two parts: session A and session B. Session A runs from May 27th to July 3rd, while session B runs from July 8th to August 16th.

What Are the Important Dates to Remember?

There are several important dates to keep in mind when it comes to the CSU East Bay Calendar 2024. These include:

  • August 26th, 2024: Fall semester begins
  • September 2nd, 2024: Labor Day holiday
  • November 11th, 2024: Veterans Day holiday
  • November 28th-29th, 2024: Thanksgiving holiday
  • December 13th, 2024: Fall semester ends
  • December 23rd, 2024 - January 17th, 2025: Winter session
  • January 20th, 2025: Martin Luther King Jr. Day holiday
  • January 21st, 2025: Spring semester begins
  • February 17th, 2025: Presidents' Day holiday
  • March 31st, 2025: Cesar Chavez Day holiday
  • May 16th, 2025: Spring semester ends
  • May 27th - July 3rd, 2025: Summer session A
  • July 4th, 2025: Independence Day holiday
  • July 8th - August 16th, 2025: Summer session B

What About Final Exams?

Final exams for fall semester 2024 will take place from December 16th to December 20th. Final exams for spring semester 2025 are scheduled for May 19th to May 23rd. Note that these dates are subject to change, so be sure to check with your professors and the CSU East Bay website for any updates.

Conclusion

Now that you know everything you need to know about the CSU East Bay Calendar for 2024, you can start planning your academic year accordingly. Remember to mark important dates on your calendar and to stay informed about any changes or updates. Good luck with your studies!
