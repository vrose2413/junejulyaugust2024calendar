Introduction

In this article, we'll take a closer look at what the advent calendar has to offer and answer some of your burning questions.

What's Inside the Advent Calendar?

The advent calendar is designed to make the holiday countdown extra special. It contains 24 doors, each revealing a dazzling surprise from Tiffany & Co. Some of the items you can expect to find inside include:

  • Diamond earrings
  • Silver bracelets
  • Necklaces
  • Watches
  • Fragrances

Each item is carefully selected to ensure that it's both luxurious and practical, making the advent calendar the perfect gift for anyone who loves all things Tiffany & Co.

How Much Does the Advent Calendar Cost?

The price of the advent calendar varies depending on the year and the items included. In 2023, the advent calendar was priced at $15,000. While the price may seem steep, keep in mind that each item is individually wrapped and comes in its own signature blue box, making it a truly special experience.

How Can I Get My Hands on the Advent Calendar?

The advent calendar is available for purchase exclusively at Tiffany & Co. stores. It's important to note that availability is limited, so if you want to get your hands on one, you'll need to act fast. Sign up for the Tiffany & Co. mailing list to be notified when the advent calendar becomes available for purchase.

Is the Advent Calendar Worth the Investment?

Whether or not the advent calendar is worth the investment ultimately depends on your personal preferences and budget. If you're a fan of Tiffany & Co. and love the idea of unwrapping a new luxury item every day leading up to the holidays, then the advent calendar is definitely worth considering. However, if you're looking for a more budget-friendly option, you may want to consider other gift ideas.

No, items from the advent calendar cannot be returned or exchanged. All sales are final.

Are the Items in the Advent Calendar Authentic?

Yes, all items in the advent calendar are authentic Tiffany & Co. products.

Can I Purchase the Advent Calendar Online?

No, the advent calendar is only available for purchase in Tiffany & Co. stores.

What Happens If I Miss Out on the Advent Calendar?

If you miss out on purchasing the advent calendar, you may be able to find it on resale websites such as eBay. However, keep in mind that prices may be significantly higher than the original retail price.

When Will the Advent Calendar Be Available for Purchase?

The release date for the advent calendar varies from year to year. Be sure to sign up for the Tiffany & Co. mailing list to be notified when the advent calendar becomes available for purchase.

Conclusion

The Tiffany & Co. advent calendar is a luxurious and exciting way to countdown to the holidays. While it may be a splurge, it's a gift that's sure to make anyone feel special. If you're a fan of Tiffany & Co. and want to make the holiday season extra special, the advent calendar is definitely worth considering.
