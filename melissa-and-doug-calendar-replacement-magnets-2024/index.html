Why Do You Need Replacement Magnets?

If you have a Melissa and Doug magnetic calendar, you may find that some of the magnets get lost or damaged over time. Using Melissa and Doug calendar replacement magnets is easy. Simply remove the old magnets from your calendar and replace them with the new ones. Make sure that the magnets are aligned properly and that the days, months, and holidays are in the correct order.
