What are the Top Events to Attend in 2024?

The Nantucket Wine & Food Festival

The Nantucket Wine & Food Festival is one of the top events to attend on the island. It takes place in May and attracts wine and food enthusiasts from all over the world. The festival features a variety of events, including wine tastings, cooking demonstrations, and seminars. If you are a foodie, this is an event that you will not want to miss.

The Nantucket Book Festival

The Nantucket Book Festival is another popular event that takes place in June. It features a variety of authors who come to the island to discuss their latest works. The festival includes book readings, panel discussions, and book signings. If you are a book lover, this is an event that you will want to attend.

The Nantucket Film Festival is an event that takes place in June. It features a variety of independent films and attracts filmmakers from all over the world. The festival includes film screenings, panel discussions, and Q&A sessions with the filmmakers. If you are a movie buff, this is an event that you will want to check out.

The Nantucket Comedy Festival

The Nantucket Comedy Festival is an event that takes place in July. It features a variety of comedians who come to the island to perform. The festival includes stand-up comedy shows, improv comedy shows, and workshops. If you are looking for a good laugh, this is an event that you will want to attend.

If you are planning a trip to Nantucket in 2024, you will want to plan your trip around these events. You can check the Nantucket Chamber of Commerce website for a complete calendar of events.
