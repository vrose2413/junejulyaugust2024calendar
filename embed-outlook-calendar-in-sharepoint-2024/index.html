How To Embed Outlook Calendar In Sharepoint 2024

Written by Juan Stafford Jul 31, 2022 · 3 min read

Table of Contents

Introduction

SharePoint is a great platform that enables teams to collaborate and share information. One of the most useful features of SharePoint is the ability to embed an Outlook calendar into a SharePoint site. This feature is particularly useful for teams that need to coordinate schedules and events. In this article, we will discuss how to embed an Outlook calendar in SharePoint 2024. We will also answer some commonly asked questions about this feature.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Create a New Calendar in Outlook

The first step in embedding an Outlook calendar in SharePoint is to create a new calendar in Outlook. To do this, open Outlook and click on the "Calendar" tab. Then, click on the "New Calendar" button and give your calendar a name.

Step 2: Share the Calendar

Once you have created your new calendar, you need to share it with your team members. To do this, right-click on the calendar and select "Sharing Permissions". Then, add the email addresses of the people you want to share the calendar with.

Step 3: Get the Calendar URL

To embed the calendar in SharePoint, you need to get the calendar URL. To do this, right-click on the calendar and select "Properties". Then, copy the URL from the "Webcal" field.

Step 4: Embed the Calendar in SharePoint

Now that you have the calendar URL, it's time to embed the calendar in SharePoint. To do this, open your SharePoint site and navigate to the page where you want to embed the calendar. Then, click on the "Edit" button and select "Insert". From there, select "Web Part" and then "Media and Content". Finally, select "Content Editor" and click "Add".

Step 5: Customize the Calendar

Once you have embedded the calendar in SharePoint, you can customize it to meet your needs. For example, you can change the size of the calendar or add color-coding to differentiate between different types of events.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I embed multiple calendars in SharePoint?

A: Yes, you can embed multiple calendars in SharePoint. Simply repeat the steps above for each calendar you want to embed.

Q: Can I embed a calendar from a different Outlook account?

A: Yes, you can embed a calendar from a different Outlook account as long as you have the necessary permissions to view the calendar.

Q: What happens if I delete an embedded calendar in SharePoint?

A: If you delete an embedded calendar in SharePoint, it will no longer appear on the page where it was embedded. However, the calendar itself will not be deleted from Outlook.
