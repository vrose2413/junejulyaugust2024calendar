Introduction

West Point Military Academy is one of the most prestigious military academies in the world. Founded in 1802, it is located in New York and has a rich history of producing some of the best military officers in the world. The academy has a rigorous academic curriculum, and it is important for students and their families to be aware of the academic calendar.

What is the West Point Academic Calendar?

The West Point Academic Calendar is a schedule of all academic activities that take place at the academy. This includes the start and end dates of each semester, as well as holidays and other important events.

When is the Academic Year?

The academic year at West Point begins in late August and ends in early May. There are two semesters in the academic year, with a winter break in between.

What are the Important Dates?

The important dates for the academic year 2024-25 are as follows: - August 26, 2024: Cadet basic training begins - August 29, 2024: Classes begin - November 27-December 1, 2024: Thanksgiving break - December 19, 2024-January 12, 2025: Winter break - January 13, 2025: Classes resume - March 17-24, 2025: Spring break - May 6, 2025: Graduation

What are the Holidays?

The academy observes several holidays throughout the academic year. These include Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veterans Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, President's Day, Memorial Day, and Independence Day.

FAQs

What is the Grading System at West Point?

The grading system at West Point is based on a 4.0 scale. Grades are assigned based on a student's performance on exams, quizzes, papers, and other assignments.

What Happens if a Cadet Fails a Class?

If a cadet fails a class, they may be required to repeat the course or take a remedial class. If a cadet fails multiple classes, they may be subject to academic probation or dismissal.

How Many Credit Hours are Required for Graduation?

Cadets at West Point are required to complete 141 credit hours in order to graduate. This includes courses in various academic disciplines, as well as military training and physical education.

What is the Honor Code?

The Honor Code at West Point is a set of ethical principles that all cadets are expected to follow. It includes values such as honesty, integrity, and respect for others.

Conclusion

The West Point Academic Calendar is an important resource for cadets and their families. By being aware of the important dates and events, cadets can plan ahead and ensure that they are successful in their academic and military careers.
