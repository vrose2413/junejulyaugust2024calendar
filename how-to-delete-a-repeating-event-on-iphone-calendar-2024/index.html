Are you having trouble deleting a repeating event on your iPhone calendar? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many iPhone users struggle with this issue, but luckily, there's an easy solution. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to delete a repeating event on your iPhone calendar for the year 2024.

Step 1: Open Your Calendar App

The first step to deleting a repeating event on your iPhone calendar is to open your calendar app. You can do this by tapping on the calendar icon on your home screen or by searching for it in your app drawer. Once you have your calendar open, you should see all of your upcoming events for the year 2024.

Step 2: Find the Repeating Event You Want to Delete

Next, you'll need to find the repeating event that you want to delete. This may take some scrolling depending on how many events you have on your calendar. Once you find the event, tap on it to open up the details for that event.

Step 3: Tap on "Edit"

Once you have the event details open, you should see an "Edit" button in the top right corner of the screen. Tap on this button to enter editing mode for the event.

Step 4: Scroll Down to "Repeat"

Now that you're in editing mode for the event, scroll down until you see the "Repeat" option. This is where you'll be able to turn off the repeating feature for the event.

Step 5: Select "Never"

Under the "Repeat" option, you should see a few different choices for how often you want the event to repeat. Select the option that says "Never" to turn off the repeating feature for the event.

Step 6: Tap on "Done"

Once you've selected "Never," tap on the "Done" button in the top right corner of the screen to save your changes. This will turn off the repeating feature for the event, so it will no longer appear on your calendar after the date of the event has passed.

Question and Answer

Q: What if I only want to delete one instance of a repeating event?

A: If you only want to delete one instance of a repeating event, you can do so by following the same steps as above, but instead of selecting "Never" under the "Repeat" option, select the option that says "This Event Only." This will delete the one instance of the event without affecting the rest of the repeating events.

Q: What if I accidentally delete the wrong event?

A: If you accidentally delete the wrong event, you can easily recover it by going into your "Recently Deleted" folder in the calendar app. From there, you can select the event and tap on "Restore" to bring it back to your calendar.

Conclusion

Deleting a repeating event on your iPhone calendar may seem tricky at first, but with these simple steps, you'll be able to do it in no time. Remember to always double-check your changes before saving them and to use the "Recently Deleted" folder if you accidentally delete the wrong event. Happy scheduling!
