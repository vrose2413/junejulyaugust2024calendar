How To Sync Google Calendar With Macbook Calendar 2024

Table of Contents

Introduction

Keeping track of your schedule is important, and with the increasing use of technology, it has become easier than ever to do so. Google Calendar is a popular application used by many to keep track of their schedules. However, if you are a MacBook user, you may find it difficult to sync your Google Calendar with your MacBook Calendar. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to sync Google Calendar with your MacBook Calendar in the year 2024.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Open Google Calendar

The first step is to open Google Calendar on your MacBook. If you do not have it installed, you can download it from the App Store.

Step 2: Click on Settings

Once you have opened Google Calendar, click on the gear icon located at the top right corner of the screen. This will open the settings menu.

Step 3: Click on "Calendars"

In the settings menu, click on the "Calendars" tab. This will show you a list of all the calendars that you have added to your Google Calendar.

Step 4: Copy the Calendar URL

Next, click on the three dots located next to the calendar that you want to sync with your MacBook Calendar. From the drop-down menu, select "Settings and Sharing". In the "Integrate calendar" section, copy the calendar URL.

Step 5: Open the Calendar App on your MacBook

Now, open the Calendar app on your MacBook.

Step 6: Click on "File" and select "New Calendar Subscription"

In the Calendar app, click on "File" located in the top left corner of the screen. From the drop-down menu, select "New Calendar Subscription".

Step 7: Paste the Calendar URL

A new window will open. Paste the calendar URL that you copied from Google Calendar in step 4. Click on "Subscribe".

Step 8: Name your Calendar

Give your calendar a name and select a color for it. This will help you differentiate it from other calendars.

Step 9: Select how often to refresh the calendar

You can choose how often you want your MacBook Calendar to refresh the synced calendar. You can choose from every 5 minutes to every week.

Step 10: Click on "OK"

Finally, click on "OK" to complete the syncing process.

FAQs

Q: Can I sync multiple Google Calendars with my MacBook Calendar?

A: Yes, you can sync multiple Google Calendars with your MacBook Calendar. Just repeat the steps above for each calendar that you want to sync.

Q: Do I need an internet connection to sync my Google Calendar with my MacBook Calendar?

A: Yes, you need an internet connection to sync your Google Calendar with your MacBook Calendar.

Q: How often does my MacBook Calendar refresh the synced calendar?

A: You can choose how often your MacBook Calendar refreshes the synced calendar. You can choose from every 5 minutes to every week.

Q: Can I edit events in the synced calendar?

A: Yes, you can edit events in the synced calendar. However, any changes you make will only be reflected in the synced calendar and not in your Google Calendar.

Conclusion

Syncing your Google Calendar with your MacBook Calendar is a simple process as long as you follow the steps above. With your calendars synced, you can easily keep track of your schedule on both your MacBook and your Google Calendar. This will help you stay organized and manage your time better.
