Welcome to Lenox, Massachusetts!

If you're planning to visit Lenox, Massachusetts in 2024, you're in for a treat. Lenox is a charming town located in the Berkshire Mountains, known for its beautiful scenery, rich history, and vibrant cultural scene. Whether you're a nature lover, history buff, or arts enthusiast, Lenox has something for everyone. In this article, we'll guide you through the Lenox MA calendar of events 2024, so you can make the most of your trip.

What are the top events in Lenox in 2024?

Lenox is known for its year-round events and festivals, but some of the top events in 2024 include:

  • The Berkshire International Film Festival
  • The Tanglewood Music Festival
  • The Lenox Apple Squeeze
  • The Lenox Craft Fair
  • The Shakespeare & Company Summer Festival

These events offer a variety of entertainment options, from film screenings and live music performances to craft fairs and food festivals. No matter what your interests are, you're sure to find something that catches your eye.

What is the Tanglewood Music Festival?

The Tanglewood Music Festival is one of the most popular events in Lenox, and for good reason. This world-renowned music festival takes place every summer at the Tanglewood Music Center, an outdoor venue located in the heart of the Berkshires. The festival features performances by some of the world's most talented musicians, including the Boston Symphony Orchestra and the Tanglewood Music Center Orchestra. In 2024, the festival will run from June 22nd to September 1st.

What is the Lenox Apple Squeeze?

The Lenox Apple Squeeze is a beloved annual event that celebrates the town's apple harvest. This two-day festival features live music, food vendors, and craft booths, as well as plenty of apple-themed activities. Visitors can sample fresh apple cider, try their hand at apple pressing, and even participate in an apple pie baking contest. The Lenox Apple Squeeze takes place on September 21st and 22nd in 2024.

What is the Shakespeare & Company Summer Festival?

The Shakespeare & Company Summer Festival is a must-see event for theater lovers. This festival features performances of classic Shakespeare plays, as well as contemporary works and special events. The festival takes place at the Tina Packer Playhouse, a beautiful outdoor theater located in the heart of Lenox. In 2024, the festival will run from June 28th to September 1st.

What is there to do in Lenox besides attending events?

While attending events is a great way to experience Lenox, there are plenty of other activities to enjoy as well. Some popular options include:

  • Hiking in the nearby mountains
  • Visiting historic sites like the Ventfort Hall Mansion and Gilded Age Museum
  • Browsing local art galleries and shops
  • Relaxing at one of the town's many spas or yoga studios

With so much to see and do, Lenox is the perfect destination for a relaxing and rejuvenating vacation.

Where can I find more information about events in Lenox?

If you're looking for more information about events in Lenox, be sure to check out the town's official website or the websites of local event organizers. You can also pick up a copy of the Lenox Visitor's Guide at the town's Visitor Center, located at 4 Housatonic Street.

Conclusion

From music festivals and food fairs to hiking and historic sites, Lenox, Massachusetts has something for everyone. Whether you're visiting for a weekend or a week, be sure to check out the town's calendar of events to make the most of your trip. We hope this guide has helped you plan your Lenox adventure and that you have a wonderful time exploring this beautiful town.
