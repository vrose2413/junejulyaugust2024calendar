What is a timeshare?

A timeshare is a property ownership model where multiple individuals own the right to use a property for a certain period of time each year. In the case of La Cabana, the timeshare owners have the right to use the resort's facilities and accommodations for a specific week or weeks each year.

How does the La Cabana timeshare calendar work?
