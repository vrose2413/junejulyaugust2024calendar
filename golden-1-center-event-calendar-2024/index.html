What is the Golden 1 Center?

The Golden 1 Center is a multi-purpose arena that opened in 2016. It is the home of the Sacramento Kings, and has a seating capacity of up to 17,608 for basketball games. The arena was designed with sustainability in mind, and features a number of environmentally-friendly features, such as solar panels, a rainwater capture system, and a green roof.
