What is a Lego Advent Calendar?

A Lego Advent Calendar is a special edition calendar that counts down the days until Christmas. Each day, a new door is opened to reveal a small Lego set or minifigure. The Star Wars Lego Advent Calendar is particularly popular among fans of the franchise, as it features mini-builds, vehicles, and characters from the Star Wars universe.
