Introduction

As we approach the end of 2023, it's time to start thinking about the upcoming academic year. For students at Springfield Technical Community College (STCC), this means looking at the academic calendar for Spring 2024. In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know about the STCC academic calendar for Spring 2024.

When Does the Spring 2024 Semester Start?

The Spring 2024 semester at STCC will start on Monday, January 22nd. This is the first day of classes for the semester, so make sure to mark it on your calendar.

When Do Classes End?

Classes for the Spring 2024 semester will end on Friday, May 10th. This means that students will have approximately 16 weeks of classes for the semester.

What Are the Holidays and Breaks?

There are several holidays and breaks throughout the Spring 2024 semester. The first holiday is Presidents' Day, which falls on Monday, February 19th. STCC will be closed on this day, and there will be no classes. The next break is Spring Break, which runs from Monday, March 18th to Friday, March 22nd. There will be no classes during this week, giving students a chance to recharge before the final stretch of the semester.

Are There Any Other Holidays?

Yes, there are two other holidays during the Spring 2024 semester. The first is Patriot's Day, which falls on Monday, April 15th. STCC will be closed on this day, and there will be no classes. The second holiday is Memorial Day, which falls on Monday, May 27th. STCC will be closed on this day, and there will be no classes.

What Are the Final Exam Dates?

Final exams for the Spring 2024 semester will take place from Monday, May 13th to Friday, May 17th. It's important to note that the final exam schedule may vary by class, so make sure to check with your professor to confirm the date and time of your final exam.

What Happens if You Miss a Final Exam?

If you miss a final exam, there are several things that may happen. Your professor may allow you to take a makeup exam, but this is not guaranteed. In some cases, your professor may assign a grade of zero for the missed exam, which can significantly impact your final grade for the class.

What Should You Do if You Need Help?

If you're struggling with your classes or need help with anything related to the Spring 2024 semester, there are several resources available to you. The STCC Academic Support Center offers tutoring and academic coaching to help you succeed in your classes. Additionally, the STCC Counseling Center provides free and confidential counseling services to students.

What Should You Do if You Need to Withdraw from a Class?

If you need to withdraw from a class during the Spring 2024 semester, you should speak with your professor and an academic advisor as soon as possible. Withdrawing from a class can have significant consequences, including impacting your financial aid and delaying your graduation, so it's important to carefully consider your options before making a decision.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the STCC academic calendar for Spring 2024 provides students with a clear roadmap for the upcoming semester. By understanding the start and end dates of the semester, as well as the holidays, breaks, and final exam schedule, students can better plan their schedules and ensure that they stay on track to graduate. If you need help or have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to the resources available to you at STCC.
